|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the fee for a permit authorizing the movement of certain  
| vehicles transporting an intermodal shipping container.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 623.404(a), Transportation Code, as  
| added by Chapter 108 (S.B. 1524), Acts of the 85th Legislature,  
| Regular Session, 2017, is amended to read as follows:  
|  (a) An application for a permit under Section 623.402(a) or  
| (b) must be accompanied by a permit fee of $6,000, of which:  
|  (1) _45_ [ ~~50~~ ] percent shall be deposited to the credit of  
| the state highway fund;  
|  (2) _33_ [ ~~30~~ ] percent shall be equally divided among and  
| distributed to each county designated in the permit application;  
|  (3) _20_ [ ~~16~~ ] percent shall be equally divided among and  
| distributed to each municipality designated in the permit  
| application; and  
|  (4) _2_ [ ~~4~~ ] percent shall be deposited to the credit of  
| the Texas Department of Motor Vehicles fund.  
|  SECTION 2. The change in law made by this Act applies only  
| to a permit issued on or after the effective date of this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
