|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the manufacture of wine by the holder of a winery  
| permit.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 16, Alcoholic Beverage Code, is amended  
| by adding Section 16.045 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___16.045._ _____MANUFACTURING REQUIRED. (a)_ ___The holder of a_  
| _winery permit must be a bona fide winery actively engaged in the_  
| _manufacture of wine._  
|  _(b)_ _____The commission shall adopt rules necessary to implement_  
| _this section. Rules adopted under this section must provide:_  
|  _(1)_ _____a reasonable amount of time after the issuance of a_  
| _winery permit for the permit holder to begin manufacturing wine;_  
| _and_  
|  _(2)_ _____reasonable variances for crop failure or other_  
| _impediments to manufacture that are beyond the permit holder's_  
| _control._  
|  _(c)_ _____The holder of a winery permit which was applied for or_  
| _issued on or before March 7, 2019 and who on that date was not_  
| _actively engaged in the manufacture of wine may be grandfathered_  
| _from this section by filing a sworn affidavit with the commission on_  
| _or before December 31, 2019 stating those facts and stating that it_  
| _does not intend to actively engage in the manufacture of wine in the_  
| _future. This section shall not apply to a winery permit which timely_  
| _files such an affidavit with the commission._  
|  SECTION 2. (a)  Section 16.045(a), Alcoholic Beverage Code,  
| as added by this Act, takes effect on the effective date of the  
| rules adopted by the Texas Alcoholic Beverage Commission under  
| Section 16.045(b), Alcoholic Beverage Code, as added by this Act.  
|  (b) Rules adopted under Section 16.045(b), Alcoholic  
| Beverage Code, as added by this Act, must provide a reasonable  
| amount of time after the effective date of the rules for a holder of  
| a winery permit issued before the effective date of the rules to  
| begin manufacturing wine and otherwise come into compliance with  
| the rules.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
