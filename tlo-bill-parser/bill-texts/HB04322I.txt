|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to prohibited employment of certain former commissioners  
| of insurance and employees of the Texas Department of Insurance;  
| creating an offense.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 33, Insurance Code, is amended by adding  
| Section 33.008 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___33.008._ _____PROHIBITED EMPLOYMENT. (a) An individual who_  
| _served as commissioner or an employee of the department commits an_  
| _offense if, before the second anniversary of the date the_  
| _individual ceases to serve, the individual is employed by or under_  
| _contract with:_  
|  _(1) a company regulated by the department;_  
|  _(2)_ _____an affiliate of a company regulated by the_  
| _department;_  
|  _(3)_ _____an individual or entity representing a company_  
| _regulated by the department; or_  
|  _(4)_ _____an association of which a company regulated by the_  
| _department is a member._  
|  _(b) An offense under this section is a Class A misdemeanor._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 33.008, Insurance Code, as added by this  
| Act, applies only to an individual who ceases to serve the Texas  
| Department of Insurance on or after the effective date of this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
