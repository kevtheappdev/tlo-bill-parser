|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the location at which an application for a disabled  
| parking placard may be made.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 681.003(b), Transportation Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (b) An application for a disabled parking placard must be:  
|  (1) on a form furnished by the department;  
|  (2) submitted to the county assessor-collector of the  
| county in which the person with the disability _:_  
|  _(A)_ resides _;_ or  
|  _(B)_ [ ~~in which the applicant~~ ] is seeking medical  
| treatment [ ~~if the applicant is not a resident of this state~~ ]; and  
|  (3) accompanied by a fee of $5 if the application is  
| for a temporary placard.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
