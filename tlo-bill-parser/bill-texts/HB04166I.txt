|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a study of the feasibility of the expansion of  
| navigation on the Red River by the Red River Authority of Texas.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. (a)  The Red River Authority of Texas shall study  
| the feasibility of increasing navigation on the Red River between  
| Texarkana and Denison by completing the navigation system of locks  
| and dams or other means.  
|  (b) Not later than January 1, 2021, the Red River Authority  
| of Texas shall submit to the standing committees of the legislature  
| having jurisdiction over navigation a report on the findings of the  
| study.  
|  (c) This section expires January 2, 2021.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
