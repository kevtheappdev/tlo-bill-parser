|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to prohibiting a public school counselor from being  
| required to act in a disciplinary capacity in certain  
| circumstances.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 33.006, Education Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (d) to read as follows:  
|  _(d)_ _____A school counselor may not be required to assume a_  
| _disciplinary role at a school, or to otherwise be assigned a duty_  
| _involving student discipline that is inconsistent with the_  
| _counselor's primary responsibility under Subsection (a) and_  
| _additional responsibilities under this section._ ___This subsection_  
| _does not prohibit a school counselor from serving on a committee to_  
| _review a student's placement in an alternative education program_  
| _under Section 37.306._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
