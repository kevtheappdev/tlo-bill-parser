|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the posting of notice of intent to obtain a permit from  
| the Texas Commission on Environmental Quality at the site of the  
| facility or proposed facility for which the permit is sought.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 5.552, Water Code, is amended by adding  
| Subsection (c-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(c-1)_ _____At the site of the facility or proposed facility for_  
| _which an applicant is required to publish notice under this_  
| _section, the applicant shall place a sign that complies with rules_  
| _adopted by the commission stating:_  
|  _(1)_ _____that an application for a permit for a facility at_  
| _the site has been filed; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____the manner in which the commission may be_  
| _contacted for further information._  
|  SECTION 2. The change in law made by this Act applies only  
| to a permit application that is filed with the Texas Commission on  
| Environmental Quality on or after the effective date of this Act. A  
| permit application filed before the effective date of this Act is  
| governed by the law in effect on the date of filing, and that law is  
| continued in effect for that purpose.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
