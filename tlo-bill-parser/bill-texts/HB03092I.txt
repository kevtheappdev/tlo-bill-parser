|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to notice of an epizootic infectious disease occurring in  
| an animal shelter.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 823, Health and Safety Code, is amended  
| by adding Section 823.004 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___823.004._ _____REQUIRED NOTICE OF EPIZOOTIC INFECTIOUS_  
| _DISEASES. (a)_ ___In this section, "epizootic" has the meaning_  
| _assigned by Section 826.002._  
|  _(b)_ _____An animal shelter shall provide written notice to each_  
| _person who adopts an animal from the shelter of any epizootic_  
| _infectious disease that occurs among the animals in the shelter_  
| _within the 30-day period:_  
|  _(1) before the date the person adopts the animal; or_  
|  _(2) after the date the person adopts the animal._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
