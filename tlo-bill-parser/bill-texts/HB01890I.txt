|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to emergency contact and medical information maintained by  
| the Department of Public Safety.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 521.060(e), Transportation Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (e) The forms and procedures _established and_ maintained  
| under Subsection (d) must _:_  
|  _(1)_ comply with Subsection (c) _; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____allow the holder of a driver's license or personal_  
| _identification certificate or an authorized agent of the holder to_  
| _add, amend, or delete information described by Subsection (d) by_  
| _submitting an electronic form on the department's Internet website_.  
|  SECTION 2. Not later than January 1, 2020, the Department of  
| Public Safety of the State of Texas shall produce and make available  
| on the department's Internet website an electronic form in  
| accordance with Section 521.060(e), Transportation Code, as  
| amended by this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
