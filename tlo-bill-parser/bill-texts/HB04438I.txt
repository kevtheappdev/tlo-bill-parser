|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the powers and duties of the Port of Houston Authority  
| of Harris County, Texas.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 5007, Special District  
| Local Laws Code, is amended by adding Section 5007.011 to read as  
| follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___5007.011._ _____SHIP CHANNEL TRAFFIC. The authority shall_  
| _control traffic in the ship channel in a manner consistent with:_  
|  _(1)_ _____best practices for the navigation of large ships;_  
| _and_  
|  _(2)_ _____fair and equitable access to the ship channel for_  
| _all vessels._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
