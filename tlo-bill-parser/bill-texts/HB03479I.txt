|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to cleanliness standards for pipelines crossing karst  
| topographic areas.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 117.012, Natural Resources Code, is  
| amended by adding Subsection (p) to read as follows:  
|  _(p)_ _____The commission by rule shall establish cleanliness_  
| _standards for intrastate pipelines transporting hazardous liquids_  
| _through a karst topographic area._  
|  SECTION 2. Subchapter E, Chapter 121, Utilities Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 121.2017 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___121.2017._ _____DISTRIBUTION THROUGH KARST AREA. The_  
| _railroad commission by rule shall establish cleanliness standards_  
| _for intrastate distribution gas pipelines that pass through a karst_  
| _topographic area._  
|  SECTION 3. The Railroad Commission of Texas shall adopt the  
| rules required by Section 117.012(p), Natural Resources Code, as  
| added by this Act, and Section 121.2017, Utilities Code, as added by  
| this Act, not later than December 1, 2019.  
|  SECTION 4. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
