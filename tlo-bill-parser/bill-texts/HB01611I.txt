|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to regulation in the unincorporated area of a county of  
| high-speed rail activity near schools.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 112, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 112.104 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___112.104._ _____COUNTY REGULATION OF HIGH-SPEED RAIL ACTIVITY_  
| _NEAR SCHOOLS. In the interest of safety, a county by order may_  
| _regulate operations and activities in the unincorporated area of_  
| _the county related to high-speed rail, as defined by Section_  
| _112.201, that occur within five miles of the campus of an elementary_  
| _or secondary school, day-care center, preschool, or institution of_  
| _higher education._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
