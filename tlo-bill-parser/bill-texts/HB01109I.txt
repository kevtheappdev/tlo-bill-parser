|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to waiving certain annual training requirements for foster  
| parents.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 42.042, Human Resources Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (p-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(p-1)_ _____The executive commissioner by rule shall create a_  
| _procedure to allow a foster child's caseworker to waive any annual_  
| _training requirement for the child's foster parent that is not_  
| _directly related to caring for the foster child._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
