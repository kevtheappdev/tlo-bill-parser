|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to public inspection of an early voting roster; creating a  
| criminal offense.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 87.121, Election Code, is amended by  
| amending Subsection (h) and adding Subsections (i) and (j) to read  
| as follows:  
|  (h) Information on the roster for a person who votes an  
| early voting ballot by mail shall be made available for public  
| inspection not later than the day following the day the early voting  
| clerk receives a ballot voted by mail. _A person may request a copy_  
| _of the roster._  
|  _(i) A person commits an offense if the person:_  
|  _(1)_ _____is responsible for maintaining the roster under_  
| _this section; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____fails to provide information on the roster under_  
| _Subsection (h)._  
|  _(j)_ _____An offense under Subsection (i) is a Class C_  
| _misdemeanor._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
