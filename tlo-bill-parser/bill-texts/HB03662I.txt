|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the composition of the board of trustees of the Teacher  
| Retirement System of Texas.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 825.001, Government Code, is amended to  
| read as follows:  
|  Sec. 825.001. COMPOSITION OF BOARD OF TRUSTEES. The board  
| of trustees is composed of _12_ [ ~~nine~~ ] members.  
|  SECTION 2. Section 825.002, Government Code, is amended by  
| amending Subsections (a), (b), (d), and (e) and adding Subsection  
| (e-2) to read as follows:  
|  (a) The governor shall appoint, with the advice and consent  
| of the senate and as provided by this section, _10_ [ ~~seven~~ ] members of  
| the board of trustees.  
|  (b) The governor shall appoint _four_ [ ~~three~~ ] members of the  
| board to hold office for staggered terms, with the term of one  
| trustee expiring on August 31 of each odd-numbered year. These  
| members must be persons who have demonstrated financial expertise,  
| who have worked in private business or industry, and who have broad  
| investment experience, preferably in investment of pension funds.  
| None of the members appointed under this subsection may be a member  
| or annuitant of the retirement system.  
|  (d) _Subject to Subsection (e-2), the_ [ ~~The~~ ] governor shall  
| appoint _two members_ [ ~~one member~~ ] of the board from a slate of _four_  
| [ ~~three~~ ] former members of the retirement system who have retired  
| and are receiving benefits from the retirement system and who have  
| been nominated in accordance with Subsections (f) and (g) by the  
| persons who have retired and are receiving benefits from the  
| retirement system.  
|  (e) _Subject to Subsection (e-2), the_ [ ~~The~~ ] governor shall  
| appoint _two members_ [ ~~one member~~ ] of the board from a slate of _four_  
| [ ~~three~~ ] persons who have been nominated in accordance with  
| Subsection (f) by the following groups collectively:  
|  (1) members of the retirement system whose most recent  
| credited service was performed for an institution of higher  
| education;  
|  (2) members of the retirement system whose most recent  
| credited service was performed for a public school district,  
| charter school, or regional education service center; and  
|  (3) persons who have retired and are receiving  
| benefits from the retirement system.  
|  _(e-2)_ _____The governor shall ensure that at least one of the_  
| _members appointed under Subsection (d) or (e), as appropriate, is:_  
|  _(1)_ _____a former member who retired from an institution of_  
| _higher education; or_  
|  _(2)_ _____currently employed by an institution of higher_  
| _education._  
|  SECTION 3. (a)  As soon as practicable after the effective  
| date of this Act, the governor shall appoint an additional member to  
| the board of trustees of the Teacher Retirement System of Texas  
| under Section 825.002(b), Government Code, as amended by this Act.  
|  (b) Notwithstanding the term of six years stipulated by  
| Section 825.004(a), Government Code, as soon as practicable after  
| the effective date of this Act, the Teacher Retirement System of  
| Texas shall prepare a slate of three nominees and the governor shall  
| appoint from that slate an additional member to the board of  
| trustees of the Teacher Retirement System of Texas under Section  
| 825.002(d), Government Code, as amended by this Act, to serve a term  
| that expires on a date that preserves the required staggering of  
| terms. In making the appointment under this subsection, the  
| governor shall ensure that the composition of the board of trustees  
| complies with Section 825.002(e-2)(1), Government Code, as added by  
| this Act.  Subsequent board of trustee members appointed to that  
| position shall serve the six-year term.  
|  (c) Notwithstanding the term of six years stipulated by  
| Section 825.004(a), Government Code, as soon as practicable after  
| the effective date of this Act, the members of the Teacher  
| Retirement System of Texas who are described by Sections  
| 825.002(e)(1)-(3), Government Code, as amended by this Act, shall  
| prepare a slate of three nominees and the governor shall appoint  
| from that slate an additional member to the board of trustees of the  
| Teacher Retirement System of Texas under Section 825.002(e),  
| Government Code, as amended by this Act, to serve a term that  
| expires on a date that preserves the required staggering of terms.  
| In making the appointment under this subsection, the governor shall  
| ensure that the composition of the board of trustees complies with  
| Section 825.002(e-2)(2), Government Code, as added by this Act.  
| Subsequent board of trustee members appointed to that position  
| shall serve the six-year term.  
|  SECTION 4. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
