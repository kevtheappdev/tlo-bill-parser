|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to an expedited licensing process for certain advanced  
| practice registered nurses; authorizing a fee.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter F, Chapter 301, Occupations Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 301.262 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___301.262._ _____EXPEDITED LICENSING PROCESS FOR OUT-OF-STATE_  
| _ADVANCED PRACTICE REGISTERED NURSES._ ___(a) The board by rule shall_  
| _create an expedited licensing process for an applicant who holds a_  
| _license or other authorization to practice nursing as an advanced_  
| _practice registered nurse issued by another state._  
|  _(b) The board may establish a fee for the expedited process._  
|  _(c)_ _____The 180-day deadline and extension provisions under_  
| _Section 301.260(c) do not apply to the expedited licensing process_  
| _required under this section._  
|  SECTION 2. The Texas Board of Nursing shall create and  
| implement the expedited licensing process under Section 301.262,  
| Occupations Code, as added by this Act, not later than January 1,  
| 2020.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
