|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the construction of small occupancy facilities by the  
| Texas Juvenile Justice Department.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 242, Human Resources Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 242.0525 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___242.0525._ _____SMALL OCCUPANCY FACILITIES. (a)_ ___The_  
| _department shall construct small occupancy facilities with the_  
| _purpose of gradually closing and replacing the large, remote secure_  
| _facilities operated by the department. In constructing the small_  
| _occupancy facilities, the department shall ensure that each small_  
| _occupancy facility:_  
|  _(1)_ _____provides a therapeutic and rehabilitative_  
| _setting;_  
|  _(2)_ _____provides housing for not more than 40 children;_  
| _and_  
|  _(3)_ _____is constructed in conjunction with the_  
| _regionalization plan described by Section 203.017._  
|  _(b)_ _____Not later than July 1, 2020, the department shall_  
| _complete the construction of five small occupancy facilities._ ___Not_  
| _later than July 1, 2021, the department shall complete the_  
| _construction of five additional small occupancy facilities._  
|  _(c)_ _____After the completion of construction of each small_  
| _occupancy facility, the department, in coordination with the office_  
| _of inspector general, shall move children who are committed to the_  
| _large, remote secure facilities operated by the department into the_  
| _small occupancy facility._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
