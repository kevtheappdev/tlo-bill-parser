|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to 72-hour and 144-hour registration permits for  
| commercial motor vehicles, trailers, semitrailers, and motor  
| buses.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 502.094, Transportation Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (i) to read as follows:  
|  _(i)_ _____A receipt for a temporary registration permit issued_  
| _under this section shall be carried in the vehicle at all times_  
| _during the period for which the permit is valid. The temporary tag_  
| _must contain all pertinent information required by this section and_  
| _must be displayed in the rear window of the vehicle so that the tag_  
| _is clearly visible and legible when viewed from the rear of the_  
| _vehicle._ ___If the vehicle does not have a rear window, the temporary_  
| _tag must be attached to or carried in the vehicle to allow ready_  
| _inspection._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
