|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to requiring commissioners court approval of a proposed  
| county road alteration for high-speed rail construction.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 112.057, Transportation Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (c) to read as follows:  
|  _(c)_ _____A private entity may not begin construction of a_  
| _high-speed rail project in a county in which the construction would_  
| _alter a county road unless the alteration is approved by the_  
| _commissioners court of that county._ ___In this subsection,_  
| _"high-speed rail"_ ___has the meaning assigned by Section 112.201._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
