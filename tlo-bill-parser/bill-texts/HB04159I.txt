|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to state programs not funded by appropriations.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. The legislature finds that any mandatory  
| governmental programs for which no appropriation is made should be  
| eliminated as unnecessary.  
|  SECTION 2. Section 403.0147, Government Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (d) to read as follows:  
|  _(d)_ _____Notwithstanding any other law, a state agency is not_  
| _required to implement a program identified in the two most recent_  
| _reports made under this section and for which no appropriation is_  
| _made for the current state fiscal year._  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
