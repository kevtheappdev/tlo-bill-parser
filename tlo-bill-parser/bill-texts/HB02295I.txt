|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the state contribution to the Teacher Retirement System  
| of Texas.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 825.404(a), Government Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  (a) Except as provided by Subsection (a-1), during each  
| fiscal year, the state shall contribute to the retirement system an  
| amount equal to at least _seven_ [ ~~six~~ ] and not more than 10 percent of  
| the aggregate annual compensation of all members of the retirement  
| system during that fiscal year.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
