|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the filling of a vacancy in the office of county  
| commissioner.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 87.042, Local Government Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  Sec. 87.042. COUNTY COMMISSIONER VACANCY. _Not later than_  
| _the 30th day after the date_ [ ~~If~~ ] a vacancy occurs in the office of  
| county commissioner, the county judge shall appoint a suitable  
| resident of the precinct in which the vacancy exists to fill the  
| vacancy until the next general election.  
|  SECTION 2. Section 87.042, Local Government Code, as  
| amended by this Act, applies only to a vacancy in the office of  
| county commissioner that occurs on or after the effective date of  
| this Act. If a vacancy in the office of county commissioner  
| occurred and has not been filled before the effective date of this  
| Act, the county judge shall appoint, not later than the 30th day  
| after the effective date of this Act, a suitable resident of the  
| precinct in which the vacancy exists to fill the vacancy until the  
| next general election.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
