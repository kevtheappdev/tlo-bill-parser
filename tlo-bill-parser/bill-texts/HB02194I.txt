|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the translation of certain informational materials  
| regarding the essential knowledge and skills of the required public  
| school curriculum.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 28, Education Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 28.0151 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___28.0151._ _____TRANSLATION OF INFORMATIONAL MATERIALS ON_  
| _CURRICULUM REQUIREMENTS. The agency must make available in_  
| _English, Spanish, and Vietnamese any materials the agency develops_  
| _to provide the public with information regarding the essential_  
| _knowledge and skills of the required curriculum under Section_  
| _28.002, regardless of the format in which the materials are_  
| _provided._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
