|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to payments to school districts for certain special  
| education evaluations.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 29, Education Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 29.0042 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___29.0042._ _____PAYMENTS FOR FULL INDIVIDUAL AND INITIAL_  
| _EVALUATIONS. (a)_ ___Each school year from funds available to the_  
| _agency for the purpose, a school district is entitled to receive a_  
| _$1,000 payment for each full individual and initial evaluation the_  
| _district conducts under Section 29.004 during that school year._  
| _Payments authorized under this section are in addition to other_  
| _funding the district receives through other provisions of this_  
| _code, including the allotment provided under Section 42.152._  
|  _(b)_ _____To receive payment under this section for a school year,_  
| _a school district must submit to the commissioner on or before a_  
| _date established by commissioner rule:_  
|  _(1)_ _____a list that identifies each evaluation conducted_  
| _by the district under Section 29.004 for the year; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____documentation sufficient to verify each_  
| _evaluation for which the district requests a payment._  
|  _(c)_ _____The commissioner shall approve an evaluation for_  
| _payment under this section if the commissioner determines a school_  
| _district's documentation submitted under Subsection (b) for the_  
| _evaluation is sufficient._  
|  _(d)_ _____If the funds available for a school year are not_  
| _sufficient to make a full $1,000 payment for each evaluation_  
| _approved for payment for that year, the commissioner shall reduce_  
| _the payment amount provided for each evaluation approved_  
| _proportionately to an amount equal to the total amount of funds_  
| _available for payments under this section divided by the total_  
| _number of evaluations approved._  
|  _(e)_ _____The commissioner shall adopt rules as necessary to_  
| _implement this section, including rules:_  
|  _(1)_ _____to provide guidance for submitting a request for_  
| _payment, including submitting documentation for verification of an_  
| _evaluation under Subsection (b); and_  
|  _(2)_ _____to ensure this section complies with federal law_  
| _regarding confidentiality of student medical or educational_  
| _information, including the Health Insurance Portability and_  
| _Accountability Act of 1996 (42 U.S.C. Section 1320d et seq.) and the_  
| _Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act of 1974 (20 U.S.C._  
| _Section 1232g), and any state law relating to the privacy of student_  
| _information._  
|  SECTION 2. (a) This Act applies beginning with the  
| 2019-2020 school year.  
|  (b) As soon as practicable after the effective date of this  
| Act, the commissioner of education shall adopt rules necessary to  
| implement Section 29.0042, Education Code, as added by this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
