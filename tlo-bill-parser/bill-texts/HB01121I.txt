|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the recovery of property from certain vehicles stored  
| at a vehicle storage facility or governmental vehicle storage  
| facility.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter D, Chapter 2303, Occupations Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 2303.162 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___2303.162._ _____RECOVERY OF PROPERTY FROM CERTAIN STORED_  
| _VEHICLES. (a) In this section:_  
|  _(1)_ _____"Governmental vehicle storage facility" means a_  
| _garage, parking lot, or other facility that is:_  
|  _(A) owned by a governmental entity; and_  
|  _(B)_ _____used to store or park at least 10 vehicles_  
| _each year._  
|  _(2)_ _____"Motor carrier"_ ___has the meaning assigned by_  
| _Section 643.001, Transportation Code._  
|  _(b)_ _____The operator of a vehicle storage facility or_  
| _governmental vehicle storage facility shall allow the owner of_  
| _property that is left in a vehicle stored at the facility to recover_  
| _the property if:_  
|  _(1)_ _____at the time the vehicle was towed to the facility_  
| _for storage:_  
|  _(A)_ _____the vehicle was operated by a motor carrier;_  
| _and_  
|  _(B)_ _____the property was transported under an_  
| _agreement between the property owner and the motor carrier; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____the property owner demonstrates ownership of the_  
| _property._  
|  _(c)_ _____The commission by rule shall determine the documents_  
| _necessary to establish a person's ownership of property under this_  
| _section._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
