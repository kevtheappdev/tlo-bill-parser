|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to authorizing parents of students enrolled at a  
| low-performing public school campus to petition the commissioner of  
| education for the appointment of a board of managers, alternative  
| management, or closure of the campus.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 39A.112, Education Code, is amended by  
| amending Subsection (b) and adding Subsections (b-1) and (b-2) to  
| read as follows:  
|  (b) _Notwithstanding Section 39A.101, if a campus has been_  
| _assigned an unacceptable performance rating under Section 39.054_  
| _for two consecutive school years, a majority of the parents of the_  
| _students enrolled at the campus may, at any time, submit a petition_  
| _to the commissioner requesting that the commissioner order one of_  
| _the following actions:_  
|  _(1)_ _____appointment of a board of managers to govern the_  
| _school district as provided by Section 39A.202;_  
|  _(2) alternative management of the campus; or_  
|  _(3) closure of the campus_ [ ~~If the commissioner is~~  
|  ~~presented, in the time and manner specified by commissioner rule,~~  
|  ~~with a written petition signed by the parents of a majority of the~~  
|  ~~students enrolled at a campus to which Section 39A.111 applies,~~  
|  ~~specifying an action authorized under that section that the parents~~  
|  ~~request the commissioner to order, the commissioner shall, except~~  
|  ~~as otherwise authorized by this section, order the specific action~~  
|  ~~requested~~ ].  
|  _(b-1)_ _____The commissioner shall consider the action requested_  
| _in a parents'_ ___petition under Subsection (b) in determining whether_  
| _to order the requested action or another permissible action under_  
| _this subchapter, including ordering the campus to prepare or_  
| _implement a campus turnaround plan._  
|  _(b-2)_ _____A petition submitted under Subsection (b) must be in a_  
| _form prescribed by the commissioner._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act applies beginning with the 2019-2020  
| school year.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
