|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the metering of certain wells by the Panola County  
| Groundwater Conservation District.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 8819, Special District  
| Local Laws Code, is amended by adding Section 8819.106 to read as  
| follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___8819.106._ _____METERING OF CERTAIN WELLS PROHIBITED._ ___The_  
| _district may not require a meter for a well that is not capable of_  
| _producing more than 25,000 gallons of groundwater per day._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
