|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a landlord's obligation to rekey a security device for a  
| dwelling.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 92.156(a), Property Code, is amended to  
| read as follows:  
|  (a) Except as otherwise provided by Subsection (e), a  
| security device operated by a key, card, or combination shall be  
| rekeyed by the landlord at the landlord's expense _before_ [ ~~not later~~  
|  ~~than the seventh day after~~ ] each tenant turnover date.  
|  SECTION 2. The changes in law made by this Act apply only to  
| a lease entered into or renewed on or after the effective date of  
| this Act. A lease entered into or renewed before the effective date  
| of this Act is governed by the law as it existed immediately before  
| the effective date of this Act, and that law is continued in effect  
| for that purpose.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
