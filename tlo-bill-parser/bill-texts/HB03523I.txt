|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to educational requirements for hospital case managers.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 241, Health and Safety  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 241.013 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___241.013._ _____HOSPITAL CASE MANAGERS. (a)_ ___In this section,_  
| _"hospital case manager" means a person who provides health care_  
| _coordination, utilization review, discharge planning, and other_  
| _services to meet the health care needs of a patient in a hospital._  
|  _(b)_ _____The executive commissioner by rule shall establish_  
| _educational requirements for hospital case managers who are_  
| _employed by or are providing services to a hospital._ ___The rules at a_  
| _minimum must require a hospital case manager to:_  
|  _(1)_ _____hold a license issued under Chapter 301,_  
| _Occupations Code; or_  
|  _(2)_ _____have a master's degree from an accredited_  
| _institution of higher education._  
|  _(c)_ _____The commission shall ensure compliance with the_  
| _requirements established under Subsection (b)._  
|  SECTION 2. As soon as practicable after the effective date  
| of this Act, the Health and Human Services Commission shall adopt  
| the rules necessary to implement Section 241.013, Health and Safety  
| Code, as added by this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
