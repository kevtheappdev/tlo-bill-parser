|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to training regarding fire safety inspections required by  
| law.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 419.909(a), Government Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  (a) Only an individual certified by the commission as a fire  
| inspector may conduct a fire safety inspection required by a state  
| or local law, rule, regulation, or ordinance. _The following_  
| _entities may provide training related to fire safety inspections:_  
|  _(1) the commission;_  
|  _(2)_ _____the State Firemen's and Fire Marshals' Association_  
| _of Texas;_  
|  _(3) the National Fire Protection Association;_  
|  _(4) a public junior college;_  
|  _(5) a regional fire academy; or_  
|  _(6)_ _____any state agency with authority over fire safety_  
| _inspections._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution.  
| If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
