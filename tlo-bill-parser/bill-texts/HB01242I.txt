|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to authorization to enter onto property to survey for a  
| proposed high-speed rail facility.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 112, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 112.0515 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___112.0515._ _____ENTRY ONTO PROPERTY:_ ___EXAMINATION OR SURVEY_  
| _FOR HIGH-SPEED RAIL. (a) In this section, "high-speed rail" means_  
| _intercity passenger service that is reasonably expected to reach_  
| _speeds of at least 110 miles per hour._  
|  _(b)_ _____A private entity may not enter on the lands or waters of_  
| _any person or corporation under Section 112.051 to make an_  
| _examination or survey, including a lineal survey, for a proposed_  
| _high-speed rail facility unless the department has determined that_  
| _the entity is a railroad company for the purposes of Section_  
| _112.002._  
|  _(c)_ _____The department by rule shall adopt procedures to_  
| _implement this section._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
