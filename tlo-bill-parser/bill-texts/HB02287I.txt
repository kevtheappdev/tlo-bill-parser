|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the operations of certain housing authorities.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 392, Local Government  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 392.020 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___392.020._ _____Operation of Certain Municipal Housing_  
| _Authorities in a County Area. (a) This section applies only to the_  
| _operation of a housing authority operating in:_  
|  _(1)_ _____a county that has a population of 800,000 or more_  
| _and is located on the international border; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____a municipality that has a population of more than_  
| _600,000 and is located in a county described by Subsection (a)(1)._  
|  _(b)_ _____A municipal housing authority under this chapter may_  
| _conduct its operation in the municipality described in Subsection_  
| _(a)(2) as well as the county described in Subsection (a)(1),_  
| _excluding any parts of the county that are:_  
|  _(1)_ _____within the territorial boundaries of another_  
| _municipality not described in Subsection (a)(2); and_  
|  _(2)_ _____in which a public housing authority under this_  
| _chapter operates._  
|  _(c)_ _____Section 392.014 of this Chapter shall not apply to a_  
| _municipal housing authority operating pursuant to this Section._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
