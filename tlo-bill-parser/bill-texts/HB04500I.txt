|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to cancellation of all voter registrations following  
| decennial redistricting.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 16, Election Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 16.040 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___16.040._ _____CANCELLATION FOLLOWING DECENNIAL_  
| _REDISTRICTING. (a)_ ___Notwithstanding any other provision of this_  
| _code, on November 30 of each year that ends in one, the registrar_  
| _shall cancel all voter registrations._  
|  _(b)_ _____A cancellation under this section may not be challenged_  
| _or reinstated under this chapter._  
|  _(c)_ _____Following cancellation under this section, a person_  
| _desiring to register to vote must submit an application for_  
| _registration under Chapter 13._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
