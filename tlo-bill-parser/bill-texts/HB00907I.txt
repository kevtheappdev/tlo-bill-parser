|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the penalty for failure to register certain aggregate  
| production operations with the Texas Commission on Environmental  
| Quality.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 28A.102, Water Code, is amended to read  
| as follows:  
|  Sec. 28A.102. PENALTY.  The commission may assess a penalty  
| of not less than _$10,000_ [ ~~$5,000~~ ] and not more than _$20,000_  
| [ ~~$10,000~~ ] for each year in which an aggregate production operation  
| operates without being registered under this chapter. The total  
| penalty under this section may not exceed _$50,000_ [ ~~$25,000~~ ] for an  
| aggregate production operation that is operated in three or more  
| years without being registered.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
