|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to dispenser requirements for refilling prescriptions for  
| certain controlled substances.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 481.0765, Health and Safety Code, is  
| amended by adding Subsection (d) to read as follows:  
|  _(d)_ _____A dispenser is not subject to the requirements of_  
| _Section 481.0764(a) if the dispenser is refilling a prescription._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
