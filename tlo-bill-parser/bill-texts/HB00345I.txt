|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the automatic issuance of a personal identification  
| certificate to a person 60 years of age or older whose driver's  
| license has been surrendered or revoked.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 521.101, Transportation Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (m) to read as follows:  
|  _(m)_ _____The department shall adopt procedures for the automatic_  
| _issuance of a personal identification certificate to a person who_  
| _is 60 years of age or older at the time the person's driver's license_  
| _is surrendered or revoked. The procedures:_  
|  _(1)_ _____must be in compliance with federal guidelines_  
| _governing the issuance of identity documents; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____may require the person issued a personal_  
| _identification certificate to update information previously_  
| _provided to the department under Section 521.142._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
