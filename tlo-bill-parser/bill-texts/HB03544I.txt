|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to participation in and rates for coverage provided under  
| the uniform group coverage program for active school employees.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 1579, Insurance Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 1579.056 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___1579.056._ _____REGIONAL RATING AUTHORIZED._  
| _Notwithstanding any other provision of this chapter, the trustee by_  
| _rule may establish a regional rating method for determining_  
| _premiums charged in different regions of the state for the benefits_  
| _provided under a plan of group coverage established under the_  
| _program._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 1579.152, Insurance Code, is amended to  
| read as follows:  
|  Sec. 1579.152. PARTICIPATION OF [ ~~OTHER~~ ] SCHOOL DISTRICTS  
| _AND CERTAIN RISK POOLS_. Effective September 1, _2019_ [ ~~2005~~ ], a  
| school district _or a risk pool established under Chapter 172, Local_  
| _Government Code, as provided by Section 22.004, Education Code,_  
| _that is in existence on January 1, 2019, and whose members are_  
| _school districts_ [ ~~with more than 500 employees~~ ] may elect to  
| participate in the program. A school district _or risk pool_ that  
| elects to participate under this section shall apply for  
| participation in the manner prescribed by the trustee by rule.  
|  SECTION 3. Subchapter D, Chapter 1579, Insurance Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 1579.155 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___1579.155._ _____ELECTION NOT TO PARTICIPATE. A school_  
| _district or risk pool established under Chapter 172, Local_  
| _Government Code, as provided by Section 22.004, Education Code, may_  
| _elect not to participate in the program, notwithstanding any_  
| _previous election or requirement to participate._  
|  SECTION 4. Sections 1579.151 and 1579.153, Insurance Code,  
| are repealed.  
|  SECTION 5. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
