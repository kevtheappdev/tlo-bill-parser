|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the addition of territory to a crime control and  
| prevention district.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 363.181, Local Government Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  Sec. 363.181. ELECTION REQUIRED.  _(a)_ The governing body  
| that created a district may add all or part of the territory in the  
| political subdivision governed by that body to the district and the  
| district may impose a tax in that territory only if the addition of  
| the territory and the imposition of the tax are approved by a  
| majority of the qualified voters of the territory to be added voting  
| at an election held for that purpose.  
|  _(b)_ _____The municipality that created a district may add all or_  
| _part of the territory in the municipality's extraterritorial_  
| _jurisdiction to the district and the district may impose a tax in_  
| _that territory only if the addition of the territory and the_  
| _imposition of the tax are approved by a majority of the qualified_  
| _voters of the territory to be added voting at an election held for_  
| _that purpose._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
