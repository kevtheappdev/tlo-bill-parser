|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the authorization of the construction of an electric  
| railway on or across a road, street, alley, square, or property of a  
| county or municipality.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 131.014(d), Transportation Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (d) _A corporation described by Section 131.012 may not_  
| _construct_ [ ~~This section does not authorize the construction of~~ ] an  
| electric railway on or across a _road,_ street, alley, square, or  
| property of a _county or municipality unless the construction is_  
| _approved by the governing body of the county or_ municipality _, as_  
| _applicable_ [ ~~without the consent of the governing body of the~~  
|  ~~municipality~~ ].  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
