|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a written response by the General Land Office to a  
| request by a governmental entity to use certain real property owned  
| by the state.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 31, Natural Resources  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 31.069 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___31.069._ _____RESPONSE TO REQUEST TO USE CERTAIN REAL_  
| _PROPERTY OWNED BY STATE. (a)_ ___The commissioner or the_  
| _commissioner's designee shall respond in writing to a written_  
| _request by a political subdivision or state agency for approval of_  
| _the use of real property owned by the state that is administered by_  
| _the land office._  
|  _(b)_ _____A written response made under this section, if the_  
| _request is denied, must provide a reason for the denial._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
