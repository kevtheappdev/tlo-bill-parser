|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the creation of an additional judicial district  
| composed of Guadalupe County.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 24, Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 24.600 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___24.600._ _____456TH JUDICIAL DISTRICT (GUADALUPE COUNTY)._ __  
| _(a)_ ___The 456th Judicial District is composed of Guadalupe County._  
|  _(b)_ _____The 456th District Court shall give preference to civil_  
| _cases._  
|  SECTION 2. The 456th Judicial District is created on the  
| effective date of this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
