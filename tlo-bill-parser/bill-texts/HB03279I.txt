|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the content of certain Texas Real Estate Commission  
| contract forms for the sale of residential property.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 1101.155, Occupations Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (d) to read as follows:  
|  _(d)_ _____A contract form adopted by the commission for use in the_  
| _sale of residential property that is a single-family home, a_  
| _duplex, a triplex, or a fourplex must include a requirement that the_  
| _buyer disclose in the contract whether the buyer intends:_  
|  _(1)_ _____to market the contract and assign it to another_  
| _buyer; or_  
|  _(2)_ _____immediately on the closing of the sale, to sell the_  
| _property to another person in a transaction that will be effective_  
| _on the same date as the closing._  
|  SECTION 2. Not later than December 1, 2019, the Texas Real  
| Estate Commission shall adopt the contract form required by Section  
| 1101.155(d), Occupations Code, as added by this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
