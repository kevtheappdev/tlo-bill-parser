|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the duties of the Texas Veterans Commission regarding  
| citizenship of veterans.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 434, Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 434.026 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___434.026._ _____CITIZENSHIP OF VETERAN. (a) The Texas_  
| _Veterans Commission shall determine the citizenship status of a_  
| _veteran in this state._  
|  _(b)_ _____If a veteran in this state is not a citizen of the United_  
| _States, the commission shall assist the veteran in becoming a_  
| _United States citizen and facilitate the naturalization process._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
