|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to municipal control of coyotes.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 825, Health and Safety Code, is amended  
| by adding Subchapter D to read as follows:  
| _SUBCHAPTER D. CONTROL OF PREDATORY ANIMALS_  
|  _Sec._ ___825.051._ _____MUNICIPAL CONTROL OF COYOTES. A municipality_  
| _with a population density of more than 2,500 persons per square_  
| _mile may capture, relocate, or euthanize a coyote located within_  
| _the municipality or the municipality's extraterritorial_  
| _jurisdiction._ __  
| _The municipality may request assistance from Texas_  
| _Wildlife Services to capture, relocate, or euthanize a coyote._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
