|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the cessation of tolls on state highway segments in  
| certain counties.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 228, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 228.060 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___228.060._ _____CESSATION OF TOLLS RESULTING IN SAFETY HAZARD_  
| _IN CERTAIN COUNTIES. The department may not charge a toll, either_  
| _directly or indirectly through an agreement with another entity, on_  
| _the segment of State Highway 99 located in a county that:_  
|  _(1) has a population of less than 75,000;_  
|  _(2)_ _____adopts a resolution stating that the imposition of_  
| _tolls on the segment has created a safety hazard on another state_  
| _highway located in the county; and_  
|  _(3) submits the resolution to the department._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
