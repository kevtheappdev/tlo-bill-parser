|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to driving safety courses provided by certain  
| organizations.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 1001.002, Education Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsections (a-1) and (a-2) to read as follows:  
|  _(a-1)_ _____Except as provided by Subsection (a-2), an_  
| _organization is exempt from this chapter and any fee imposed under_  
| _this chapter if the organization:_  
|  _(1)_ _____is exempt from federal taxation under Section_  
| _501(a), Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as an organization described_  
| _by Section 501(c)(3) of that code; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____provides to individuals who are at least 50 years_  
| _of age a driving safety course that may be used for purposes of_  
| _Article 45.0511, Code of Criminal Procedure._  
|  _(a-2)_ _____Sections 1001.0561, 1001.351(b), 1001.451, and_  
| _1001.555 apply to an organization described by Subsection (a-1)._  
|  SECTION 2. Subchapter B, Chapter 1001, Education Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 1001.0561 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___1001.0561._ _____UNIFORM CERTIFICATES OF COURSE COMPLETION_  
| _ISSUED BY CERTAIN EXEMPT ORGANIZATIONS._ ___For an organization exempt_  
| _from this chapter under Section 1001.002(a-1):_  
|  _(1)_ _____the department shall provide the organization with_  
| _course completion certificate numbers to enable the organization to_  
| _issue original and duplicate department-approved uniform_  
| _certificates of course completion in a manner that, to the greatest_  
| _extent possible, prevents the unauthorized production or the misuse_  
| _of the certificates; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____the organization may purchase original or_  
| _duplicate certificate numbers for only one approved driving safety_  
| _course._  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
