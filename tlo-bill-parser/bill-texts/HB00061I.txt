|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the use of certain lighting equipment on escort flag  
| vehicles.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 547.305, Transportation Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (e-3) and amending Subsection (f) to read as  
| follows:  
|  _(e-3)_ _____An escort flag vehicle may be equipped with_  
| _alternating or flashing blue and amber lights._  
|  (f) In this section:  
|  (1) _"Escort flag vehicle" means a vehicle that_  
| _precedes or follows an oversize or overweight vehicle described by_  
| _Subtitle E for the purpose of facilitating the safe movement of the_  
| _oversize or overweight vehicle over roads._  
|  _(2)_ "Security patrol vehicle" means a motor vehicle  
| being used for the purpose of providing security services by:  
|  (A) a guard company described by Section  
| 1702.108, Occupations Code; or  
|  (B) a security officer as defined by Section  
| 1702.002, Occupations Code.  
|  _(3)_ [ ~~(2)~~ ] "Tow truck" means a motor vehicle or  
| mechanical device that is adapted or used to tow, winch, or move a  
| disabled vehicle.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
