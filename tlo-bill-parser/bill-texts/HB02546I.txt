|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the energy efficiency performance standards for  
| construction of industrialized housing or buildings.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter D, Chapter 1202, Occupations Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 1202.1536 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___1202.1536._ _____Notwithstanding Section 1202.151, a_  
| _manufacturer or builder of industrialized housing or buildings may_  
| _construct industrialized housing or buildings in accordance with_  
| _the energy efficiency performance standards outlined in:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the energy code described by Section 388.003(a),_  
| _Health and Safety Code; or_  
|  _(2)_ _____a local amendment to the energy code described by_  
| _Section 388.003(a), Health and Safety Code, that is:_  
|  _(A)_ _____applicable to the municipality or county in_  
| _which the industrialized housing or buildings will be located; and_  
|  _(B)_ _____determined by the laboratory, as defined by_  
| _Section 388.002, Health Safety Code, to be equally or more_  
| _stringent than the energy code described by Subdivision (1)._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
