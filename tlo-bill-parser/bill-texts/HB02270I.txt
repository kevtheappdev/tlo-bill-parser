|  AN ACT  
| relating to the delegation to the Railroad Commission of Texas of  
| purchasing functions regarding the reclamation of certain  
| abandoned mines.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 2155.150(a), Government Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  (a) The Railroad Commission of Texas is delegated all  
| purchasing functions relating to purchases under:  
|  (1) Chapter 89, Natural Resources Code; [ ~~or~~ ]  
|  (2) Sections 81.067 and 81.068, Natural Resources  
| Code _; and_  
|  _(3) Chapters 131 and 134, Natural Resources Code_.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
