|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the delegation of powers and duties by the Texas  
| Historical Commission.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 442, Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 442.0045 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___442.0045._ _____DELEGATION OF POWERS AND DUTIES. The_  
| _commission by order or rule may delegate to the executive director_  
| _the authority to perform the duties or exercise the powers of the_  
| _commission under this chapter or other law, including Chapter 191,_  
| _Natural Resources Code. A delegation under this section may be_  
| _amended or withdrawn by commission vote._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
