|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the use of job order contracts method by certain joint  
| airport boards.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 2269.402, Government Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (b) to read as follows:  
|  Sec. 2269.402. APPLICABILITY OF SUBCHAPTER TO BUILDINGS;  
| EXCEPTIONS. _(a)_ This subchapter applies only to a facility that is  
| a building, the design and construction of which is governed by  
| accepted building codes, or a structure or land, whether improved  
| or unimproved, that is associated with a building. This subchapter  
| does not apply to:  
|  (1) a highway, road, street, bridge, utility, water  
| supply project, water plant, wastewater plant, water and wastewater  
| distribution or conveyance facility, wharf, dock, airport runway or  
| taxiway, drainage project, or related type of project associated  
| with civil engineering construction; or  
|  (2) a building or structure that is incidental to a  
| project that is primarily a civil engineering construction project.  
|  _(b)_ _____Subsection (a) does not apply to a joint board created_  
| _under Section 22.074, Transportation Code, that:_  
|  _(1)_ _____is comprised of constituent agencies that are_  
| _home-rule municipalities with a population of more than 400,000,_  
| _and_  
|  _(2) operates a facility that:_  
|  _(A)_ _____is an airport roadway, bridge, runway,_  
| _taxiway, or building or structure incidental to an airport_  
| _roadway, bridge, runway, or taxiway, or related type of project_  
| _associated with civil engineering construction; and_  
|  _(B)_ _____for which an individual job, task or purchase_  
| _order for the facility do not exceed $300,000 and are for the_  
| _maintenance, repair, alteration, renovation, remediation, or_  
| _minor construction of a facility described in Subsection (b)(2)(A)._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution.  
| If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
