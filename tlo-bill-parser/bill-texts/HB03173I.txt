|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the creation of an additional judicial district  
| composed of Comal County.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1.  
|  Subchapter C, Chapter 24, Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 24.60010 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___24.60010._ _____466th JUDICIAL DISTRICT (COMAL COUNTY)._ ___The_  
| _466th Judicial District is composed of Comal County._  
|  SECTION 2. The 466th Judicial District is created on the  
| effective date of this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect January 1, 2021.
