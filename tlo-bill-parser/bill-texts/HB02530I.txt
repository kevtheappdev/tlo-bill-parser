|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the development by the Texas Veterans Commission of an  
| online repository of information of use to active duty military  
| relocating to this state.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 434, Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 434.026 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___434.026._ _____ONLINE REPOSITORY FOR ACTIVE DUTY MILITARY._  
| _(a)_ ___The Texas Veterans Commission shall develop and maintain on the_  
| _state electronic Internet portal project described by Section_  
| _2054.252 an online repository of information of use to active duty_  
| _members of the United States armed forces and their families_  
| _relocating to this state._  
|  _(b)_ _____The repository under Subsection (a) must include_  
| _information on assistance, fee waivers, and programs the state_  
| _provides to active duty members of the United States armed forces_  
| _and their families related to occupational and professional_  
| _licenses, education, and health care._  
|  _(c)_ _____A link to the repository developed under Subsection (a)_  
| _must be prominently displayed on the public home page of the state_  
| _electronic Internet portal project described by Section 2054.252._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
