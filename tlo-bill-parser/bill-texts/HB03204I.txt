|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the form of ballot propositions in an election seeking  
| authorization for the issuance of school district bonds supported  
| by property taxes.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 45.003, Education Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (a-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(a-1)_ _____The governing board or commissioners court may not_  
| _include more than one project in any proposition submitted to_  
| _authorize the issuance of bonds._  
|  SECTION 2. The change in law made by this Act applies only  
| to an election ordered on or after the effective date of this Act.  
| An election ordered before the effective date of this Act is  
| governed by the law in effect when the election was ordered.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
