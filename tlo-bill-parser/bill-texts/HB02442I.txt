|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating  
|  to the exchange of certain state property in Orange County  
| by the Public Safety Commission.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. (a)  The Public Safety Commission shall exchange  
| the real property described by Section 2 of this Act for other real  
| property if the other real property:  
|  (1) is located not more than 10 miles from the property  
| line of the real property described by Section 2 of this Act;  
|  (2) has at least an equivalent fair market value; and  
|  (3) has on the property a facility that the Public  
| Safety Commission determines is comparable to the facility located  
| on the property described by Section 2 of this Act.  
|  (b) The General Land Office shall negotiate and close a  
| transaction under this section on behalf of the Public Safety  
| Commission.  
|  (c) Section 31.158, Natural Resources Code, does not apply  
| to a transaction under this section.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act applies to the real property described
