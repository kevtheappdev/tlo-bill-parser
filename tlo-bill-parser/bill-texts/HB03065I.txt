|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to adoption by a stepparent.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 162, Family Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 162.0027 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___162.0027._ _____ADOPTION BY STEPPARENT. (a) In a suit for_  
| _adoption, a court or person performing an adoption evaluation may_  
| _not grant preference to a relative of the child over a stepparent of_  
| _the child solely on the basis of consanguinity._  
|  _(b)_ _____If a parent of the child dies, leaving the child with no_  
| _living parent whose parental rights have not been terminated, the_  
| _court may grant a stepparent's petition for adoption on an_  
| _expedited basis, provided that all requirements of this chapter are_  
| _fulfilled. The child may reside with the stepparent while the_  
| _adoption petition is pending, unless the court finds that would not_  
| _be in the best interest of the child._  
|  SECTION 2. The change in law made by this Act applies to a  
| petition for adoption filed on or after the effective date of this  
| Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
