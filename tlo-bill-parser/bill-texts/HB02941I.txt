|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to requiring each state agency to post on the agency's  
| Internet website information on suicide prevention for certain  
| individuals.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter F, Chapter 2054, Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 2054.137 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___2054.137._ _____VETERANS CRISIS LINE INFORMATION ON AGENCY_  
| _WEBSITE. Each state agency shall prominently post on the agency's_  
| _Internet website the phone number for the Veterans Crisis Line and a_  
| _link to the Veterans Crisis Line Internet website or any successor_  
| _website._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
