|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the use of certain license plates on classic motor  
| vehicles and travel trailers, custom vehicles, street rods, and  
| certain exhibition vehicles.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 504.202, Transportation Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (j) to read as follows:  
|  _(j)_ _____A person entitled to license plates under this section_  
| _may instead use, for a vehicle that meets the requirements of_  
| _Section 504.501 or 504.502, disabled veteran license plates issued_  
| _by this state that are:_  
|  _(1) embossed with an alphanumeric pattern;_  
|  _(2)_ _____of a plate design that was issued in the same year_  
| _as the model year of the vehicle; and_  
|  _(3) approved for use by the department._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 504.501, Transportation Code, is amended  
| by amending Subsection (b) and adding Subsection (b-1) to read as  
| follows:  
|  (b) A person eligible for the license plates may instead use  
| license plates that _are:_  
|  _(1) embossed with an alphanumeric pattern;_  
|  _(2) of a plate design that was_ [ ~~were~~ ] issued by this  
| state in the same year as the model year of the vehicle _;_ and  
|  _(3)_ [ ~~are~~ ] approved _for use_ by the department.  
|  _(b-1)_ The department may require the attachment of a  
| registration insignia to _a_ [ ~~the~~ ] license plate _described by_  
| _Subsection (b)_ in a manner that does not affect the display of  
| information originally on the license plate.  
|  SECTION 3. Section 504.502, Transportation Code, is amended  
| by amending Subsection (c) and adding Subsection (c-1) to read as  
| follows:  
|  (c) A person eligible for the license plates may instead use  
| license plates _that are:_  
|  _(1) embossed with an alphanumeric pattern;_  
|  _(2) of a plate design that was_ issued by this state in  
| the same year as the model year of the vehicle [ ~~and approved by the~~  
|  ~~department~~ ], provided that a passenger car must bear passenger car  
| or truck license plates and a truck must bear passenger car or truck  
| license plates _; and_  
|  _(3) approved for use by the department_.  
|  _(c-1)_ The department may require attachment of a  
| registration insignia to _a_ [ ~~the~~ ] license plate _described by_  
| _Subsection (c)_ in a manner that does not affect the display of  
| information originally on the license plate.  
|  SECTION 4. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
