|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to billing requirements for certain health care  
| facilities.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 324, Health and Safety  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 324.1015 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___324.1015._ _____BILLING STATEMENT; CIVIL LIABILITY. (a) A_  
| _facility's billing statement to a consumer must include:_  
|  _(1)_ _____an itemized list of each health care service_  
| _provided to the consumer;_  
|  _(2)_ _____an itemized list of each health care supply_  
| _provided to the consumer; and_  
|  _(3)_ _____a list of each physician or other health care_  
| _practitioner licensed to provide health care in this state who_  
| _provided a health care service to the consumer._  
|  _(b)_ _____A consumer is not liable to a facility for any billed_  
| _charge on a billing statement that violates this section. If a_  
| _facility files a civil action against a consumer for a charge_  
| _described by this subsection and the consumer prevails in the_  
| _action, the consumer may recover reasonable attorney's fees and_  
| _costs._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 324.1015, Health and Safety Code, as  
| added by this Act, applies only to a health care service provided on  
| or after the effective date of this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
