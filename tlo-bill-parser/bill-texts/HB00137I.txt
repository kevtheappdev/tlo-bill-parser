|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to reports by the Texas Commission on Environmental  
| Quality about dams that have certain hazard classifications.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 12.052, Water Code, is amended by adding  
| Subsection (e-3) to read as follows:  
|  _(e-3)_ _____For purposes of this subsection, the emergency_  
| _management director for a political subdivision is the person_  
| _designated the subdivision's emergency management director by_  
| _Section 418.1015, Government Code. The commission shall provide a_  
| _report of a dam that has a hazard classification of high or_  
| _significant to the emergency management director for the political_  
| _subdivision in which the dam is located. The commission shall make a_  
| _report to the emergency management director not later than:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the 48th hour after the time of the designation of_  
| _a dam as a high hazard classification; or_  
|  _(2)_ _____the 30th day after the date of the designation of a_  
| _dam as a significant hazard classification._  
|  SECTION 2. For each dam that, as of the effective date of  
| this Act, the executive director of the Texas Commission on  
| Environmental Quality maintains a classification of high or  
| significant hazard, the commission shall provide a report regarding  
| the hazard classification of the dam to the emergency management  
| director for the political subdivision in which the dam is located  
| as provided by Section 12.052(e-3), Water Code, as added by this  
| Act. Each report must be provided as soon as practicable and not  
| later than March 1, 2020.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
