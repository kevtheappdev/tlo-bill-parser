|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a political subdivision's adoption of zoning  
| regulations for certain coin-operated machines.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 2153.452, Occupations Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  Sec. 2153.452. ZONING.  _A_ [ ~~(a) For purposes of zoning, a~~ ]  
| political subdivision of this state _may adopt zoning regulations to_  
| _govern_ [ ~~shall treat~~ ] the exhibition of a music or skill or pleasure  
| coin-operated machine [ ~~in the same manner as the political~~  
|  ~~subdivision treats the principal use of the property where the~~  
|  ~~machine is exhibited~~ ].  
|  [ ~~(b)~~ ~~~~ ~~~~ ~~Subsection (a) does not prohibit a municipality
from~~  
|  ~~restricting the exhibition of a coin-operated amusement machine~~  
|  ~~within 300 feet of a church, school, or hospital.~~ ]  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
