|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the authority of the Department of Information  
| Resources to provide telecommunications services through statewide  
| technology centers.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 2054.376(a), Government Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  (a) This subchapter applies to all information resources  
| technologies[ ~~, other than telecommunications service, advanced~~  
|  ~~communications services, or information service, as those terms are~~  
|  ~~defined by 47 U.S.C. Section 153,~~ ] that are:  
|  (1) obtained by a state agency using state money;  
|  (2) used by a state agency; or  
|  (3) used by a participating local government.  
|  SECTION 2. Section 2054.378, Government Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (c) to read as follows:  
|  _(c)_ _____The department, on a cost-sharing basis, may provide_  
| _telecommunications service, advanced communications services, or_  
| _information service, as those terms are defined by 47 U.S.C._  
| _Section 153, through a statewide technology center under this_  
| _subchapter._  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
