|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the operation of a motor vehicle dealership by certain  
| manufacturers.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 2301.476, Occupations Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (p) to read as follows:  
|  _(p)_ _____A person who on January 1, 2018, held a manufacturer's_  
| _license issued under this chapter may:_  
|  _(1) hold:_  
|  _(A) a manufacturer's license;_  
|  _(B)_ _____a general distinguishing number issued under_  
| _Chapter 503, Transportation Code; and_  
|  _(C) one or more dealer's licenses; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____operate as both a manufacturer and dealer of motor_  
| _vehicles to the extent the person:_  
|  _(A)_ _____manufactures only motor vehicles as defined_  
| _by Section 2301.002(23)(C) and does not otherwise manufacture motor_  
| _vehicles as defined by Section 2301.002(23); and_  
|  _(B)_ _____provides substantially equal access to any_  
| _franchised dealer to all support for repairing a motor vehicle_  
| _described by Paragraph (A), including access to:_  
|  _(i) parts and assemblies;_  
|  _(ii)_ _____training and technical service_  
| _bulletins; and_  
|  _(iii) other repair information._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
