|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the computation of the standard service retirement  
| annuity for members of the Teacher Retirement System of Texas  
| enrolled in the Texas Public School Employees Group Insurance  
| Program.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 824.203(a), Government Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  (a) Except as provided by Subsections (c) and (d), _and_  
| _subject to Section 824.2035,_ the standard service retirement  
| annuity is an amount computed on the basis of the member's average  
| annual compensation for the five years of service, whether or not  
| consecutive, in which the member received the highest annual  
| compensation, times 2.3 percent for each year of service credit in  
| the retirement system.  
|  SECTION 2. Subchapter C, Chapter 824, Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 824.2035 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___824.2035._ _____STANDARD SERVICE RETIREMENT_  
| _BENEFITS:_ _____ADJUSTMENT OF PERCENTAGE FACTOR. (a)_ ___In this section,_  
| _"group program" and "health benefit plan" have the meanings_  
| _assigned by Section 1575.002, Insurance Code._  
|  _(b) The board of trustees shall:_  
|  _(1)_ _____study the out-of-pocket costs paid and projected_  
| _to be paid by annuitants for health benefit plan coverage provided_  
| _under the group program, including premiums, deductibles, and_  
| _prescription drug costs; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____based on the board's findings under Subdivision_  
| _(1) and notwithstanding any other law, by rule adopt a method or_  
| _formula for increasing the percentage factor used in the_  
| _computation of the standard service retirement annuity under_  
| _Section 824.203(a) for annuitants enrolled in the group program in_  
| _order to mitigate the impact projected out-of-pocket costs have on_  
| _the annuitants' service retirement annuities._  
|  SECTION 3. Not later than December 31, 2020, the board of  
| trustees of the Teacher Retirement System of Texas shall complete  
| the study and adopt the rule required by Section 824.2035,  
| Government Code, as added by this Act.  
|  SECTION 4. The changes in law made by this Act apply:  
|  (1) to a person who is an annuitant of the Teacher  
| Retirement System of Texas on or after September 1, 2021,  
| regardless of whether the person became an annuitant before, on, or  
| after that date; and  
|  (2) beginning with the first annuity payable on or  
| after September 1, 2021.  
|  SECTION 5. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
