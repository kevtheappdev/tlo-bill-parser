|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to collection of taxes and confiscation of unlawful  
| containers and illicit beverages by the Texas Alcoholic Beverage  
| Commission at certain ports of entry.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 5.37, Alcoholic Beverage Code, is  
| amended by adding Subsection (e) to read as follows:  
|  _(e)_ _____The commission may not engage in or allocate resources_  
| _for the collection of taxes and confiscation of unlawful containers_  
| _and illicit beverages at a port of entry facility located at a_  
| _cruise ship terminal._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
