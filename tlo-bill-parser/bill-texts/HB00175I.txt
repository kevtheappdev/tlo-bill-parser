|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the amount of late fees that may be charged to certain  
| tenants.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 92.019, Property Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (b-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(b-1)_ _____Notwithstanding Subsections (a) and (b), a landlord_  
| _may not charge a tenant who fails to pay rent for a rental pay period_  
| _and who participates in the housing choice voucher program under_  
| _Section 8, United States Housing Act of 1937 (42 U.S.C. Section_  
| _1437f), an initial late fee or daily late fees the total amount of_  
| _which would exceed five percent of the amount of rent owed by the_  
| _tenant during a rental pay period under the lease._  
|  SECTION 2. The change in law made by this Act applies to a  
| late fee charged on or after the effective date of this Act,  
| regardless of whether the conduct for which the fee was imposed was  
| committed before, on, or after that date.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
