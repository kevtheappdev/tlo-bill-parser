|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to certain contracts requiring competitive bidding by the  
| Texas Department of Transportation.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 223.001, Transportation Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  Sec. 223.001. CONTRACT REQUIRING COMPETITIVE BIDS.  _(a)_ __  
| _This section applies only to a highway that is part of the state_  
| _highway system._  
|  _(b)_ The department shall submit for competitive bids each  
| contract for:  
|  (1) the improvement of a highway [ ~~that is part of the~~  
|  ~~state highway system~~ ]; [ ~~or~~ ]  
|  (2) materials to be used in the construction or  
| maintenance of _a_ [ ~~that~~ ] highway _; or_  
|  _(3)_ _____traffic control or safety devices to be used on a_  
| _highway_.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act applies only in relation to a contract  
| for which the Texas Department of Transportation first advertises  
| or otherwise solicits bids, proposals, offers, or qualifications on  
| or after the effective date of this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
