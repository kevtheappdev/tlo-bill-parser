|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to transporting dogs in open truck or trailer beds in  
| certain counties.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter I, Chapter 545, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 545.4146 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___545.4146._ _____TRANSPORTING DOGS IN OPEN BEDS._ ___(a)_ ___This_  
| _section applies only to a vehicle being operated or a trailer being_  
| _drawn in a county with a population of more than 125,000._  
|  _(b)_ _____A person may not operate an open-bed pickup truck or an_  
| _open flatbed truck or draw an open flatbed trailer described by_  
| _Subsection (a) at a speed greater than 30 miles per hour when a dog_  
| _is occupying the bed of the truck or trailer unless the dog_  
| _occupying the bed of the truck or trailer is secured in a crate,_  
| _cage, or other closed container._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
