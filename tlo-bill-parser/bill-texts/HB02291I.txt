|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the operation of a gas-powered bicycle.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 502.143, Transportation Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  Sec. 502.143. OTHER VEHICLES. An owner may not register the  
| following vehicles for operation on a public highway:  
|  (1) power sweepers;  
|  (2) motorized mobility devices;  
|  (3) electric personal assistive mobility devices;  
| [ ~~and~~ ]  
|  (4) electric bicycles _; and_  
|  _(5)_ _____gas-powered bicycles, as defined by Section_  
| _541.201_.  
|  SECTION 2. Section 521.225, Transportation Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (e) to read as follows:  
|  _(e)_ _____For the purposes of this section, a gas-powered bicycle,_  
| _as defined by Section 541.201, is not a moped._  
|  SECTION 3. Section 541.201, Transportation Code, is amended  
| by adding Subdivision (4-a) and amending Subdivisions (8) and (11)  
| to read as follows:  
|  _(4-a) "Gas-powered bicycle" means a bicycle that:_  
|  _(A)_ _____is designed to be propelled by a gas motor,_  
| _exclusively or in combination with the application of human power;_  
|  _(B)_ _____cannot attain a speed of more than 20 miles_  
| _per hour without the application of human power; and_  
|  _(C) does not exceed 100 pounds._  
|  (8) "Moped" means a motor-driven cycle that cannot  
| attain a speed in one mile of more than 30 miles per hour _, has an_  
| [ ~~and the~~ ] engine _that_ [ ~~of which:~~  
|  [ ~~(A)~~ ] cannot produce more than two-brake  
| horsepower _,_ [ ~~;~~ ] and  
|  [ ~~(B)~~ ] if _the engine is_ an internal combustion  
| engine, has a piston displacement of 50 cubic centimeters or less  
| and connects to a power drive system that does not require the  
| operator to shift gears. _The term does not include a gas-powered_  
| _bicycle._  
|  (11) "Motor vehicle" means a self-propelled vehicle or  
| a vehicle that is propelled by electric power from overhead trolley  
| wires. The term does not include an electric bicycle _, a gas-powered_  
| _bicycle,_ or an electric personal assistive mobility device, as  
| defined by Section 551.201.  
|  SECTION 4. Section 551.002, Transportation Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  Sec. 551.002. MOPED _,_ [ ~~AND~~ ] ELECTRIC BICYCLE _, AND_  
| _GAS-POWERED BICYCLE_ INCLUDED. A provision of this subtitle  
| applicable to a bicycle also applies to:  
|  (1) a moped, other than a provision that by its nature  
| cannot apply to a moped; [ ~~and~~ ]  
|  (2) an electric bicycle, other than a provision that  
| by its nature cannot apply to an electric bicycle _; and_  
|  _(3)_ _____a gas-powered bicycle, other than a provision that_  
| _by its nature cannot apply to a gas-powered bicycle_.  
|  SECTION 5. Section 551.351(2), Transportation Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (2) "Pocket bike or minimotorbike" means a  
| self-propelled vehicle that is equipped with an electric motor or  
| internal combustion engine having a piston displacement of less  
| than 50 cubic centimeters, is designed to propel itself with not  
| more than two wheels in contact with the ground, has a seat or  
| saddle for the use of the operator, is not designed for use on a  
| highway, and is ineligible for a certificate of title under Chapter  
| 501. The term does not include:  
|  (A) a moped or motorcycle;  
|  (B) an electric bicycle _, gas-powered bicycle,_ or  
| motor-driven cycle, as defined by Section 541.201;  
|  (C) a motorized mobility device, as defined by  
| Section 542.009;  
|  (D) an electric personal assistive mobility  
| device, as defined by Section 551.201; or  
|  (E) a neighborhood electric vehicle, as defined  
| by Section 551.301.  
|  SECTION 6. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
