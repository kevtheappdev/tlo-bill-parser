|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to restrictions on municipal regulation in certain areas.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 229, Local Government Code, is amended  
| by adding Subchapter Z to read as follows:  
| _SUBCHAPTER Z._ ___MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS_  
|  _Sec._ ___229.901._ _____RESTRICTION ON MUNICIPAL REGULATION IN_  
| _CERTAIN AREAS. Notwithstanding any other law, in an area in which_  
| _the residents are ineligible or have only limited eligibility to_  
| _vote in municipal elections, a municipality may not regulate an_  
| _activity or structure that is regulated by this state._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
