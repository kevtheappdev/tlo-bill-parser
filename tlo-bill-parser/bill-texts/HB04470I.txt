|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to community development by municipal management  
| districts in certain counties.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter E, Chapter 375, Local Government  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 375.099 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___375.099._ _____COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT IN CERTAIN COUNTIES._  
| _(a)_ __  
| _This section applies only to a district in a county with a_  
| _population of more than two million that is adjacent to another_  
| _county with a population of more than one million._  
|  _(b)_ _____The district may develop and administer a community and_  
| _economic development program described by Section 381.004(b)(2),_  
| _(5), (6), or (7) in the same manner as a commissioners court._  
|  _(c)_ _____If the municipality creates a tax increment_  
| _reinvestment zone under Chapter 311, Tax Code, the municipality, by_  
| _contract with the district, may grant money deposited in the tax_  
| _increment fund to the district to be used by the district for the_  
| _purposes permitted for money granted to a corporation under Section_  
| _380.002(b), Local Government Code._  
|  _(d)_ _____The district may require that a person who receives a_  
| _financial incentive or benefit from the district for construction_  
| _or the development of property in the district provide support for a_  
| _community development project in the district._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
