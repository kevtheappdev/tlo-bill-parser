|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to establishing a minimum wage for public school  
| employees.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 62, Labor Code, is amended  
| by adding Section 62.058 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___62.058._ _____MINIMUM WAGE FOR PUBLIC SCHOOL EMPLOYEES. (a)_  
| _In this section:_  
|  _(1)_ _____"Open-enrollment charter school" means a school_  
| _that has been granted a charter under Subchapter D, Chapter 12,_  
| _Education Code._  
|  _(2)_ _____"School district" means any public school district_  
| _in this state._  
|  _(b)_ _____Notwithstanding Section 62.051 or 62.151 or any other_  
| _law, an open-enrollment charter school or school district shall pay_  
| _to each employee of the open-enrollment charter school or school_  
| _district not less than the greater of:_  
|  _(1) $15 an hour; or_  
|  _(2)_ _____the federal minimum wage under Section 6, Fair_  
| _Labor Standards Act of 1938 (29 U.S.C. Section 206)._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
