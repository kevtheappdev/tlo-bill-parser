|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the creation of additional county courts at law in  
| Hidalgo County.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 25.1101(a), Government Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  (a) Hidalgo County has the following statutory county  
| courts:  
|  (1) County Court at Law No. 1 of Hidalgo County;  
|  (2) County Court at Law No. 2 of Hidalgo County;  
|  (3) County Court at Law No. 4 of Hidalgo County;  
|  (4) County Court at Law No. 5 of Hidalgo County;  
|  (5) County Court at Law No. 6 of Hidalgo County;  
|  (6) County Court at Law No. 7 of Hidalgo County; [ ~~and~~ ]  
|  (7) County Court at Law No. 8 of Hidalgo County _;_  
|  _(8) County Court at Law No. 9 of Hidalgo County;_  
|  _(9) County Court at Law No. 10 of Hidalgo County;_  
|  _(10) County Court at Law No. 11 of Hidalgo County; and_  
|  _(11) County Court at Law No. 12 of Hidalgo County_.  
|  SECTION 2. Notwithstanding Section 25.0009, Government  
| Code, the initial vacancies in the offices of judge of County Court  
| at Law No. 9 of Hidalgo County and judge of County Court at Law  
| No. 10 of Hidalgo County shall be filled by election.  The offices  
| exist for purposes of the primary and general elections of 2022.  A  
| vacancy after the initial vacancies is filled as provided by  
| Section 25.0009, Government Code.  
|  SECTION 3. Notwithstanding Section 25.0009, Government  
| Code, the initial vacancies in the offices of judge of County Court  
| at Law No. 11 of Hidalgo County and judge of County Court at Law  
| No. 12 of Hidalgo County shall be filled by election.  The offices  
| exist for purposes of the primary and general elections of 2024.  A  
| vacancy after the initial vacancies is filled as provided by  
| Section 25.0009, Government Code.  
|  SECTION 4. County Court at Law No. 9 of Hidalgo County and  
| County Court at Law No. 10 of Hidalgo County are created on January  
| 1, 2023.  
|  SECTION 5. County Court at Law No. 11 of Hidalgo County and  
| County Court at Law No. 12 of Hidalgo County are created on January  
| 1, 2025.  
|  SECTION 6. This Act takes effect September 1, 2021.
