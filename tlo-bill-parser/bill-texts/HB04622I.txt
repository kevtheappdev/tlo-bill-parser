|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to alternative contributions to the Employee Retirement  
| System of Texas.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  _SECTION_ ___1_ _._ _____Subchapter B, Chapter 813, Government Code, is_  
| _amended by adding Section 813.105 to read as follows:_  
|  _Sec._ ___813.105._ _____ALTERNATIVE CONTRIBUTION METHODS TO_  
| _ESTABLISH OR REESTABLISH SERVICE CREDIT. (a)_ ___The board of trustees_  
| _shall adopt rules to provide procedures to establish or reestablish_  
| _credit in the retirement system for an eligible retired member to_  
| _make contributions to the retired member's individual account for_  
| _the purposes of:_  
|  (1) _designating funds for contributions to the_  
| _member's individual account as an employee or employer;_  
|  (2) _supplementing contributions made by a member prior_  
| _to retirement;_  
|  (3) _reconsideration of the calculation of the 36_  
| _highest months of pay; and_  
|  (4)  
| _reconsideration of the calculation of the retired_  
| _member's monthly retirement annuity._  
|  _(b)_ _____This section only applies to an eligible retired member_  
| _if they have an agreement with the State of Texas to establish or_  
| _reestablish credit prior to January 1st of 2019._  
|  _(c)_ _____The board of trustees shall adopt rules to administer_  
| _this section, including rules that impose restrictions on the_  
| _application of this section as necessary to cost-effectively_  
| _administer this section._  
|  _SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019._
