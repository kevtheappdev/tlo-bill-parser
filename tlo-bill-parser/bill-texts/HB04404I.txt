|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to prohibited practices by an insurer that issues a  
| preferred provider benefit plan regarding use of freestanding  
| emergency medical care facilities.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 1301, Insurance Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 1301.0059 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___1301.0059._ _____USE OF FREESTANDING EMERGENCY MEDICAL CARE_  
| _FACILITIES. It is an unfair method of competition or an unfair or_  
| _deceptive act or practice under Chapter 541 for an insurer to_  
| _encourage or instruct an insured to not receive health care_  
| _services from a freestanding emergency medical care facility._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
