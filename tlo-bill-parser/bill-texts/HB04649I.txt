|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the effect of municipal annexation of territory in the  
| Montgomery County Municipal Utility District No. 147.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 7907.001, Special District Local Laws  
| Code, is amended by adding Subsection (1-a) to read as follows:  
|  _(1-a) "City" means the City of Conroe, Texas._  
|  SECTION 2. Subchapter A, Chapter 7907, Special District  
| Local Laws Code, is amended by adding Section 7907.007 to read as  
| follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___7907.007._ _____EFFECT OF ANNEXATION. Notwithstanding any_  
| _other law, if all or any part of the territory of the district is_  
| _annexed by the city into the city's corporate limits, the district_  
| _retains all of the district's outstanding debt and obligations and_  
| _is not dissolved._  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
