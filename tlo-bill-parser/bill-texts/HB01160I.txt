|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the use of the compensatory education allotment for a  
| school district's school guidance and counseling program or  
| counseling or social work services.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 42.152, Education Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (c-6) to read as follows:  
|  _(c-6)_ _____Notwithstanding Subsection (c), funds allocated under_  
| _this section may be used to fund:_  
|  _(1)_ _____a district's school guidance and counseling_  
| _program under Subchapter A, Chapter 33; or_  
|  _(2)_ _____counseling or social work services provided to a_  
| _district by a licensed social worker, as defined by Section_  
| _505.002, Occupations Code, or a licensed professional counselor, as_  
| _defined by Section 503.002, Occupations Code._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
