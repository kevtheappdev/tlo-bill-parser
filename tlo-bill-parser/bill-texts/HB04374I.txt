|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to liability of prescribed burn directors in connection  
| with prescribed burns.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 153, Natural Resources  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 153.083 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___153.083._ _____LIMITATION OF PRESCRIBED BURN DIRECTOR_  
| _LIABILITY. (a) Except as provided by Subsection (b), an individual_  
| _directing a prescribed burn is not liable for property damage,_  
| _personal injury, or death caused by or resulting from the burn in_  
| _excess of the insurance requirements established by the board under_  
| _Section 153.046 if the individual has:_  
|  _(1)_ _____completed an accredited prescribed burning_  
| _training course approved by the board; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____satisfied the experience requirements prescribed_  
| _by the board._  
|  _(b)_ _____This section does not apply to an individual who commits_  
| _gross negligence or intentionally causes property damage, personal_  
| _injury, or death._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 153.083, Natural Resources Code, as  
| added by this Act, applies only to a cause of action that accrues on  
| or after the effective date of this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
