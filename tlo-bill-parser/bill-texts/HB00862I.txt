|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the calculation of charges for a gas utility customer  
| after a leak is detected.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter F, Chapter 104, Utilities Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 104.2571 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___104.2571._ _____ADJUSTMENT OF CHARGES ON DETECTION OF LEAK._  
| _(a)_ ___A gas distribution utility that detects a leak in the utility's_  
| _distribution system shall recompute charges for customers not later_  
| _than the 60th day after the date the leak was detected to ensure_  
| _that the customers are not charged for gas that was not delivered as_  
| _a result of the leak._  
|  _(b)_ _____The regulatory authority may adopt rules necessary to_  
| _implement this section._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
