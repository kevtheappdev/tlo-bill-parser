|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a rodeo hosted by the Texas Department of Criminal  
| Justice; authorizing a fee.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 493, Government Code, is amended by  
| adding Section 493.032 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___493.032._ _____DEPARTMENT RODEO._ ___(a)_ ___The department may_  
| _annually host a rodeo in Huntsville in which inmates participate on_  
| _a voluntary basis._  
|  _(b) The department may charge an admission fee to offset the_ __  
| _department's costs in hosting the rodeo._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
