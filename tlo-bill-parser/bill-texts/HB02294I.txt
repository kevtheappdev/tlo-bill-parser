|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to state contributions to fund the group insurance program  
| for retired school employees.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 1575.202(a), Insurance Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  (a) Each state fiscal year, the state shall contribute to  
| the fund an amount equal to _5_ [ ~~1.25~~ ] percent of the salary of each  
| active employee.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
