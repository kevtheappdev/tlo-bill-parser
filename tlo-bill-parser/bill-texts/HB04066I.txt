|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to repealing provisions prohibiting a physician assistant  
| from serving as a governing person or officer of certain business  
| entities.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Sections 22.0561(b), 152.0551(b), and  
| 301.012(a-2), Business Organizations Code, are repealed.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
