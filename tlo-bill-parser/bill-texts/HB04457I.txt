|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the minimum number of county jailers necessary to staff  
| a county jail.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 351, Local Government  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 351.048 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___351.048._ _____MINIMUM STAFF. (a)_ ___The sheriff or the_  
| _administrator of a county jail, as applicable, shall employ at_  
| _least one county jailer for every 48 inmates housed in the county_  
| _jail._  
|  _(b)_ _____The commissioners court of a county shall provide_  
| _sufficient funding for the employment of county jailers in the_  
| _number required by this section._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 351.048, Local Government Code, as added  
| by this Act, applies to the commissioners court of a county and to  
| the sheriff or jail administrator of a county beginning on the first  
| day of the first county fiscal year that begins after the effective  
| date of this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
