|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a challenge to a grand juror.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Article 19.31, Code of Criminal Procedure, is  
| amended by adding Subsection (b-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(b-1)_ _____The court shall allow the attorney representing the_  
| _state a reasonable opportunity to question a grand juror in order to_  
| _determine whether the juror may be challenged under Subsection_  
| _(a)(1), (2), (3), (4), (7), or (10)._  
|  SECTION 2. The change in law made by this Act applies to a  
| grand jury impaneled on or after the effective date of this Act. A  
| grand jury impaneled before the effective date of this Act is  
| governed by the law in effect on the date the grand jury was  
| impaneled, and the former law is continued in effect for that  
| purpose.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
