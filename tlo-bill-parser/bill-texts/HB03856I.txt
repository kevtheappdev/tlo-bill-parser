|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the costs of conducting a traffic study on the effects  
| of a development project proposed to be located in the county.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter Z, Chapter 240, Local Government  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 240.910 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___240.910._ _____TRAFFIC STUDY REQUIREMENT FOR DEVELOPMENT_  
| _PROJECTS. A commissioners court by order may require the developer_  
| _of a development project proposed to be located in the county to pay_  
| _the county for the cost of conducting a traffic study on the effects_  
| _the proposed development will have on roads in the county._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
