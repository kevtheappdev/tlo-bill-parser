|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the regulation of certain low-volume poultry processing  
| establishments.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 433.0245, Health and Safety Code, is  
| amended by adding Subsections (e) and (f) to read as follows:  
|  _(e)_ _____For the purposes of this section, a low-volume livestock_  
| _processing establishment:_  
|  _(1)_ _____includes a poultry processing establishment that_  
| _processes more than 1,000 but not more than 20,000 poultry raised by_  
| _the operator of the establishment in a calendar year; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____does not include a poultry processing_  
| _establishment that processes 1,000 or fewer poultry raised by the_  
| _operator of the establishment in a calendar year._  
|  _(f)_ _____A poultry processing establishment described by_  
| _Subsection (e)(2):_  
|  _(1) is not subject to additional state regulation; and_  
|  _(2) may sell poultry products directly to:_  
|  _(A) consumers; and_  
|  _(B)_ _____restaurants that provide food for immediate_  
| _human consumption._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
