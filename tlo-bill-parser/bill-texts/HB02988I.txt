|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a prohibition on the sale of prophylactics containing  
| nitrosamines.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 432, Health and Safety Code, is amended  
| by adding Section 432.0175 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___432.0175._ _____SALE OF PROPHYLACTIC CONTAINING_  
| _NITROSAMINES PROHIBITED. (a)_ ___In this section, "prophylactic"_ __  
| _means a device intended to prevent disease._ ___The term includes a_  
| _condom._  
|  _(b)_ _____A person may not sell a prophylactic containing_  
| _nitrosamines in this state._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
