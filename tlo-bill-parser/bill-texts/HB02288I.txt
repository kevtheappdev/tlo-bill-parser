|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the definition of a political subdivision for the  
| purpose of the operation of certain housing authorities.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 392.017, Local Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Subsection (b-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(b-1)_ _____For purposes of Subsection (b), a "political_  
| _subdivision" includes any subdivision of state government and any_  
| _subdivision or its equivalent body located in another state_  
|  
| _contiguous with El Paso County._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
