|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a study and report on the optimum water level of Falcon  
| Lake.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. (a)  The Texas Commission on Environmental  
| Quality and the Texas Parks and Wildlife Commission shall study and  
| report on the optimum water level of Falcon Lake.  
|  (b) In conducting the study the commissions shall include a  
| written report that includes:  
|  (1) an estimate of the optimum water level for  
| recreational fishing;  
|  (2) the factors that contribute to the rise and drop in  
| water levels;  
|  (3) recommendations on how to maintain the optimum  
| water level for recreational fishing on Falcon Lake.  
|  (c) Not later than December 1, 2020, the commission shall  
| submit to the governor, lieutenant governor, and speaker of the  
| house of representatives the report prepared under Subsection (b)  
| of this section.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act expires September 1, 2021.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
