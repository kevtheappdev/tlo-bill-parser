|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the duty of a county to refund an amount of $5 or less  
| paid to the county clerk.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter Z, Chapter 130, Local Government  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 130.911 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___130.911._ _____COUNTY REFUND OF SMALL AMOUNT._ ___A county is_  
| _not required to refund an amount overpaid or otherwise paid in error_  
| _to the county clerk by a person if:_  
|  _(1) that amount is $5 or less;_  
|  _(2)_ _____the county clerk notifies the person by mail of the_  
| _amount overpaid or paid in error and includes with the notice a form_  
| _for requesting a refund; and_  
|  _(3)_ _____the person fails to request a refund before the_  
| _90th day after the date the clerk mailed the notice._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
