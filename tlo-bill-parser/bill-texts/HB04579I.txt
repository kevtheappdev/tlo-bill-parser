|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the introduction of the appropriations bill.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 322.008, Government Code, is amended by  
| amending Subsection (a) and adding Subdivisions (1) and (2) to read  
| as follows:  
|  Sec. 322.008. APPROPRIATIONS BILL. (a)  The director,  
| under the direction of the board _and members of the House Committee_  
| _on Appropriations and Senate Committee on Finance_ , shall prepare  
| the general appropriations bill for introduction at each regular  
| legislative session _, provided that:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the members of the House Committee on_  
| _Appropriations and Senate Committee on Finance are presented with_  
| _the information for each recommended appropriation before the_  
| _appropriations bill is introduced; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____the members of the House Appropriations Committee_  
| _and Senate Committee on Finance authorize each appropriation in the_  
| _introduced appropriations bill before the appropriations bill is_  
| _introduced._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
