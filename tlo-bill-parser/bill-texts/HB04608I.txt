|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the authority of the Texas Department of Licensing and  
| Regulation to contract with municipalities and counties to perform  
| inspections of electrical work.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 1305, Occupations Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 1305.106 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___1305.106._ _____MUNICIPAL AND COUNTY INSPECTORS. The_  
| _department may contract with a municipal or county inspector to_  
| _enforce or assist in the enforcement of this chapter and rules_  
| _adopted under this chapter, including the performance of_  
| _inspections and investigations required under this chapter._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
