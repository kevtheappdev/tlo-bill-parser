|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the eligibility of school bus drivers for unemployment  
| compensation benefits.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 207.041, Labor Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (b-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(b-1)_ _____Subsection (b) does not apply to an individual if the_  
| _services performed by the individual for an educational institution_  
| _consist only of driving a school bus transporting students to and_  
| _from school or a school-sponsored or school-related activity._  
|  SECTION 2. The change in law made by this Act applies only  
| to a claim for unemployment compensation benefits filed with the  
| Texas Workforce Commission on or after the effective date of this  
| Act. A claim filed before the effective date of this Act is  
| governed by the law in effect on the date the claim was filed, and  
| the former law is continued in effect for that purpose.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
