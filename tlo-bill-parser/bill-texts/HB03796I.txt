|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to reducing the limitation on the maximum amount of gross  
| receipts taxes collected for combative sports events.  
|  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 2052.151, Occupations Code, is amended  
| by amending Subsection (b) to read as follows:  
|  (b) The tax is three percent of the gross receipts obtained  
| from the sale of tickets to the event, plus three percent of gross  
| receipts received from sales of broadcast rights or _$20,000_  
| [ ~~$30,000~~ ], whichever is less.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
