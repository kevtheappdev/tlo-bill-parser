|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to information on certain municipal utility districts'  
| Internet websites.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 54, Water Code, is amended  
| by adding Section 54.104 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___54.104._ _____INFORMATION ON CERTAIN DISTRICTS'_ ___INTERNET_  
| _WEBSITES. (a)_ ___This section applies only to a district that has 250_  
| _or more registered voters._  
|  _(b)_ _____A district shall publish on the district's Internet_  
| _website:_  
|  _(1) the directors' names;_  
|  _(2) contact information for the district;_  
|  _(3) the minutes for any meeting held by the district;_  
|  _(4)_ _____at least three days before the date of a meeting_  
| _held by the district, the agenda of the meeting; and_  
|  _(5)_ _____at least 30 days before the date of an election for_  
| _the board, information about the election, including the purpose of_  
| _the election._  
|  _(c)_ _____If appropriate, a district shall respond within a_  
| _reasonable period of time to a person who contacts the district_  
| _through the information listed on the district's Internet website_  
| _under Subsection (b)._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
