|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to costs associated with the statewide assessment program  
| of public school students.  
|  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 42.006, Education Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (a-6) to read as follows:  
|  _(a-6)_ _____The commissioner by rule shall provide means for each_  
| _school district and open-enrollment charter school to submit_  
| _through the Public Education Information Management System to the_  
| _agency the district's or school's total annual costs incurred_  
| _relating to the statewide assessment program under Subchapter B,_  
| _Chapter 39, including the costs for:_  
|  _(1) administration;_  
|  _(2) participation;_  
|  _(3) preparation; and_  
|  _(4) training._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
