|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to designating the third week of October as Veterinary  
| Technician Week.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter E, Chapter 662, Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 662.157 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___662.157._ _____VETERINARY TECHNICIAN WEEK. The third full_  
| _week in October is Veterinary Technician Week in recognition of the_  
| _critical role veterinary technicians play in the day-to-day_  
| _functions of veterinary practices and for their vital role in_  
| _preserving animal health and welfare._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
