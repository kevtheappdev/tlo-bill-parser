|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the issuance of specialty license plates for justices  
| of the peace.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter E, Chapter 504, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 504.4055 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___504.4055._ _____JUSTICES OF THE PEACE. The department shall_  
| _issue specialty license plates for current justices of the peace of_  
| _this state. The license plates must include the words "Justice of_  
| _the Peace."_  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
