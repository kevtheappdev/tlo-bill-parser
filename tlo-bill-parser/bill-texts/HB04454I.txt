|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the adoption of social and emotional learning standards  
| for public school educational programs.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter Z, Chapter 29, Education Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 29.9061 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___29.9061._ _____SOCIAL AND EMOTIONAL LEARNING. (a) The State_  
| _Board of Education shall adopt developmentally appropriate,_  
| _evidence-based standards for social and emotional learning,_  
| _including standards regarding:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the development of competency in self-management,_  
| _self-awareness, and social awareness for students; and_  
|  _(2) responsible decision-making._  
|  _(b)_ _____Each school district shall provide in appropriate_  
| _educational programs in each grade level the social and emotional_  
| _learning standards adopted under Subsection (a)._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
