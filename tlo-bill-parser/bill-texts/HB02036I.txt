|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the issuance of permits for the analysis of human  
| remains recovered on state archeological landmarks.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 191.054, Natural Resources Code, is  
| amended by adding Subsection (a-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(a-1)_ _____The committee may issue a permit to a person described_  
| _by Subsection (a) for analysis, including genetic analysis, of_  
| _human remains at, in, or on a landmark if it is the opinion of the_  
| _committee that the permit is in the best interest of the State of_  
| _Texas._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 191.131(b), Natural Resources Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (b) No person, firm, or corporation may conduct an operation  
| on any landmark _, including the analysis of human remains,_ without  
| first obtaining a permit and having the permit in _the person's,_  
| _firm's,_ [ ~~his~~ ] or _corporation's_ [ ~~its~~ ] possession at the site
of the  
| operation, or conduct the operation in violation of the provisions  
| of the permit.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
