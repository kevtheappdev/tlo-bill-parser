|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to prohibiting the use of money in the Texas economic  
| development fund or the Texas Enterprise Fund to finance a facility  
| or establishment at which gambling is to be conducted.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 12.0273, Agriculture Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (a-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(a-1)_ _____Money in the fund may not be used to finance a facility_  
| _or establishment at which gambling is to be conducted. The term_  
| _"gambling" includes playing bingo for a prize and wagering on_  
| _greyhound or horse races._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 481.078, Government Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (e-2) to read as follows:  
|  _(e-2)_ _____The governor may not award a grant to an entity to_  
| _finance a facility or establishment at which gambling is to be_  
| _conducted. The term "gambling" includes playing bingo for a prize_  
| _and wagering on greyhound or horse races._  
|  SECTION 3. The changes in law made by this Act apply only to  
| a loan or grant made on or after the effective date of this Act. A  
| loan or grant made before the effective date of this Act is governed  
| by the law in effect on the date the loan or grant was made, and the  
| former law is continued in effect for that purpose.  
|  SECTION 4. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
