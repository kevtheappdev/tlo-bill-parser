|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to electronic correction of voter registration  
| information by a person on the suspense list.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 15.021(d), Election Code, is amended to  
| read as follows:  
|  (d) A voter _, including a voter on the suspense list,_ who  
| continues to reside in the county in which the voter is registered  
| may correct information under this section by digital transmission  
| of the information under a program administered by the secretary of  
| state and the Department of Information Resources. _A voter on the_  
| _suspense list who submits a correction under this subsection is_  
| _considered to have complied with the requirements of Section_  
| _15.053._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
