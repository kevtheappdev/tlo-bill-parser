|  363.262. EFFECTIVE DATE OF TAX CHANGE.(a) If less than a  
| majority of the votes cast in a continuation referendum are for the  
| continuation of the district or if a majority of the votes cast in a  
| dissolution referendum are for dissolution of the district, the  
| board shall notify the comptroller in writing of the results of the
