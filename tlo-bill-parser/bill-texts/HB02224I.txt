|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to public awareness of utility payment assistance programs  
| and consumer rights during a disaster.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 17, Utilities Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 17.010 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___17.010._ _____DISASTER BILLING AWARENESS. (a) The_  
| _commission in cooperation with the Texas Division of Emergency_  
| _Management and the Railroad Commission of Texas shall:_  
|  _(1)_ _____promote public awareness of bill payment_  
| _assistance available during a disaster for electric, gas,_  
| _telecommunications, water, and wastewater services; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____provide the public with information about billing_  
| _practices during a disaster to ensure that consumers of electric,_  
| _gas, telecommunications, water, and wastewater services have an_  
| _adequate understanding of their rights._  
|  _(b)_ _____Customer awareness efforts by the commission must be_  
| _conducted in English and Spanish and any other language as_  
| _necessary to further the purposes of Subsection (a)._  
|  SECTION 2. Not later than December 1, 2019, the Texas  
| Division of Emergency Management, the Public Utility Commission of  
| Texas, and the Railroad Commission of Texas shall begin the public  
| awareness campaign required by Section 17.010, Utilities Code, as  
| added by this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
