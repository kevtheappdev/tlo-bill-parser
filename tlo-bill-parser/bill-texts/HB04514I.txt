|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the requirement that the Texas Veterans Commission  
| provide training to support the veterans mental health program.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 434.353, Government Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (c) to read as follows:  
|  _(c)_ _____The commission shall cross-train agency staff to_  
| _support the mental health program for veterans developed under_  
| _Chapter 1001, Health and Safety Code._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
