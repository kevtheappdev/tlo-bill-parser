|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the issuance of 173rd Airborne Brigade specialty  
| license plates.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter D, Chapter 504, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 504.325 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___504.325._ _____173RD AIRBORNE BRIGADE. The department shall_  
| _issue specialty license plates for persons who have served in the_  
| _173rd Airborne Brigade of the United States Army._ ___The license_  
| _plates must include the 173rd Airborne Brigade emblem and must_  
| _include the words "173rd Airborne Brigade" at the bottom of each_  
| _plate._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
