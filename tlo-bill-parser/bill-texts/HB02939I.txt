|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the operation of certain aggregate production  
| operations.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. The heading to Chapter 28A, Water Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
| CHAPTER 28A.  _REGULATION_ [ ~~REGISTRATION AND INSPECTION~~ ] OF CERTAIN  
| AGGREGATE PRODUCTION OPERATIONS  
|  SECTION 2. The heading to Subchapter B, Chapter 28A, Water  
| Code, is amended to read as follows:  
| SUBCHAPTER B.  REGISTRATION _,_ [ ~~AND~~ ] INSPECTION _, AND OPERATION_  
|  SECTION 3. Subchapter B, Chapter 28A, Water Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 28A.055 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___28A.055._ _____HOURS OF OPERATION. An aggregate production_  
| _operation located within one-half mile of a single-family residence_  
| _or hospital may operate only between the hours of 7 a.m. and 9 p.m._  
|  SECTION 4. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
