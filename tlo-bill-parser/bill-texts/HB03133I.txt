|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the use of personal leave during school holidays by  
| school district employees.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 22.003, Education Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (g) to read as follows:  
|  _(g)_ _____A school district employee with available personal_  
| _leave under this section is entitled to use the leave for_  
| _compensation for a day designated as a school holiday for which the_  
| _employee would otherwise not receive compensation._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
