|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to warning signs posted by certain food service  
| establishments that prepare food items containing peanut products.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 438, Health and Safety Code, is amended  
| by adding Subchapter E to read as follows:  
| _SUBCHAPTER E. PEANUT ALLERGEN WARNING SIGNS_  
|  _Sec._ ___438.051._ _____APPLICABILITY. This subchapter applies only_  
| _to a food service establishment:_  
|  _(1)_ _____with a designed or designated space for eating;_  
| _and_  
|  _(2)_ _____that prepares a food item containing peanuts or a_  
| _peanut product as an ingredient in the food item._  
|  _Sec._ ___438.052._ _____WARNING SIGNS REQUIRED FOR PEANUT PRODUCTS._  
| _A food service establishment described by Section 438.051 shall_  
| _post in a conspicuous place in the area of the establishment in_  
| _which food is consumed a sign that states:_  
|  _"This establishment prepares food items that contain peanut_  
| _products."_  
|  _Sec._ ___438.053._ _____RULES. The executive commissioner shall_  
| _adopt any rules necessary to implement this subchapter._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect January 1, 2020.
