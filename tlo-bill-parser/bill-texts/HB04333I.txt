|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to providing free full-day prekindergarten for certain  
| children.

| _Subsection (b)(1)_ [ ~~at least three years of age~~ ]. A district may not  
| charge tuition for a prekindergarten class offered under this

| class under this section if the child is _:_  
|  _(1)_ at least three years of age and:  
|  _(A)_ [ ~~(1)~~ ] is unable to speak and comprehend the

|  _(D)_ [ ~~(4)~~ ] is the child of an active duty member  
| of the armed forces of the United States, including the state  
| military forces or a reserve component of the armed forces, who is  
| ordered to active duty by proper authority;  
|  _(E)_ [ ~~(5)~~ ] is the child of a member of the armed
