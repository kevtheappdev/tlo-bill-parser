|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to enforcement of rules for certain county and municipal  
| parks, playgrounds, historical museums, or historic or prehistoric  
| sites; creating a criminal offense.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 331, Local Government Code, is amended  
| by adding Section 331.0075 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___331.0075._ _____ENFORCEMENT OF RULES IN CERTAIN COUNTIES AND_  
| _MUNICIPALITIES; OFFENSE. (a) This section applies only to:_  
|  _(1)_ _____a county with a population of more than 410,000 and_  
| _less than 455,000; or_  
|  _(2)_ _____a municipality any part of which is located in a_  
| _county described by Subdivision (1)._  
|  _(b)_ _____The governing body of a county or municipality may adopt_  
| _an order or ordinance prohibiting a violation of a rule prescribed_  
| _under Section 331.007 for a park, playground, historical museum, or_  
| _historic or prehistoric site maintained by the county or_  
| _municipality._  
|  _(c)_ _____A person commits an offense if the person violates an_  
| _order or ordinance adopted under Subsection (b)._ ___An offense under_  
| _this subsection is a Class C misdemeanor._  
|  _(d)_ _____For purposes of Subsection (b), the governing body of_  
| _the county or municipality may choose which of a park's,_  
| _playground's, museum's, or site's rules to enforce under that_  
| _subsection and is not required to enforce all of a park's,_  
| _playground's, museum's, or site's rules under that subsection._  
|  _(e)_ _____Fines collected under Subsection (b) shall be deposited_  
| _in the general fund of the municipality or county._  
|  SECTION 2. The heading to Section 331.007, Local Government  
| Code, is amended to read as follows:  
|  Sec. 331.007. PUBLIC USE _; RULES_.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
