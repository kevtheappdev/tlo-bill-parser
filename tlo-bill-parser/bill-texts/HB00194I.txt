|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the minimum wage.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 62.051, Labor Code, is amended to read as  
| follows:  
|  Sec. 62.051. MINIMUM WAGE. Except as provided by Section  
| 62.057, an employer shall pay to each employee _not less than the_  
| _greater of:_  
|  _(1) $15.00 an hour; or_  
|  _(2)_ the federal minimum wage under Section 6, Fair  
| Labor Standards Act of 1938 (29 U.S.C. Section 206).  
|  SECTION 2. Section 62.151, Labor Code, is repealed.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
