|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to an exemption for certain small producers from  
| regulations on nursery products and florist items.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 71, Agriculture Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 71.0415 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___71.0415._ _____APPLICABILITY. This chapter does not apply_  
| _to a florist or nursery grower with less than $25,000 in annual_  
| _receipts from the sale or lease of florist items and nursery_  
| _products._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
