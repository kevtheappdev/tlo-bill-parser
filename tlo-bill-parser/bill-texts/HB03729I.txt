|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to creating the criminal offense of possession of an  
| animal by a person who has been previously convicted of an offense  
| involving animal cruelty.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 42, Penal Code, is amended by adding  
| Section 42.107 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___42.107._ _____POSSESSION OF ANIMAL BY PERSON CONVICTED OF_  
| _ANIMAL CRUELTY. (a)_ ___A person commits an offense if the person:_  
|  _(1) possesses an animal; and_  
|  _(2) has been previously convicted of an offense under:_  
|  _(A)_ _____Section 42.09, 42.091, 42.092, 42.10, or_  
| _42.105; or_  
|  _(B)_ _____federal law or a penal law of another state_  
| _containing elements that are substantially similar to the elements_  
| _of an offense described by Paragraph (A)._  
|  _(b) An offense under this section is a Class B misdemeanor._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
