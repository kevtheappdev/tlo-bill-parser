|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to advertising or other information regarding licensed  
| bail bond sureties posted in secure correctional facilities.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 1704, Occupations Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 1704.106 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___1704.106._ _____ADVERTISING BY LICENSED BAIL BOND SURETY._ ___A_  
| _secure correctional facility, as defined by Section 1.07, Penal_  
| _Code, may post in the facility an advertisement or other_  
| _information regarding a bail bond surety or an agent of a corporate_  
| _surety licensed in the county where the facility is located._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
