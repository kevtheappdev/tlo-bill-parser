|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the provision of pharmacy services through a  
| telepharmacy system located at a remote dispensing site.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Sections 562.110(h) and (i), Occupations Code,  
| as added by Chapter 929 (S.B. 1633), Acts of the 85th Legislature,  
| Regular Session, 2017, are amended to read as follows:  
|  (h) _A_ [ ~~Except as provided by Subsection (j), a~~ ] telepharmacy  
| system located at a remote dispensing site under Subsection (d)(2)  
| may not be located within _five_ [ ~~25~~ ] miles by road of a Class A  
| pharmacy.  
|  (i) _If_ [ ~~Except as provided by Subsection (j), if~~ ] a Class A  
| pharmacy is established within _five_ [ ~~25~~ ] miles by road of a remote  
| dispensing site that is currently operating, the remote dispensing  
| site may continue to operate at that location.  
|  SECTION 2. (a) Sections 562.110(g), (h), and (i),  
| Occupations Code, as added by Chapter 485 (H.B. 2561), Acts of the  
| 85th Legislature, Regular Session, 2017, are repealed.  
|  (b) Section 562.110(j), Occupations Code, is repealed.  
|  SECTION 3. To the extent of any conflict, this Act prevails  
| over another Act of the 86th Legislature, Regular Session, 2019,  
| relating to nonsubstantive additions to and corrections in enacted  
| codes.  
|  SECTION 4. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
