|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a prohibition on contractors acting as public insurance  
| adjusters in certain circumstances.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. The heading to Section 4102.163, Insurance Code,  
| is amended to read as follows:  
|  Sec. 4102.163. CERTAIN _CONTRACTOR_ [ ~~ROOFING-RELATED~~ ]  
| BUSINESS PROHIBITED.  
|  SECTION 2. Section 4102.163(a), Insurance Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  (a) A [ ~~roofing~~ ] contractor may not act as a public adjuster  
| or advertise to adjust claims for any property for which the  
| contractor is providing or may provide _contracting_ [ ~~roofing~~ ]  
| services, regardless of whether the contractor holds a license  
| under this chapter.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
