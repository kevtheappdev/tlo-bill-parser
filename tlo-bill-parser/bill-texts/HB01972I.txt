|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to municipal regulation of game rooms and amusement  
| redemption machines.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 234.140, Local Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Subsection (d) to read as follows:  
|  _(d)_ _____This subchapter may not be construed to limit the_  
| _authority of a home-rule municipality to enforce a zoning_  
| _regulation adopted by the municipality to regulate game rooms or_  
| _amusement redemption machines._  
|  SECTION 2. Subchapter E, Chapter 234, Local Government  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 234.141 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___234.141._ _____INTERLOCAL AGREEMENT. A county may enter_  
| _into an interlocal agreement with a municipality to regulate the_  
| _operation of game rooms or amusement redemption machines._  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
