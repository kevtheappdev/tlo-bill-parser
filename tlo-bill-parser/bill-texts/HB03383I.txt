|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the definition of a tier 1 county for purposes of  
| municipal annexation.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 43.001(2), Local Government Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (2) "Tier 1 county" means a county:  
|  (A) with a population of less than 500,000; and  
|  (B) that is not _:_  
|  (i) a county that contains a freshwater  
| fisheries center operated by the Texas Parks and Wildlife  
| Department _; or_  
|  _(ii) a county with a population of less than_  
| _125,000 that:_  
|  _(a)_ _____is contiguous to a county with a_  
| _population of more than 2.2 million;_  
|  _(b)_ _____contains any portion of the_  
| _extraterritorial jurisdiction of a municipality with a population_  
| _of more than 1.1 million; and_  
|  _(c)_ _____has more than one municipal_  
| _utility district registered with the Texas Commission on_  
| _Environmental Quality as being active._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution.  If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
