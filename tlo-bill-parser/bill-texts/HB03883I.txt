|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to development of the form for the annual immunization  
| status report by public schools.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 38.002(c), Education Code, is amended to  
| read as follows:  
|  (c) The [ ~~Texas Education Agency and the Texas~~ ] Department of  
| _State_ Health _Services_ shall develop the form for a required annual  
| report of the immunization status of students. The report shall be  
| submitted by all schools at the time and in the manner indicated in  
| the instructions printed on the form.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
