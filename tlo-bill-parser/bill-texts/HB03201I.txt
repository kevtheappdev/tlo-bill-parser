|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to affiliation with a political party by voting by mail in  
| a primary election.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 162.003, Election Code, is amended to  
| read as follows:  
|  Sec. 162.003. AFFILIATION BY VOTING IN PRIMARY. A person  
| becomes affiliated with a political party when the person:  
|  (1) is accepted to vote in the party's primary  
| election; or  
|  (2) _returns_ [ ~~applies for and is provided~~ ] an early  
| voting or limited primary ballot _as a marked ballot_ [ ~~to be~~ ] voted by  
| mail.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
