|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to peace officer education and training on individuals  
| with disabilities.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 1701.253, Occupations Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (p) to read as follows:  
|  _(p)_ _____As part of the minimum curriculum requirements, the_  
| _commission shall require an officer to complete a comprehensive_  
| _education and training program on identifying, communicating with,_  
| _and supporting individuals with disabilities._ ___An officer shall_  
| _complete the program required under this subsection not later than_  
| _the second anniversary of the date the officer is licensed under_  
| _this chapter unless the officer completes the program as part of the_  
| _officer's basic training course._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 1701.402, Occupations Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (o) to read as follows:  
|  _(o)_ _____As a requirement for an intermediate or advanced_  
| _proficiency certificate issued by the commission, an officer must_  
| _complete the comprehensive education and training program on_  
| _identifying, communicating with, and supporting individuals with_  
| _disabilities established by the commission under Section_  
| _1701.253(p)._  
|  SECTION 3. As soon as practicable after the effective date  
| of this Act, the Texas Commission on Law Enforcement shall adopt the  
| rules necessary to implement Sections 1701.253(p) and 1701.402(o),  
| Occupations Code, as added by this Act.  
|  SECTION 4. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
