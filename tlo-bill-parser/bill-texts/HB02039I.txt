|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the issuance of specialty license plates to Master Army  
| Aviators.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter D, Chapter 504, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 504.325 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___504.325._ _____MASTER ARMY AVIATOR. The department shall_  
| _issue specialty license plates for persons awarded the Master Army_  
| _Aviator Badge. The license plates must include:_  
|  _(1) the words "Master Army Aviator"; and_  
|  _(2) a depiction of the Master Army Aviator Badge._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
