|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a study on the improvement of driver's license issuance  
| in this state.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. STUDY AND REPORT. (a) The Department of Public  
| Safety of the State of Texas shall contract with a third party  
| entity to conduct a study and create a report on improving the  
| process of issuing driver's licenses in this state.  
|  (b) The study must examine:  
|  (1) the procedures for issuing driver's licenses in  
| other states;  
|  (2) technological advancements in the issuance of  
| driver's licenses, including the development of digital driver's  
| licenses;  
|  (3) the process for issuing driver's licenses in this  
| state, including metrics on the amount of time between application  
| for a driver's license and receipt of a driver's license; and  
|  (4) best practices for issuing driver's licenses.  
|  (c) Not later than May 1, 2020, the Department of Public  
| Safety of the State of Texas shall submit to the governor,  
| lieutenant governor, and members of the legislature the report  
| created under Subsection (a) of this section that includes:  
|  (1) the findings of the study conducted under this  
| section; and  
|  (2) any recommendations to improve the process for  
| issuing driver's licenses in this state.  
|  SECTION 2. EXPIRATION DATE. This Act expires September 1,  
| 2020.  
|  SECTION 3. EFFECTIVE DATE. This Act takes effect  
| immediately if it receives a vote of two-thirds of all the members  
| elected to each house, as provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas  
| Constitution. If this Act does not receive the vote necessary for  
| immediate effect, this Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
