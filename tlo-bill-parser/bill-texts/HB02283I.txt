|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the placement and construction of a memorial plaza  
| commemorating the Bill of Rights on the Supreme Court Building  
| plaza.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 443, Government Code, is amended by  
| adding Section 443.0141 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___443.0141._ _____BILL OF RIGHTS PLAZA. (a) Subject to_  
| _Subsection (b), the State Preservation Board and the Texas_  
| _Facilities Commission may:_  
|  _(1)_ _____authorize the placement and construction of a_  
| _memorial plaza commemorating the federal Bill of Rights on the_  
| _plaza in front of the Supreme Court Building; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____jointly determine matters relating to the_  
| _location, content, design, construction, and maintenance of the_  
| _memorial plaza on property under the jurisdiction of the board or_  
| _the commission._  
|  _(b)_ _____Public money may not be used to finance the design or_  
| _construction of the memorial plaza._  
|  _(c)_ _____The board and the commission may adopt rules necessary_  
| _to implement and administer this section._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
