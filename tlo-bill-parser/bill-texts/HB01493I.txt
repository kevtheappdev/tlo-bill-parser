|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to weight limitations for over-the-road buses.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 621.101, Transportation Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (a-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(a-1)_ _____Subsection (a) does not apply to an over-the-road bus_  
| _as defined by 42 U.S.C. Section 12181._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
