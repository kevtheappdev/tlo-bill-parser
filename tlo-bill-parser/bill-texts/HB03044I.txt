|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to political expenditures made by a corporation to finance  
| the solicitation of political contributions to a political  
| committee.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 253.100(b), Election Code, is amended to  
| read as follows:  
|  (b) A corporation may make political expenditures to  
| finance the solicitation of political contributions to a  
| general-purpose committee assisted under Subsection (a) from the  
| stockholders, employees, or families of stockholders or employees  
| of one or more corporations _, including soliciting political_  
| _contributions by making charitable contributions that fully or_  
| _partially match the political contributions_.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
