|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the funding of certain ship channel improvements.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 56.001(1), Transportation Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (1) " _Board_ [ ~~Commission~~ ]" means the Texas _Water_  
| _Development Board_ [ ~~Transportation Commission~~ ].  
|  SECTION 2. Sections 56.002(a), (b), and (c), Transportation  
| Code, are amended to read as follows:  
|  (a) The ship channel improvement revolving fund is an  
| account in the general revenue fund. The fund is administered by  
| the _board_ [ ~~commission~~ ].  
|  (b) The following money shall be credited to the fund:  
|  (1) money the _board_ [ ~~commission~~ ] receives as a gift,  
| grant, or donation for a purpose of this chapter;  
|  (2) money appropriated to the _board_ [ ~~commission~~ ] for  
| the purposes of this chapter;  
|  (3) money received by the _board_ [ ~~commission~~ ] for the  
| repayment of a loan made by the program established under Section  
| 56.003; and  
|  (4) interest earned on deposits and investments of the  
| fund.  
|  (c) Money in the fund may be appropriated only to the _board_  
| [ ~~commission~~ ] for the purposes of administering the fund and the  
| program established under Section 56.003.  
|  SECTION 3. Sections 56.003(a) and (b), Transportation Code,  
| are amended to read as follows:  
|  (a) The _board_ [ ~~commission~~ ] by rule shall establish a  
| revolving loan program to use money from the fund to finance  
| qualified projects for navigation districts.  
|  (b) To be a qualified project, a project must:  
|  (1) deepen or widen a ship channel;  
|  (2) be authorized by the United States Congress  
| _through the Water Resources Development Act_ ; and  
|  (3) meet any other standards provided by _board_  
| [ ~~commission~~ ] rule.  
|  SECTION 4. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
