|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to an exemption from the plumbing licensing law for  
| plumbing work performed on certain private property.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 1301, Occupations Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 1301.059 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___1301.059._ _____PLUMBING ON CERTAIN PRIVATE PROPERTY. (a)_ ___A_  
| _person is not required to be licensed under this chapter to perform_  
| _plumbing work consisting of installing, servicing, or repairing_  
| _service mains, service lines, appurtenances, equipment, or_  
| _appliances that provide water, sewer, or storm drainage services on_  
| _private property in an area that extends from a public right of way_  
| _or public easement to not less than five feet from a building or_  
| _structure._  
|  _(b)_ _____This section does not apply to plumbing work performed_  
| _on private property designated for use as a one-family or_  
| _two-family dwelling._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
