|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to patient medical records regarding maternal death.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter J, Chapter 1001, Health and Safety  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 1001.242 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___1001.242._ _____REQUEST FOR MEDICAL RECORDS RELATED TO_  
| _MATERNAL DEATH;_ ___REPORT. (a) Not later than the 30th business day_  
| _after receiving a request from the department, a hospital, birthing_  
| _center, or other custodian of a patient's medical records shall_  
| _submit to the department the medical records of a patient who was_  
| _pregnant at the time of death or was pregnant at any time in the year_  
| _preceding the patient's death._  
|  _(b)_ _____Any personally identifying information in a patient's_  
| _medical records acquired by the department under this section is_  
| _confidential and is not subject to disclosure under Chapter 552,_  
| _Government Code._  
|  _(c)_ _____The department shall annually prepare and submit to the_  
| _legislature a written report that includes:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the average length of time for a hospital,_  
| _birthing center, or other custodian of a patient's medical records_  
| _to submit to the department the medical records requested under_  
| _Subsection (a); and_  
|  _(2)_ _____as applicable, the name and address of any_  
| _hospital, birthing center, or other custodian of a patient's_  
| _medical records who fails to comply with Subsection (a)._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
