|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the repeal of the provisions providing for the  
| suspension of the collection of certain fees when the balance in the  
| oil and gas regulation and cleanup fund exceeds a specified amount.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 81.116(d), Natural Resources Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (d) [ ~~The comptroller shall suspend collection of the fee in~~  
|  ~~the manner provided by Section 81.067.~~ ] The exemptions and  
| reductions set out in Sections 202.052, 202.054, 202.056, 202.057,  
| 202.059, and 202.060, Tax Code, do not affect the fee imposed by  
| this section.  
|  SECTION 2. Section 81.117(d), Natural Resources Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (d) [ ~~The comptroller shall suspend collection of the fee in~~  
|  ~~the manner provided by Section 81.067.~~ ] The exemptions and  
| reductions set out in Sections 201.053, 201.057, 201.058, and  
| 202.060, Tax Code, do not affect the fee imposed by this section.  
|  SECTION 3. Section 81.067(b), Natural Resources Code, is  
| repealed.  
|  SECTION 4. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
