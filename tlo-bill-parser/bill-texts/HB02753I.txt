|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the requirement that a public video broadcast of an open  
| meeting of a state governmental body include communication access  
| realtime translation captioning.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 551, Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 551.025 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___551.025._ _____REALTIME CAPTIONING. A video broadcast,_  
| _including a video broadcast over the Internet, that is available to_  
| _the public of an open meeting of a governmental body described by_  
| _Section 551.001(3)(A) must include communication access realtime_  
| _translation captioning._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
