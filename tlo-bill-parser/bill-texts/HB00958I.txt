|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the release and inclusion of extraterritorial  
| jurisdiction by a municipality involving certain areas.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 42, Local Government Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 42.0253 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___42.0253._ _____RELEASE AND INCLUSION OF EXTRATERRITORIAL_  
| _JURISDICTION: CERTAIN AREAS. (a) This section applies only to an_  
| _area that is located in:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the extraterritorial jurisdiction of a_  
| _municipality with a population of 1.9 million or more; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____a county with a population of 550,000 or more that_  
| _is adjacent to a county with a population of 3.3 million or more._  
|  _(b)_ _____A majority of the registered voters in an area subject_  
| _to this section may petition the municipality to release the area_  
| _from the municipality's extraterritorial jurisdiction. The_  
| _petition must be filed with the secretary or clerk of the_  
| _municipality._  
|  _(c)_ _____Not later than the 10th day after the date the secretary_  
| _or clerk receives a petition under Subsection (b), the secretary or_  
| _clerk shall determine whether the petition is valid._ ___If the_  
| _petition is determined valid, the governing body of the_  
| _municipality shall immediately enter in the minutes or records of_  
| _the municipality an order releasing the area from the_  
| _municipality's_ ___extraterritorial jurisdiction._  
|  _(d)_ _____An area released under Subsection (c) becomes an_  
| _unincorporated area of the county._ ___A municipality may include the_  
| _area in the municipality's extraterritorial jurisdiction if:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the municipality is otherwise eligible to include_  
| _the area in its extraterritorial jurisdiction under this chapter;_  
| _and_  
|  _(2)_ _____the municipality receives a petition signed by a_  
| _majority of the registered voters of the area requesting inclusion._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
