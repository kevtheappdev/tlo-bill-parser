|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the waiver of certain surcharges assessed under the  
| driver responsibility program.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter D, Chapter 708, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 708.160 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___708.160._ _____WAIVER OF SURCHARGES FOR CERTAIN OFFENSES._  
| _(a)_ ___A person who receives notice of a surcharge under Section_  
| _708.151 as a result of one or more convictions may file a petition_  
| _with a convicting court for a waiver of the surcharge if:_  
|  _(1)_ _____each offense that was the basis for the surcharge_  
| _was punishable by a fine only; and_  
|  _(2) the petition includes:_  
|  _(A) a copy of the person's driving record;_  
|  _(B)_ _____a copy of the notice of surcharge received_  
| _under Section 708.151;_  
|  _(C)_ _____evidence that the person is unable to pay the_  
| _surcharge; and_  
|  _(D)_ _____any other supporting documentation that_  
| _would support a decision to grant the waiver._  
|  _(b)_ _____A court receiving a petition under this section shall_  
| _set a hearing not later than the 14th day after the date the court_  
| _receives the petition._ ___The court shall notify the department of the_  
| _date of the hearing._ ___At the hearing, the court may hear testimony_  
| _and consider:_  
|  _(1) the petitioner's ability to pay the surcharge;_  
|  _(2) the frequency of the underlying offenses;_  
|  _(3) the time and length of any license suspension; and_  
|  _(4)_ _____any other factors that would support a decision to_  
| _grant the waiver._  
|  _(c)_ _____The court may issue an order granting the waiver._ ___The_  
| _court shall notify the department of an order entered under this_  
| _subsection._  
|  _(d)_ _____Not later than the 30th day after the date the_  
| _department receives a court order under Subsection (c), the_  
| _department shall waive the surcharges as required by the order._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 708.160, Transportation Code, as added  
| by this Act, applies to any surcharge pending on the effective date  
| of this Act, regardless of whether the surcharge was imposed before  
| that date.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
