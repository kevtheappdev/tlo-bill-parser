|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to designating the first Tuesday in September as Texas  
| Voter Registration Day.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 662, Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 662.071 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___662.071._ _____TEXAS VOTER REGISTRATION DAY. The first_  
| _Tuesday in September of each year is Texas Voter Registration Day._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
