|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the issuance of specially marked driver's licenses and  
| personal identification certificates to emergency medical services  
| personnel.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter E, Chapter 521, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 521.1025 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___521.1025._ _____DESIGNATOR ON PERSONAL IDENTIFICATION_  
| _CERTIFICATE ISSUED TO EMERGENCY MEDICAL SERVICES PERSONNEL. (a)_  
| _In this section, "emergency medical services personnel" has the_  
| _meaning assigned by Section 773.003, Health and Safety Code._  
|  _(b)_ _____The department shall include the designation "EMERGENCY_  
| _MEDICAL" on a personal identification certificate issued to_  
| _emergency medical services personnel in an available space either_  
| _on the face of the personal identification certificate or on the_  
| _reverse side of the personal identification certificate if the_  
| _emergency medical services personnel:_  
|  _(1) requests the designation; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____provides proof sufficient to the department that_  
| _the person is certified as emergency medical services personnel_  
| _under Chapter 773, Health and Safety Code._  
|  _(c)_ _____A person who renews a personal identification_  
| _certificate described by this section shall provide proof_  
| _sufficient to the department that the person is certified as_  
| _emergency medical services personnel under Chapter 773, Health and_  
| _Safety Code._  
|  _(d)_ _____A person who holds a personal identification_  
| _certificate described by this section shall surrender the_  
| _certificate to the department in a manner provided by department_  
| _rule if the person's certification as emergency medical services_  
| _personnel expires or is revoked or suspended as provided by Chapter_  
| _773, Health and Safety Code._  
|  SECTION 2. Subchapter F, Chapter 521, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 521.1236 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___521.1236._ _____DESIGNATOR ON LICENSE ISSUED TO EMERGENCY_  
| _MEDICAL SERVICES PERSONNEL. (a) In this section, "emergency_  
| _medical services personnel" has the meaning assigned by Section_  
| _773.003, Health and Safety Code._  
|  _(b)_ _____The department shall include the designation "EMERGENCY_  
| _MEDICAL" on a driver's license issued to emergency medical services_  
| _personnel in an available space either on the face of the driver's_  
| _license or on the reverse side of the driver's license if the_  
| _emergency medical services personnel:_  
|  _(1) requests the designation; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____provides proof sufficient to the department that_  
| _the person is certified as emergency medical services personnel_  
| _under Chapter 773, Health and Safety Code._  
|  _(c)_ _____A person who renews a driver's license described by this_  
| _section shall provide proof sufficient to the department that the_  
| _person is certified as emergency medical services personnel under_  
| _Chapter 773, Health and Safety Code._  
|  _(d)_ _____A person who holds a driver's license described by this_  
| _section shall surrender the license to the department in a manner_  
| _provided by department rule if the person's certification as_  
| _emergency medical services personnel expires or is revoked or_  
| _suspended as provided by Chapter 773, Health and Safety Code._  
|  SECTION 3. As soon as practicable after the effective date  
| of this Act, the Department of Public Safety of the State of Texas  
| shall adopt the rules required by Sections 521.1025 and 521.1236,  
| Transportation Code, as added by this Act.  
|  SECTION 4. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
