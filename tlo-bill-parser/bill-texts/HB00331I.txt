|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the application of the open meetings law to the medical  
| advisory board.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter H, Chapter 12, Health and Safety  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 12.099 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___12.099._ _____OPEN MEETINGS. (a)_ ___All meetings of the_  
| _medical advisory board or a panel shall be conducted in accordance_  
| _with the open meetings law, Chapter 551, Government Code._  
|  _(b)_ _____This section and Chapter 551, Government Code, do not_  
| _require the medical advisory board or a panel to conduct an open_  
| _meeting to deliberate a record, a report, or testimony described by_  
| _Section 12.097(a)._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
