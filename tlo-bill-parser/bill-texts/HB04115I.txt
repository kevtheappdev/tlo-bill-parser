|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to certain suits filed against a governmental unit and an  
| employee of the governmental unit.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 101.106(e), Civil Practice and Remedies  
| Code, is amended to read as follows:  
|  (e) If a suit is filed _asserting a claim for which this_  
| _chapter waives sovereign or governmental immunity_ [ ~~under this~~  
|  ~~chapter~~ ] against both a governmental unit and any of its employees,  
| the _claim against the employee_ [ ~~employees~~ ] shall immediately be  
| dismissed on the filing of a motion by the governmental unit.  
|  SECTION 2. Section 101.106(e), Civil Practice and Remedies  
| Code, as amended by this Act, applies only to an action filed on or  
| after the effective date of this Act. An action that is filed  
| before the effective date of this Act is governed by the law  
| applicable to the action immediately before the effective date of  
| this Act, and that law is continued in effect for that purpose.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
