|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the duties of a hearing officer following a Health and  
| Human Services Commission fair hearing regarding the denial of  
| certain services.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 531, Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 531.0193 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___531.0193._ _____REQUIRED CONSULTATION WITH MEDICAL DIRECTOR_  
| _AFTER FAIR HEARING. The executive commissioner by rule shall_  
| _require that, after conducting a fair hearing and before making a_  
| _decision, a hearing officer consult with the medical director_  
| _described by Section 531.009 in determining the outcome of a_  
| _hearing regarding the denial of medically necessary services._  
|  SECTION 2. As soon as practicable after the effective date  
| of this Act, the executive commissioner of the Health and Human  
| Services Commission shall adopt rules required by Section 531.0193,  
| Government Code, as added by this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
