|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a requirement that the Railroad Commission of Texas  
| publish maps of pipeline evacuation zones.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 117.012, Natural Resources Code, is  
| amended by adding Subsection (j) to read as follows:  
|  _(j)_ _____The commission shall publish on its Internet website_  
| _maps of evacuation zones for emergencies relating to hazardous_  
| _liquid or carbon dioxide pipeline facilities._ ___The commission may_  
| _require pipeline facility operators to submit to the commission any_  
| _information needed for the purposes of this subsection._ ___Each_  
| _evacuation zone map must show:_  
|  _(1)_ _____for each pipeline facility, the associated high_  
| _consequence area and surrounding buffer zone;_  
|  _(2)_ _____the roads and bodies of water in the evacuation_  
| _zone;_  
|  _(3) a legend;_  
|  _(4) a linear scale for distance; and_  
|  _(5) a compass rose._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 121.2015, Utilities Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (d) to read as follows:  
|  _(d)_ _____The railroad commission shall publish on its Internet_  
| _website maps of evacuation zones for emergencies relating to gas_  
| _pipeline facilities._ ___The railroad commission may require pipeline_  
| _facility operators to submit to the commission any information_  
| _needed for the purposes of this subsection._ ___Each evacuation zone_  
| _map must show:_  
|  _(1)_ _____for each pipeline facility, the associated high_  
| _consequence area and surrounding buffer zone;_  
|  _(2)_ _____the roads and bodies of water in the evacuation_  
| _zone;_  
|  _(3) a legend;_  
|  _(4) a linear scale for distance; and_  
|  _(5) a compass rose._  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
