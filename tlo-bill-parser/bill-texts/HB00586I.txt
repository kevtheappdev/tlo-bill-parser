|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the annual state contribution to counties for certain  
| statutory probate court judge salaries.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 25.00211(a), Government Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  (a) Beginning on the first day of the state fiscal year, the  
| state shall annually compensate each county [ ~~that collects the~~  
|  ~~additional fees under Section 51.704~~ ] in an amount equal to _60_  
| _percent of the state salary of a district court judge in the county_  
| [ ~~$40,000~~ ] for each statutory probate court judge in the county _who:_  
|  _(1) does not engage in the private practice of law; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____presides over a court with at least the_  
| _jurisdiction provided by Section 25.0021_.  
|  SECTION 2. Section 25.00211(b), Government Code, is  
| repealed.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
