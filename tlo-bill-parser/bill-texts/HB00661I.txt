|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the release of deer by certain deer breeding facilities  
| to low-fenced acreage.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter L, Chapter 43, Parks and Wildlife  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 43.3635 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___43.3635._ _____RELEASE BY CERTAIN DEER BREEDING FACILITIES_  
| _TO LOW-FENCED ACREAGE. (a) A deer breeding facility with the_  
| _highest status transfer category as determined by commission rule_  
| _may release breeder deer to a low-fenced site that:_  
|  _(1)_ _____includes at least 5,000 acres of contiguous land;_  
| _and_  
|  _(2) is within 200 miles of the deer breeding facility._  
|  _(b)_ _____The commission shall adopt rules governing a release of_  
| _breeder deer under this section._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
