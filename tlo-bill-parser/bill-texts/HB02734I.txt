|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to an anatomical gift on behalf of a resident in a state  
| supported living center.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 555, Health and Safety  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 555.027 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___555.027._ _____ANATOMICAL GIFT. (a)_ ___The executive_  
| _commissioner by rule shall prescribe a form that a resident's_  
| _guardian may sign to authorize the center to make an anatomical gift_  
| _on behalf of a resident if the resident's guardian elects to make an_  
| _anatomical gift on behalf of the resident in accordance with_  
| _Chapter 692A and predeceases the resident._  
|  _(b)_ _____Subsection (a) does not preclude a successor guardian_  
| _from executing a document in accordance with Chapter 692A that_  
| _supersedes the form executed under that subsection._  
|  SECTION 2. Not later than December 1, 2019, the executive  
| commissioner of the Health and Human Services Commission shall  
| prescribe the form as required by Section 555.027, Health and  
| Safety Code, as added by this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
