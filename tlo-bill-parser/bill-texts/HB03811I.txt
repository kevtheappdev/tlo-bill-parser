|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the enforcement by certain local governmental entities  
| and campus police departments of state and federal laws governing  
| immigration.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 752.053(a), Government Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  (a) local entity or campus police department may not:  
|  (1) adopt  
| _or_ [ ~~,~~ ] enforce[ ~~, or endorse~~ ] a policy under  
| which the entity or department prohibits or materially limits the  
| enforcement of immigration laws;  
|  (2) as demonstrated by pattern or practice, prohibit  
| or materially limit the enforcement of immigration laws; or  
|  (3) for an entity that is a law enforcement agency or  
| for a department, as demonstrated by pattern or practice,  
| intentionally violate Article 2.251, Code of Criminal Procedure.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
