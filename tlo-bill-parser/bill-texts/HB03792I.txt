|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to clerks of justice courts and deputy constables.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 27.056(a), Government Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  (a) Each justice of the peace may designate one or more  
| persons to serve as clerk of the justice court.  _A clerk of the_  
| _justice court serves at the pleasure of the justice of the peace._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 86.011, Local Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Subsection (a-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(a-1)_ _____An elected constable may designate a person serving as_  
| _a deputy constable as the chief deputy constable._ ___The chief deputy_  
| _constable serves at the pleasure of the elected constable._  
|  SECTION 3. Section 158.013(b), Local Government Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (b) This subchapter does not apply to:  
|  (1) assistant district attorneys, investigators, or  
| other employees of a district or criminal district attorney, except  
| as provided by Section 158.007;  
|  (2) the official shorthand reporter of a court; [ ~~or~~ ]  
|  (3) an elected or appointed officer under the  
| constitution _;_  
|  _(4)_ _____a chief deputy constable appointed under Section_  
| _86.011; or_  
|  _(5)_ _____a clerk of a justice of the peace appointed under_  
| _Section 27.056, Government Code_.  
|  SECTION 4. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
