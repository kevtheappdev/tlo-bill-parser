|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to prohibiting the arrest of a person for a violation of a  
| condition of community supervision based on certain drug test  
| results.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Article 42A.751, Code of Criminal Procedure, is  
| amended by adding Subsection (b-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(b-1)_ _____Notwithstanding Subsection (b), the judge may not_  
| _issue a warrant for a violation of a condition of community_  
| _supervision solely because the results of a drug test taken by the_  
| _defendant indicated that tetrahydrocannabinol or cannabidiol was_  
| _present in the body of the defendant._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
