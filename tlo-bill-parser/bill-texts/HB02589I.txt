|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to county consent for certain municipal annexations.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 43, Local Government Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 43.035 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___43.035._ _____COUNTY CONSENT REQUIRED FOR CERTAIN_  
| _ANNEXATIONS. A municipality with a population of more than 300,000_  
| _may not annex an area located in a county with a population of more_  
| _than 50,000 and less than 300,000 without the consent of that_  
| _county._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 43.035, Local Government Code, as added  
| by this Act, applies only to an annexation that is not final on or  
| after the effective date of this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
