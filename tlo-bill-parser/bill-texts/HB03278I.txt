|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the creation of the criminal offense of predatory  
| theft.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 31, Penal Code, is amended by adding  
| Section 31.20 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___31.20._ _____PREDATORY THEFT. (a)_ ___In this section,_
___"follow"_  
| _means any conduct that tends to show that a person pursued another,_  
| _including waiting for the person, tracking or trailing the person,_  
| _and maintaining visual contact with the person._  
|  _(b)_ _____A person commits an offense if, with the intent to_  
| _commit an offense under Section 29.02, 29.03, 30.04, or 31.03, the_  
| _person:_  
|  _(1) follows another person; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____engages in conduct constituting an offense under_  
| _Section 31.03 against the person followed._  
|  _(c)_ _____An offense under this section is a felony of the third_  
| _degree._  
|  _(d)_ _____If conduct that constitutes an offense under this_  
| _section also constitutes an offense under other law, the actor may_  
| _be prosecuted under this section, the other law, or both._  
|  SECTION 2. The change in law made by this Act applies only  
| to an offense committed on or after the effective date of this Act.  
| An offense committed before the effective date of this Act is  
| governed by the law in effect on the date the offense was committed,  
| and the former law is continued in effect for that purpose. For  
| purposes of this section, an offense was committed before the  
| effective date of this Act if any element of the offense occurred  
| before that date.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
