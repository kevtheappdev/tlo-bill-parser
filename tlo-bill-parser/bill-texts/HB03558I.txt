|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to capturing images of the illegal passing of a stationary  
| tow truck.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter D, Chapter 545, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 545.158 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___545.158._ _____CAPTURING IMAGES OF ILLEGAL PASSING OF_  
| _STATIONARY TOW TRUCK. (a) In this section, "tow truck" has the_  
| _meaning assigned by Section 545.157._  
|  _(b)_ _____A tow truck operator may install and operate a system_  
| _that is capable of taking photographic, video, or digital images of_  
| _vehicles that pass the tow truck in violation of Section 545.157._  
|  _(c)_ _____A law enforcement agency may use the evidence collected_  
| _under Subsection (b) to:_  
|  _(1)_ _____notify the registered owner of a vehicle that the_  
| _vehicle may have violated Section 545.157; or_  
|  _(2) enforce Section 545.157._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
