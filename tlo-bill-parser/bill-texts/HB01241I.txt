|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the content of a polling place location notice.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 1, Election Code, is amended by adding  
| Section 1.021 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___1.021._ _____NOTICE OF POLLING PLACE LOCATION. When this_  
| _code requires notice of a polling place location, the written_  
| _notice must state the building name, if any, and street address of_  
| _the polling place._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
