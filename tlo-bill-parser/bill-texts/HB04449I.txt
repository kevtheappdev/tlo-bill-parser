|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the applicability of certain electric energy storage  
| equipment requirements to municipally owned utilities and electric  
| cooperatives.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 35.152, Utilities Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (d) to read as follows:  
|  _(d)_ _____Subsection (b) does not require a municipally owned_  
| _utility or an electric cooperative that owns or operates electric_  
| _energy storage equipment or facilities described by Subsection (a)_  
| _to register as a power generation company under Section 39.351(a)._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
