|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to supreme court orders for conducting court proceedings  
| during a disaster.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 22.0035(b), Government Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  (b) Notwithstanding any other statute, the supreme court _by_  
| _order_ may modify or suspend procedures for the conduct of any court  
| proceeding affected by a disaster during the pendency of a disaster  
| declared by the governor.  An order _issued_ under this section  
| _extends until terminated by a subsequent order issued_ [ ~~may not~~  
|  ~~extend for more than 30 days from the date the order was signed~~  
|  ~~unless renewed~~ ] by the supreme court.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
