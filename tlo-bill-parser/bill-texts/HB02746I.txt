|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to manufacturer disclosure of cosmetic ingredients;  
| authorizing an administrative penalty.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter F, Chapter 431, Health and Safety  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 431.143 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___431.143._ _____MANUFACTURER DISCLOSURE. (a) A manufacturer_  
| _selling a cosmetic in this state shall disclose on the_  
| _manufacturer's Internet website:_  
|  _(1)_ _____a list of each ingredient in the cosmetic,_  
| _including a fragrance, flavor, and color additive, in order of_  
| _predominance except that the ingredients in the cosmetic with a_  
| _concentration of less than one percent may be listed in any order_  
| _for purposes of protecting a trade secret; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____the chemical abstract service number for each_  
| _listed ingredient._  
|  _(b)_ _____A manufacturer is not required to list the concentration_  
| _of each ingredient in a cosmetic under Subsection (a)._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 431.054(a), Health and Safety Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (a) The department may assess an administrative penalty  
| against a person who violates Subchapter B _, Section 431.143,_ or an  
| order adopted or registration issued under this chapter.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
