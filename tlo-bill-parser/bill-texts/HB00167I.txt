|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the participation of certain students in University  
| Interscholastic League sponsored activities.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter D, Chapter 33, Education Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 33.0833 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___33.0833._ _____ACCESS TO UNIVERSITY INTERSCHOLASTIC LEAGUE_  
| _SPONSORED ACTIVITIES FOR STUDENTS UNDER SUPERVISION OF TEXAS_  
| _JUVENILE JUSTICE DEPARTMENT. (a)_ ___The University Interscholastic_  
| _League may provide students receiving educational services under_  
| _the supervision of the Texas Juvenile Justice Department with the_  
| _opportunity to participate in activities sponsored by the league in_  
| _the same manner that the league provides the opportunity to_  
| _participate to students enrolled in public schools._ ___Participation_  
| _of a public school in an activity in which students receiving_  
| _educational services under the supervision of the department_  
| _participate is optional._  
|  _(b)_ _____Before providing students described by Subsection (a)_  
| _with the opportunity to participate in activities described by that_  
| _subsection, the University Interscholastic League must:_  
|  _(1)_ _____enter into a memorandum of understanding with the_  
| _Texas Juvenile Justice Department regarding the policies_  
| _governing:_  
|  _(A)_ _____the conditions of eligibility for students_  
| _under the supervision of the department in activities sponsored by_  
| _the league, including the:_  
|  _(i)_ _____ages of students eligible to_  
| _participate;_  
|  _(ii)_ _____academic performance requirements for_  
| _students; and_  
|  _(iii) standards of behavior for students;_  
|  _(B)_ _____the appropriate league in which students_  
| _under the supervision of the department will participate; and_  
|  _(C)_ _____the duties of the department regarding other_  
| _policies of the league, including fees, insurance, and_  
| _transportation; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____adopt rules and administrative guidelines to_  
| _implement this section, including procedures allowing for the_  
| _optional participation of public schools in the activities provided_  
| _to students under the supervision of the department under this_  
| _section._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
