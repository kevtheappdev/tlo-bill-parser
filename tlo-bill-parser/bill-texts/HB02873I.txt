|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the room placement of individuals with Alzheimer's  
| disease or dementia in health care facilities and correctional  
| facilities.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 222, Health and Safety Code, is amended  
| by adding Subchapter D to read as follows:  
| _SUBCHAPTER D. REGULATION OF HEALTH CARE FACILITIES SERVICING_  
| _RESIDENTS WITH ALZHEIMER'S DISEASE OR DEMENTIA_  
|  _Sec. 222.101. DEFINITIONS. In this subchapter:_  
|  _(1)_ _____"Commission"_ ___means the Health and Human Services_  
| _Commission._  
|  _(2)_ _____"Executive commissioner" means the executive_  
| _commissioner of the commission._  
|  _(3)_ _____"Health care facility" means a health care_  
| _facility licensed by the commission._  
|  _(4)_ _____"Resident" means an individual who resides in a_  
| _health care facility._  
|  _Sec._ ___222.102._ _____PLACEMENT OF CERTAIN RESIDENTS._ ___The_  
| _executive commissioner by rule shall prohibit a health care_  
| _facility from requiring a resident with Alzheimer's disease or_  
| _dementia to reside in the same room as a resident with mental_  
| _illness and a history of violence._  
|  _Sec._ ___222.103._ _____DISCIPLINARY ACTION._ _____A health care facility_  
| _that violates this subchapter or a rule adopted under this_  
| _subchapter is subject to disciplinary action by the commission as_  
| _if the facility violated an applicable licensing law._  
|  SECTION 2. Chapter 493, Government Code, is amended by  
| adding Section 493.032 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___493.032._ _____PLACEMENT OF PERSONS WITH ALZHEIMER'S DISEASE_  
| _OR DEMENTIA. The department shall ensure that a person with_  
| _Alzheimer's disease or dementia who is in the custody of a jail or_  
| _other correctional facility operated by or under contract with the_  
| _department does not reside in the same room with an individual with_  
| _mental illness and a history of violence._  
|  SECTION 3. Chapter 511, Government Code, is amended by  
| adding Section 511.022 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___511.022._ _____PLACEMENT OF INMATES WITH ALZHEIMER'S DISEASE_  
| _OR DEMENTIA. The commission shall adopt rules prohibiting a_  
| _correctional facility from requiring an inmate with Alzheimer's_  
| _disease or dementia to reside in the same room as an inmate with_  
| _mental illness and a history of violence._  
|  SECTION 4. As soon as practicable after the effective date  
| of this Act, the executive commissioner of the Health and Human  
| Services Commission and the Commission on Jail Standards shall  
| adopt rules necessary to implement the changes in law made by this  
| Act.  
|  SECTION 5. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
