|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a provision of a contract that conflicts with state law.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. The heading to Title 9, Business & Commerce Code,  
| is amended to read as follows:  
| TITLE 9. APPLICABILITY OF LAW TO COMMERCIAL TRANSACTIONS _AND OTHER_  
| _CONTRACTS_  
|  SECTION 2. Title 9, Business & Commerce Code, is amended by  
| adding Chapter 275 to read as follows:  
| _CHAPTER 275. STATE LAW CONTROLS OVER CONFLICTING CONTRACT TERMS_  
|  _Sec._ ___275.001._ _____CONFLICT WITH STATE LAW. (a)_ ___To the extent a_  
| _term or condition of a contract is in conflict with a law of this_  
| _state, the law of this state controls._  
|  _(b)_ _____Subsection (a) may not be construed to authorize the_  
| _impairment of any contract in violation of the Texas Constitution_  
| _or the United States Constitution._  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
