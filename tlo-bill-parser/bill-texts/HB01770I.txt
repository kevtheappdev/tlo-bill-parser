|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the offense of passing certain vehicles on a highway.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 545.157(a), Transportation Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (a) This section applies only to the following vehicles:  
|  (1) a stationary authorized emergency vehicle using  
| visual signals that meet the requirements of Sections 547.305 and  
| 547.702;  
|  (2) a stationary tow truck using equipment authorized  
| by Section 547.305(d); [ ~~and~~ ]  
|  (3) a Texas Department of Transportation vehicle not  
| separated from the roadway by a traffic control channelizing device  
| and using visual signals that comply with the standards and  
| specifications adopted under Section 547.105 _; and_  
|  _(4)_ _____a service vehicle used in the maintenance of an_  
| _electrical power line and using visual signals that comply with the_  
| _standards and specifications adopted under Section 547.105_.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
