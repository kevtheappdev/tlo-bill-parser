|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to health benefit coverage for chemical dependency.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 1368.004(a), Insurance Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  (a) A group health benefit plan shall provide coverage for  
| the necessary care and treatment of chemical dependency _, including_  
| _outpatient rehabilitation_.  
|  SECTION 2. Chapter 1368, Insurance Code, is amended by  
| adding Section 1368.009 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___1368.009._ _____PROHIBITED CONSEQUENCE OF CLAIMS. An_  
| _employer that provides group health benefit coverage subject to_  
| _this chapter may not terminate an employee solely based on the_  
| _employee filing a claim for the benefits._  
|  SECTION 3. This Act applies only to a health benefit plan  
| that is delivered, issued for delivery, or renewed on or after  
| January 1, 2020.  
|  SECTION 4. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
