|  SECTION 33. If a conflict exists between this Act and  
| another Act of the 86th Legislature, Regular Session, 2019, that  
| extends the sunset date of a governmental entity, the provisions of  
| the other Act prevail without regard to the relative dates of  
| enactment.  
|  SECTION 34. This Act takes effect immediately if it  
| receives a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each  
| house, as provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution.  
| If this Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate  
| effect, this Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
