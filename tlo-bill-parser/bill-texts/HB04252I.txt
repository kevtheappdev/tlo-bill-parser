|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the protection of the rights of conscience for child  
| welfare services providers.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 45, Human Resources Code, is repealed.  
|  SECTION 2. The repeal of Chapter 45, Human Resources Code,  
| by this Act does not affect a cause of action or defense created by  
| that chapter that arises as a result of conduct occurring before the  
| effective date of this Act.  That cause of action or defense is  
| governed by the law in effect immediately before the effective date  
| of this Act, and that law is continued in effect for that purpose.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
