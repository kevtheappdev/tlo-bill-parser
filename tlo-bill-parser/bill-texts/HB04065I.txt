|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to tenant liability insurance.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter I, Chapter 92, Property Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 92.3516 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___92.3516._ _____REQUIREMENT OF TENANT LIABILITY INSURANCE._ ___A_  
| _landlord shall require a tenant to obtain or maintain during the_  
| _lease term liability insurance in an amount not less than $100,000_  
| _for each occurrence of damage to the landlord's property caused by_  
| _the tenant as a condition of the tenant's lease with the landlord._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 92.3516, Property Code, as added by this  
| Act, applies only to a lease entered into or renewed on or after the  
| effective date of this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
