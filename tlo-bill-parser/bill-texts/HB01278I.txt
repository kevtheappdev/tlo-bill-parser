|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to services provided by freestanding emergency medical  
| care facilities.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 254.001(5), Health and Safety Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (5) "Freestanding emergency medical care facility"  
| means a facility, structurally separate and distinct from a  
| hospital, that receives an individual and provides _:_  
|  _(A)_ emergency care, as defined by Subdivision  
| (2) _; and_  
|  _(B) outpatient acute care services_.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
