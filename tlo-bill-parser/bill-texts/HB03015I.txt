|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to educational requirements for inmates who are younger  
| than 18 years of age.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 493, Government Code, is amended by  
| adding Section 493.032 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___493.032._ _____EDUCATIONAL REQUIREMENTS FOR CERTAIN_  
| _INMATES. (a) The department shall provide a course of instruction_  
| _that includes the required curriculum provided by Section 28.002,_  
| _Education Code, to each inmate who is younger than 18 years of age_  
| _unless providing the instruction poses a significant safety threat_  
| _to instructors. The Texas Education Agency shall collaborate with_  
| _the department in providing necessary materials for instruction._  
|  _(b)_ _____The department may enter into an agreement with the_  
| _Windham School District for purposes of complying with this_  
| _section._  
|  _(c)_ _____The board may adopt rules as necessary to implement this_  
| _section._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
