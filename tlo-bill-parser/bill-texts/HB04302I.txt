|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to regulations authorizing special education hearing  
| officer subpoenas.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 2001.089, Texas Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Subsection (b) to read as follows:  
|  _(b)_ _____No subpoena may be issued under this section for video_  
| _or audio surveillance maintained pursuant to Section 29.022, Texas_  
| _Education Code, unless it meets the requirements for release under_  
| _that section._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
