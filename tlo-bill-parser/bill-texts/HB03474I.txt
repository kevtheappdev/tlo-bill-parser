|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to increasing the fee for a permit authorizing the  
| movement of certain vehicles transporting an intermodal shipping  
| container.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 623.404(a), Transportation Code, as  
| added by Chapter 108 (S.B. 1524), Acts of the 85th Legislature,  
| Regular Session, 2017, is amended to read as follows:  
|  (a) An application for a permit under Section 623.402(a) or  
| (b) must be accompanied by a permit fee of _$8,000_ [ ~~$6,000~~ ], of  
| which:  
|  (1) 50 percent shall be deposited to the credit of the  
| state highway fund;  
|  (2) 30 percent shall be equally divided among and  
| distributed to each county designated in the permit application;  
|  (3) 16 percent shall be equally divided among and  
| distributed to each municipality designated in the permit  
| application; and  
|  (4) 4 percent shall be deposited to the credit of the  
| Texas Department of Motor Vehicles fund.  
|  SECTION 2. The change in law made by this Act applies only  
| to an application for a permit that is filed with the Texas  
| Department of Motor Vehicles on or after the effective date of this  
| Act. An application for a permit filed before the effective date of  
| this Act is governed by the law in effect on the date of filing, and  
| that law is continued in effect for that purpose.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
