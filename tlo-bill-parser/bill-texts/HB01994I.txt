|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the transfer of certain real property in the city of  
| Laredo from The University of Texas System to The Texas A&M  
| University System and to the uses of that property.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. The following real property in the city of Laredo  
| is transferred from The University of Texas System to The Texas A&M  
| University System:  
|  (1) Blocks 22 and 30, Laredo Airport Subdivision, Webb  
| County, Texas; and  
|  (2) Lot 3, Block 23, Laredo Airport Subdivision, Webb  
| County, Texas.  
|  SECTION 2. Subchapter F, Chapter 87, Education Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 87.506 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___87.506._ _____LEASE OF CERTAIN PROPERTY TO THE UNIVERSITY OF_  
| _TEXAS SYSTEM. (a) This section applies to the property located in_  
| _the city of Laredo that was transferred to The Texas A &M University _  
| _System from The University of Texas System by an act of the 86th_  
| _Legislature, Regular Session, 2019._  
|  _(b)_ _____Subject to the provisions of this section, the board of_  
| _regents of The Texas A &M University System may use the property for _  
| _any purposes related to Texas A &M International University or may _  
| _lease any portion of the property that is not needed for those_  
| _purposes._  
|  _(c)_ _____On request of The University of Texas System, the board_  
| _of regents of The Texas A &M University System shall lease at least a _  
| _portion of the property to The University of Texas System for use by_  
| _that system for the campus extension of The University of Texas_  
| _Health Science Center at San Antonio in the city of Laredo_  
| _authorized by Subchapter M, Chapter 74. The portion of the property_  
| _leased under this section may not be less than the portion of the_  
| _property in active use for that campus extension in the 2018-2019_  
| _academic year unless The University of Texas System consents to_  
| _lease a smaller portion. The lease payments charged may not exceed_  
| _the total amount of direct and indirect costs incurred by The Texas_  
| _A &M ____University System to maintain the portion of the property being_  
| _leased._  
|  _(d)_ _____The requirement to lease at least a portion of the_  
| _property under Subsection (c) expires if The University of Texas_  
| _System ceases to use the property for purposes of the campus_  
| _extension for one or more academic years or notifies The Texas A &M _  
| _University System that it no longer elects to lease the property for_  
| _that purpose._  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect January 1, 2020.
