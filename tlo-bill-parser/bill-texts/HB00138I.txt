|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a distance learning program at The University of Texas  
| at Austin School of Law.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 67, Education Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 67.28 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___67.28._ _____DISTANCE LEARNING PROGRAM AT LAW SCHOOL. (a)_  
| _The board shall establish a program that allows a student admitted_  
| _to The University of Texas School of Law to participate in the_  
| _student's first year of classes remotely from a facility at The_  
| _University of Texas Rio Grande Valley._  
|  _(b)_ _____The program must be administered in a manner that is_  
| _consistent with the accreditation requirements for the law school._  
|  _(c)_ _____The board shall ensure that at least five students are_  
| _allowed to participate in the program during each academic year._  
| _The law school may allow more than five students to participate in_  
| _the program during each academic year._  
|  SECTION 2. The board of regents of The University of Texas  
| System shall ensure that the distance learning program under  
| Section 67.28, Education Code, as added by this Act, begins not  
| later than the 2020 fall semester.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
