|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to fees for services by the offices of the sheriff and  
| constables.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 118.131(h), Local Government Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (h) If the commissioners court does not set fees under this  
| section, the fees for services by the offices of the sheriff and  
| constables are those fees provided by law in effect _for the_  
| _preceding fiscal year_ [ ~~on August 31, 1981~~ ].  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
