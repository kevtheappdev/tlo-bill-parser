|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the definition of a nonborder colonia.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 15.001(12), Water Code, is amended to  
| read as follows:  
|  (12) "Nonborder colonia" means a residential  
| community:  
|  (A) located in an unincorporated area of a  
| county _:_  
|  _(i_  
| _)_ all parts of which are at least 150  
| miles from the international border of this state; _or_  
|  _(ii)_ _____has a population of 425,000 or more, is_  
| _adjacent to a county with a population of 3.3 million or more, and_  
| _contains a portion of the San Jacinto River;_  
|  (B) in which water or wastewater services are  
| inadequate to meet minimal needs of residential users as defined by  
| board rules;  
|  (C) in which the average household income is less  
| than the average household income for the county in which the  
| community is located; and  
|  (D) that consists of 11 or more dwellings that  
| are located in close proximity to each other in an area that may be  
| described as a community or neighborhood.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
