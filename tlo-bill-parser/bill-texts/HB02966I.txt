|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to facilities and services for the care of the elderly or  
| persons with disabilities in certain municipal hospital  
| authorities, hospital districts, or other authorities.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Sections 262.034(e), (f), and (g), Health and  
| Safety Code, are amended to read as follows:  
|  (e) This section applies only to an authority _:_  
|  _(1)_ that owns or operates a hospital licensed under  
| Chapter 241 and that is located in:  
|  _(A)_ [ ~~(1)~~ ] a county with a population of 225,000  
| or less;  
|  _(B)_ [ ~~(2)~~ ] those portions of extended  
| municipalities that the federal census bureau has determined to be  
| rural;  
|  _(C)_ [ ~~(3)~~ ] an area that is not delineated as an  
| urbanized area by the federal census bureau; or  
|  _(D)_ [ ~~(4)~~ ] a municipality with a population of  
| less than 12,000 and a county with a population of 2.5 million or  
| more at the time the authority begins operating a facility or  
| providing a service described by Subsection (a) _; or_  
|  _(2)_ _____that owns or operates a facility described by_  
| _Subsection (a) that participates in the Quality Incentive Payment_  
| _Program (QIPP) or a successor Medicaid supplemental payment_  
| _program_.  
|  (f) This section does not authorize _an authority described_  
| _by Subsection (e)(1)_ [ ~~the authority~~ ] to issue revenue bonds or  
| other notes in accordance with this chapter to construct, acquire,  
| own, enlarge, improve, furnish, or equip a facility or service  
| listed in Subsection (a) if a private provider of the facility or  
| service is available and accessible in the service area of the  
| authority.  
|  (g) An authority described by Subsection _(e)(1)(D)_ [ ~~(e)(4)~~ ]  
| may not own or operate more than 50 licensed nursing home beds under  
| this section and is not subject to Subsection (f).  
|  SECTION 2. Sections 285.101(a) and (d), Health and Safety  
| Code, are amended to read as follows:  
|  (a) This subchapter applies only to a hospital, hospital  
| district, or authority created and operated under Article IX, Texas  
| Constitution, under a special law, or under this title _:_  
|  _(1)_ that is located in:  
|  _(A)_ [ ~~(1)~~ ] a county with a population of 35,000 or  
| less;  
|  _(B)_ [ ~~(2)~~ ] those portions of extended  
| municipalities that the federal census bureau has determined to be  
| rural; or  
|  _(C)_ [ ~~(3)~~ ] an area that is not delineated as an  
| urbanized area by the federal census bureau _; or_  
|  _(2)_ _____that owns or operates a facility described by_  
| _Subsection (b) that participates in the Quality Incentive Payment_  
| _Program (QIPP) or a successor Medicaid supplemental payment_  
| _program_.  
|  (d) This section does not authorize a hospital, hospital  
| district, or authority _described by Subsection (a)(1)_ to issue  
| revenue bonds or other notes in accordance with this chapter to  
| construct, acquire, own, enlarge, improve, furnish, or equip a  
| facility or service listed in Subsection (b)(1) if a private  
| provider of the facility or service is available and accessible in  
| the service area of the hospital, hospital district, or authority.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
