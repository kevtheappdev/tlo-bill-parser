|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the authority of certain counties to acquire a  
| right-of-way in accordance with the county's master drainage plan.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 561, Local Government Code, is amended  
| by adding Section 561.0075 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___561.0075._ _____AUTHORITY TO ACQUIRE RIGHT-OF-WAY REQUIRED_  
| _BY MASTER DRAINAGE PLAN IN CERTAIN COUNTIES._ ___(a)_ ___This section_  
| _applies only to a county with a population of 3.3 million or more._  
|  _(b)_ _____After notice of a master drainage plan is published in a_  
| _newspaper of general circulation in the county_  
| _, or on a county_  
| _website, the commissioners court of a county may acquire a_  
| _right-of-way on a strip of land that is undeveloped or part of a new_  
| _site development or redevelopment plan if the acquisition is_  
| _required by the county's master drainage plan._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
