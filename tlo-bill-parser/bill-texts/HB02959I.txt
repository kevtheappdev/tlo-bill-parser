|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the authority of certain counties to impose a hotel  
| occupancy tax.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 352.002, Tax Code, is amended by adding  
| Subsection (s) to read as follows:  
|  _(s)_ _____The commissioners court of a county with a population of_  
| _less than 17,000 that borders the Red River and in which at least_  
| _two United States highways cross into Oklahoma may impose a tax as_  
| _provided by Subsection (a)._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
