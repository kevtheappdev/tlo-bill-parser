|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to sexual harassment prevention training and information  
| for employees.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 21, Labor Code, is amended  
| by adding Section 21.013 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___21.013._ _____SEXUAL HARASSMENT PREVENTION TRAINING AND_  
| _INFORMATION. (a)_ ___In this section, the term "supervisor"_ ___includes a_  
| _person with the authority to direct an employee's daily activities_  
| _while at work, regardless of whether the person has the authority to_  
| _hire, fire, or take other tangible employment action with respect_  
| _to the employee._  
|  _(b)_ _____At least once every two years, an employer other than a_  
| _state agency shall provide to each employee of the employer at_  
| _least:_  
|  _(1)_ _____two hours of sexual harassment prevention_  
| _training, if the employee is a supervisor; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____one hour of sexual harassment prevention training,_  
| _if the employee is not a supervisor._  
|  _(c)_ _____The commission shall develop or obtain, and make_  
| _available on its Internet website, one-hour and two-hour sexual_  
| _harassment prevention training videos that may be used by employers_  
| _to provide the training required by this section._  
|  _(d)_ _____The commission shall develop and make available on its_  
| _Internet website for use by employers informational posters and_  
| _fact sheets displaying information regarding the prevention of_  
| _sexual harassment._  
|  _(e)_ _____The commission may adopt rules as necessary for the_  
| _administration of this section._  
|  SECTION 2. Not later than December 1, 2019, the Texas  
| Workforce Commission shall post on its Internet website the videos,  
| posters, and fact sheets required by Section 21.013, Labor Code, as  
| added by this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
