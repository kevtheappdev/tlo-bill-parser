|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the adoption of instructional materials for the  
| technology applications curriculum in public schools.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 31.023, Education Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (c) to read as follows:  
|  _(c)_ _____In adopting the list of instructional materials for the_  
| _technology applications curriculum, the State Board of Education_  
| _must include instructional materials that cover the essential_  
| _knowledge and skills for the digital citizenship strand of the_  
| _curriculum for each grade level in which digital citizenship is_  
| _required to be covered._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 31.023(c), Education Code, as added by  
| this Act, applies only to an instructional material list adopted on  
| or after the effective date of this Act. An instructional material  
| list adopted before the effective date of this Act is governed by  
| the law in effect when the instructional material list was adopted,  
| and the former law is continued in effect for that purpose.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
