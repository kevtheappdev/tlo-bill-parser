|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to terminating participation in the Texas Emergency  
| Services Retirement System.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 862.001, Government Code, is amended to  
| read as follows:  
|  Sec. 862.001. PARTICIPATION BY DEPARTMENT. (a)  The  
| governing body of a department that performs emergency services  
| may, in the manner provided for taking official action by the body,  
| elect to participate in the pension system.  A governing body shall  
| notify the executive director as soon as practicable of an election  
| made under this subsection.  [ ~~Except as provided by Subsection (b),~~  
|  ~~an election to participate under this subsection is irrevocable.~~ ]  
|  (b) _Except as provided by Subsection (c), the_ [ ~~The~~ ]  
| governing body of a department that makes an election under  
| Subsection (a) may _not_ terminate participation in the pension  
| system [ ~~not later than the fifth anniversary of the date of the~~  
|  ~~election to participate, except that a department that begins~~  
|  ~~participation after September 1, 2005, may not terminate that~~  
|  ~~participation~~ ].  
|  _(c)_ _____The governing body of a department that makes an_  
| _election under Subsection (a) may terminate participation in the_  
| _pension system if:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the department consists of both volunteer_  
| _firefighters and at least six full-time firefighters;_  
|  _(2)_ _____the full-time firefighters described by_  
| _Subdivision (1) are employees of a home-rule municipality:_  
|  _(A) governed by the same governing body; and_  
|  _(B) of which the department is a part; and_  
|  _(3)_ _____the governing body elects to provide retirement_  
| _benefits to the volunteer firefighters described by Subdivision (1)_  
| _through participation in an alternative retirement system._  
|  _(d)_ _____If a governing body of a department elects to terminate_  
| _participation in the pension system under Subsection (c), the_  
| _department forfeits all contributions to the system necessary to_  
| _pay the benefits of vested members._  
|  _(e)_ _____The state board may adopt rules necessary to implement_  
| _this section._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
