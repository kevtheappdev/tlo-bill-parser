|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to toll collection and enforcement by private participants  
| in comprehensive development agreements with the Texas Department  
| of Transportation.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 223.208, Transportation Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (i) to read as follows:  
|  _(i)_ _____Sections 228.0545, 228.0546, and 228.0547 do not apply_  
|  
| _to toll collection by a private participant under a comprehensive_  
| _development agreement entered into with the department before_  
| _September 1, 2017._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
