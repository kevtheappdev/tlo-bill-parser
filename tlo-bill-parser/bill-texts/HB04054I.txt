|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a landowner petition to partition and transfer a  
| geographic portion of a service area of an electric cooperative.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 39, Utilities Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 39.056 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___39.056._ _____LANDOWNER PETITION TO TRANSFER TERRITORY TO_  
| _COMPETITIVE RETAIL MARKET. (a)_ ___The owner of a parcel of land part_  
| _of which is in an area open to competition in ERCOT and part of which_  
| _is in the certificated service area of an electric cooperative_  
| _created under Chapter 161 may petition the commission to:_  
|  _(1)_ _____partition the part of the land that is subject to_  
| _the electric cooperative's certificate of convenience and_  
| _necessity;_  
|  _(2)_ _____transfer the land described by Subdivision (1) to_  
| _the certificated service area of a transmission and distribution_  
| _utility that serves the part of the land that is in the area open to_  
| _competition; and_  
|  _(3)_ _____amend accordingly the certificate of convenience_  
| _and necessity of the:_  
|  _(A) electric cooperative; and_  
|  _(B) transmission and distribution utility._  
|  _(b)_ _____The petition must provide the commission with an_  
| _accurate description of the land as necessary to facilitate the_  
| _requested action._  
|  _(c)_ _____The commission shall hold a hearing on the petition. If_  
| _the commission determines the partition and transfer of territory_  
| _is in the best interest of ratepayers, the commission shall approve_  
| _the partition and conduct a proceeding to effectuate the transfer._  
|  _(d)_ _____Through the proceeding, the commission shall determine_  
| _the net book value of all of the cooperative's electric_  
| _distribution assets attributable to the service area that is_  
| _proposed to be transferred._  
|  _(e)_ _____Not later than the 90th day after the date the_  
| _commission determines the value of the cooperative's electric_  
| _distribution assets attributable to the transferred service area,_  
| _the transmission and distribution utility shall compensate the_  
| _cooperative for the assets as directed by the commission._  
|  _(f)_ _____The commission shall amend or issue certificates of_  
| _convenience and necessity to implement the transfer of the service_  
| _area._  
|  _(g)_ _____The commission shall adopt rules to implement this_  
| _section._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
