|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the issuance of Register to Vote specialty license  
| plates.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter G, Chapter 504, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 504.671 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___504.671._ _____REGISTER TO VOTE LICENSE PLATES. (a) The_  
| _department shall issue specialty license plates that include the_  
| _words "Register to Vote."_  
|  _(b)_ _____After deduction of the department's administrative_  
| _costs, the remainder of the fee for issuance of the license plates_  
| _shall be deposited to the credit of an account created by the_  
| _comptroller in the manner provided by Section 504.6012(b). Money_  
| _deposited to that account may be used only by the secretary of state_  
| _for Project V.O.T.E. or a successor voter education program_  
| _administered by the office of the secretary of state._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
