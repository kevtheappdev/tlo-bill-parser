|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to notice of the health of children attending a child-care  
| facility.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 42.043, Human Resources Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (g) to read as follows:  
|  _(g)_ _____A child-care facility may provide notice on the_  
| _percentage of children attending the facility who are immunized in_  
| _accordance with Subchapter A, Chapter 161, Health and Safety Code._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
