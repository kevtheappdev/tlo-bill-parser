|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a study on migrant labor housing facilities.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. STUDY REGARDING MIGRANT LABOR HOUSING  
| FACILITIES. (a) The Texas Department of Housing and Community  
| Affairs shall conduct a study evaluating the quantity, quality, and  
| availability of, and the needs associated with, migrant labor  
| housing facilities as defined by Section 2306.921, Government Code,  
| in this state.  
|  (b) Not later than September 1, 2020, the Texas Department  
| of Housing and Community Affairs shall submit a written report to  
| the legislature containing:  
|  (1) the findings of the study conducted under  
| Subsection (a) of this section;  
|  (2) recommendations for appropriate actions based on  
| the findings of the study, including any necessary legislative  
| changes; and  
|  (3) a timeline for implementing the recommendations  
| made under Subdivision (2) of this subsection.  
|  SECTION 2. EFFECTIVE DATE. This Act takes effect September  
| 1, 2019.
