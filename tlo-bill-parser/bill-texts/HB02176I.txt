|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the authority of the Lavaca Hospital District to lease  
| district property.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 1051.107(b), Special District Local Laws  
| Code, is amended to read as follows:  
|  (b) The board may lease all or part of the district's _land,_  
| buildings _,_ and [ ~~other~~ ] facilities on terms considered to be in the  
| best interest of the district's inhabitants. The term of the lease  
| may not exceed _50_ [ ~~25~~ ] years.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
