|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the purchase of necessary real property for the  
| construction of a high-speed rail project.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 112, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 112.0571 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___112.0571._ _____CONSTRUCTION OF HIGH-SPEED RAIL. (a) In_  
| _this section, "high-speed rail" has the meaning assigned by Section_  
| _112.201._  
|  _(b)_ _____A private entity may not begin construction of a_  
| _high-speed rail project unless the department has determined that_  
| _the entity has acquired all real property necessary for the_  
| _construction of the project._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
