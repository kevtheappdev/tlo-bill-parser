|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the amount of time an authority with whom an application  
| for a place on the ballot is filed has to review the application.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 141.032, Election Code, is amended by  
| amending Subsection (b) and adding Subsection (b-1) to read as  
| follows:  
|  (b) Except as provided by _Subsections (b-1) and_  
| [ ~~Subsection~~ ] (c), the review shall be completed not later than the  
| fifth _business_ day after the date the application is received by the  
| authority.  
|  _(b-1)_ _____If an application is submitted fewer than five_  
| _business days before the applicable filing deadline, the review_  
| _shall be completed not later than the first Friday after the filing_  
| _deadline._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect January 1, 2020.
