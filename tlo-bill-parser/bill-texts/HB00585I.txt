|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the issuance of F-35 fighter jet specialty license  
| plates.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter G, Chapter 504, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 504.671 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___504.671._ _____F-35 FIGHTER JET LICENSE PLATES. (a) The_  
| _department shall issue specialty license plates that include the_  
| _image of an F-35 fighter jet and the words "The Sound of Freedom."_  
|  _(b)_ _____After deduction of the department's administrative_  
| _costs, the remainder of the fee for issuance of the license plates_  
| _shall be deposited to the credit of the general revenue fund._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
