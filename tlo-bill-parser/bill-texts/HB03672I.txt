|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the collection and reporting of data on services  
| provided by chemical dependency treatment facilities.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 464, Health & Safety Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 464.021 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___464.021._ _____SUBSTANCE USE DISORDER DATA COLLECTION. (a)_  
| _In this section, "commission" means the Health and Human Services_  
| _Commission._  
|  _(b)_ _____The commission, in cooperation with the department,_  
| _shall collect admission, discharge, and transfer data from a_  
| _treatment facility licensed under this chapter._ ___The commission_  
| _shall collect data necessary to complete the data set required by_  
| _the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration of_  
| _the United States Department of Health and Human Services and_  
| _maximize the overall state allotment of federal funds._  
|  
| _(c)_ _____The commission shall collect and report data described_  
| _by Subsection (b) in a manner that maintains confidentiality as_  
| _required by state and federal law._ ___The data is not public_  
| _information and is not subject to disclosure under Chapter 552,_  
| _Government Code._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
