|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the creation of an additional judicial district  
| composed of Brazos County and the duties of the district attorney  
| for the 85th Judicial District in Brazos County.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 24, Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 24.6007 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___24.6007._ _____463RD JUDICIAL DISTRICT (BRAZOS COUNTY). (a)_  
| _The 463rd Judicial District is composed of Brazos County._  
|  _(b)_ _____The 463rd District Court has concurrent jurisdiction_  
| _with the statutory county courts of Brazos County in misdemeanor_  
| _cases as well as the jurisdiction prescribed by general law for_  
| _district courts._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 43.143(a), Government Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  (a) The voters of the 85th Judicial District elect a  
| district attorney _who represents the state in the district courts_  
| _having jurisdiction in that county_.  
|  SECTION 3. The 463rd Judicial District is created on the  
| effective date of this Act.  
|  SECTION 4. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
