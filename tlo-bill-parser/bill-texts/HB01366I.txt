|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the method of returning a ballot to be voted by mail.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 86.006, Election Code, is amended by  
| amending Subsection (a-1) and adding Subsection (a-2) to read as  
| follows:  
|  (a-1) The voter may deliver a marked ballot in person to _:_  
|  _(1)_ the early voting clerk's office only while the  
| polls are open on election day _; or_  
|  _(2)_ _____a polling place while polls are open during the_  
| _early voting period or on election day_.  
|  _(a-2)_ A voter who delivers a marked ballot in person _under_  
| _Subsection (a-1)_ must present an acceptable form of identification  
| described by Section 63.0101.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
