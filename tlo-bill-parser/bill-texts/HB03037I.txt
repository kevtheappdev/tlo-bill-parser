|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the creation of a task force to study the segregation of  
| public school students with disabilities from other public school  
| students.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 29, Education Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 29.028 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___29.028._ _____TASK FORCE ON THE SEGREGATION OF STUDENTS WITH_  
| _DISABILITIES. (a) The agency shall establish a task force to study_  
| _the segregation of public school students with disabilities from_  
| _other public school students._  
|  _(b)_ _____The task force shall be composed of teachers, advocates,_  
| _parents, and other stakeholders selected by the agency._  
|  _(c)_ _____The agency shall designate a member of the task force to_  
| _serve as presiding officer._  
|  _(d)_ _____A vacancy on the task force shall be filled in the same_  
| _manner as the initial selection._  
|  _(e)_ _____A member of the task force may not receive compensation_  
| _for service on the task force but is entitled to reimbursement for_  
| _actual and necessary expenses incurred in performing task force_  
| _duties._  
|  _(f)_ _____The task force may accept gifts and grants from any_  
| _source to be used to carry out a function of the task force._  
|  _(g)_ _____The task force shall meet at least quarterly at the call_  
| _of the presiding officer and at other times as determined by the_  
| _presiding officer._  
|  _(h) The task force shall:_  
|  _(1)_ _____conduct an ongoing study of the effect of public_  
| _schools segregating students with disabilities from other_  
| _students._ ___The study must address:_  
|  _(A)_ _____student achievement on assessment_  
| _instruments adopted under Chapter 39;_  
|  _(B) student absences from class;_  
|  _(C)_ _____student violations of the student code of_  
| _conduct; and_  
|  _(D)_ _____other factors as determined relevant by the_  
| _agency; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____develop recommendations for schools to better_  
| _integrate students with disabilities into all school activities._  
|  _(i)_ _____Not less than once every two years, the task force shall_  
| _submit to the governor, the lieutenant governor, the speaker of the_  
| _house of representatives, and the standing committees of the_  
| _legislature having jurisdiction over the agency a report that_  
| _summarizes the study conducted and recommendations developed under_  
| _Subsection (h)._  
|  _(j)_ _____Chapter 2110, Government Code, does not apply to the_  
| _task force established under this section._  
|  _(k)_ _____The task force shall submit the first report required_  
| _under Subsection (i) not later than December 31, 2020._  
|  SECTION 2. Not later than December 1, 2019, the Texas  
| Education Agency shall appoint members to the task force  
| established by Section 29.028, Education Code, as added by this  
| Act, in the manner required by Subsection (b) of that section.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
