|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the filing of a bond by a private high-speed rail  
| operator with the Texas Department of Transportation.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 112, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 112.063 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___112.063._ _____BOND FOR RESTORATION OF REAL PROPERTY USED BY_  
| _PRIVATE HIGH-SPEED RAIL OPERATOR._ ___(a)_ ___In this section,_  
| _"high-speed rail service" means intercity passenger rail service_  
| _that is reasonably expected to reach speeds of at least 110 miles_  
| _per hour._  
|  _(b)_ _____Before a private entity begins operation of high-speed_  
| _rail service, including the extension of an existing service, the_  
| _entity must file with the department a bond in an amount determined_  
| _by the commission to be sufficient to restore real property used for_  
| _the service to its original condition if the service ceases_  
| _operation._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
