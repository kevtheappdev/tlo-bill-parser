|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the regulation of automated neighborhood electric  
| vehicles.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 545.452(b), Transportation Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (b) A political subdivision of this state or a state agency  
| may not impose a franchise or other regulation related to the  
| operation of _:_  
|  _(1)_ an automated motor vehicle _, including an automated_  
| _motor vehicle that is a neighborhood electric vehicle, as defined_  
| _by Section 551.301;_ or  
|  _(2) an_ automated driving system.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
