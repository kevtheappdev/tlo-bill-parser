|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a local agreement with the Department of State Health  
| Services to improve laboratory capabilities in border counties.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. The heading to Subchapter F, Chapter 12, Health  
| and Safety Code, is amended to read as follows:  
| SUBCHAPTER F. [ ~~OFFICE OF~~ ] BORDER HEALTH  
|  SECTION 2. Subchapter F, Chapter 12, Health and Safety  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 12.073 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___12.073._ _____LOCAL AGREEMENT. The department shall enter_  
| _into an agreement with an institution of higher education as_  
| _defined by Section 61.003, Education Code, located in Cameron or_  
| _Hidalgo County to increase the availability of laboratory services_  
| _in counties adjacent to an international border._  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
