|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the reimbursement of excess costs incurred by a  
| recipient of certain grants awarded by the Texas Department of  
| Transportation.  
|  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter I, Chapter 201, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 201.711 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___201.711._ _____REIMBURSEMENT OF EXCESS COSTS INCURRED BY_  
| _GRANT RECIPIENT. (a) This section applies only to a grant awarded_  
| _by the department for the construction of a transportation project_  
| _in a county or municipality with a population of less than 15,000._  
|  _(b)_ _____The department shall reimburse a grant recipient for_  
| _costs incurred by the recipient that exceed the amount of the grant_  
| _awarded by the department if the construction of the transportation_  
| _project is managed by the department._  
|  SECTION 2. The change in law made by this Act applies only  
| to a grant awarded by the Texas Department of Transportation on or  
| after the effective date of this Act. A grant awarded before the  
| effective date of this Act is governed by the law in effect on the  
| date the award was made, and the former law is continued in effect  
| for that purpose.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
