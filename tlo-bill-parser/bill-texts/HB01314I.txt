|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the release on bail of, and the reimbursement to a  
| county for certain costs of confining, a person charged with an  
| administrative violation of parole or mandatory supervision.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 508.254, Government Code, is amended by  
| amending Subsections (c), (d), (e), and (f) and adding Subsection  
| (d-1) to read as follows:  
|  (c) Except as provided by _Subsections_ [ ~~Subsection~~ ] (d) _and_  
| _(d-1)_ , pending a hearing on a charge of parole violation,  
| ineligible release, or violation of a condition of mandatory  
| supervision, a person returned to custody shall remain confined.  
|  (d) A magistrate of the county in which the person is held in  
| custody may release the person on bond pending the hearing if:  
|  (1) the person is arrested or held in custody only on a  
| charge that the person committed an administrative violation of  
| release;  
|  (2) [ ~~the division, in accordance with Subsection (e),~~  
|  ~~included notice on~~ ] the warrant for the person's arrest _did not_  
| _include a notice_ that the person is _ineligible_ [ ~~eligible~~ ] for  
| release on bond _as provided by Subsection (e)_ ; and  
|  (3) the magistrate determines that the person is not a  
| threat to public safety.  
|  _(d-1)_ _____A magistrate of the county in which the person is held_  
| _in custody shall release the person on personal bond pending the_  
| _hearing if:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the person has been confined for not less than 30_  
| _days only on a charge that the person committed an administrative_  
| _violation of release;_  
|  _(2)_ _____the warrant for the person's arrest did not include_  
| _a notice that the person is ineligible for release on bond as_  
| _provided by Subsection (e); and_  
|  _(3)_ _____the magistrate determines that the person is not a_  
| _threat to public safety._  
|  (e) The division shall include a notice on the warrant for  
| the person's arrest indicating that the person is _ineligible_  
| [ ~~eligible~~ ] for release on bond under _this section_ [ ~~Subsection
(d)~~ ]  
| if the division determines that the person:  
|  (1) has [ ~~not~~ ] been previously convicted of:  
|  (A) an offense under Chapter 29, Penal Code;  
|  (B) an offense under Title 5, Penal Code,  
| punishable as a felony; or  
|  (C) an offense involving family violence, as  
| defined by Section 71.004, Family Code;  
|  (2) is [ ~~not~~ ] on intensive supervision or  
| super-intensive supervision;  
|  (3) is [ ~~not~~ ] an absconder; _or_ [ ~~and~~ ]  
|  (4) is [ ~~not~~ ] a threat to public safety.  
|  (f) The provisions of Chapters 17 and 22, Code of Criminal  
| Procedure, apply to a person released under _this section_  
| [ ~~Subsection (d)~~ ] in the same manner as those provisions apply to a  
| person released pending an appearance before a court or magistrate,  
| except that the release under _this section_ [ ~~that subsection~~ ] is  
| conditioned on the person's appearance at a hearing under this  
| subchapter.  
|  SECTION 2. Subchapter I, Chapter 508, Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 508.285 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___508.285._ _____REIMBURSEMENT TO COUNTIES. (a) A county is_  
| _entitled to reimbursement from the department for the costs of_  
| _confining in the county jail a releasee or person described by_  
| _Section 508.281(a) who is confined only on a charge that the_  
| _releasee or person has committed an administrative violation of_  
| _release and that are incurred beginning on the 11th day of the_  
| _releasee's or person's confinement in the county jail._  
|  _(b)_ _____Not later than the 15th day of each quarter of a county's_  
| _fiscal year, the county judge shall certify to the comptroller of_  
| _public accounts the costs of confinement incurred during the_  
| _preceding fiscal quarter for which the county is entitled to_  
| _reimbursement under Subsection (a). The comptroller shall issue a_  
| _warrant in that amount to the commissioners court of the county._  
|  SECTION 3. The change in law made by this Act applies only  
| to a person who is arrested on or after the effective date of this  
| Act.  A person arrested before the effective date of this Act is  
| governed by the law in effect on the date the person was arrested,  
| and the former law is continued in effect for that purpose.  
|  SECTION 4. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
