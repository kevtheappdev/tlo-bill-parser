|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the disclosure of the composition of fluids and  
| materials transported by pipelines regulated by the Railroad  
| Commission of Texas.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. The heading to Subchapter S, Chapter 91, Natural  
| Resources Code, is amended to read as follows:  
| SUBCHAPTER S. DISCLOSURE OF COMPOSITION OF [ ~~HYDRAULIC FRACTURING~~ ]  
| FLUIDS _AND MATERIALS_  
|  SECTION 2. Subchapter S, Chapter 91, Natural Resources  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 91.852 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___91.852._ _____DISCLOSURE OF COMPOSITION OF FLUIDS AND_  
| _MATERIALS IN PIPELINES. (a)_ ___The commission by rule shall require a_  
| _holder of a permit or registration for a pipeline under the_  
| _jurisdiction of the commission's pipeline safety and regulatory_  
| _program to provide to the commission a list of the fluids and_  
| _materials transported by the pipeline._  
|  _(b) The commission shall:_  
|  _(1)_ _____adopt a standard reporting form to be used for the_  
| _purposes of this section; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____post lists received under this section on the_  
| _commission's Internet website._  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
