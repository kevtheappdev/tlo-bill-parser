|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to including cuttings as a form of propagation for citrus  
| budwood and citrus nursery stock certification programs.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Sections 19.002(3) and (5), Agriculture Code,  
| are amended to read as follows:  
|  (3) "Citrus budwood" means a portion of a stem or  
| branch of a citrus tree containing buds used in propagation _from_  
| _cuttings or_ by budding or grafting.  
|  (5) "Citrus nursery" means a producer of citrus trees  
| propagated _from cuttings or_ through the budding or grafting of  
| citrus trees using certified citrus budwood.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
