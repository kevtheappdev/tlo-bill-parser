|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to school district compliance with certain school safety  
| measures; providing an administrative penalty.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter G, Chapter 37, Education Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 37.219 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___37.219._ _____REPORT OF NONCOMPLIANCE WITH CERTAIN SCHOOL_  
| _SAFETY MEASURES. (a) The center shall report to the agency if a_  
| _school district fails to comply with a requirement under any of the_  
| _following provisions:_  
|  _(1) Section 37.108(a), (b), or (c);_  
|  _(2) Section 37.109;_  
|  _(3) Section 37.207(b); or_  
|  _(4) Section 37.2121(d)._  
|  _(b)_ _____If the agency receives a report under Subsection (a) of_  
| _a school district's_ ___failure to comply, the agency may impose an_  
| _administrative penalty against the district in an amount not_  
| _greater than the amount of the annual salary of the district's_  
| _superintendent._  
|  _(c)_ _____The commissioner may adopt rules as necessary to_  
| _implement this section._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
