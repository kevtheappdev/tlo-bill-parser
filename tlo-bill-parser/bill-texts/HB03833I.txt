|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to participation in a local provider participation fund by  
| certain counties.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Sec. 292.151, Health and Safety Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (f) to read as follows:  
|  _(f)_ _____For a county described in Section 292.002(1), the phrase_  
| _"institutional health care provider located in the county," as used_  
| _in subsection (a), shall include hospital services provided in any_  
| _facility located in the county, even if the hospital_  
| _providing such_  
| _services is not licensed in the county. Such county may determine_  
| _the net patient revenue attributable to the facility located in the_  
| _county based on publicly available information._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
