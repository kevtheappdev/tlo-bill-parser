|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the requirements for issuing and renewing certain  
| certificates and licenses to practice barbering.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 1601.265(a), Occupations Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (a) _Subject to Section 1603.2545, the_ [ ~~The~~ ] department  
| shall issue a license or certificate to an applicant for a license  
| or certificate issued under Section 1601.253, 1601.256, or 1601.258  
| if the applicant:  
|  (1) submits an application on a form prescribed by the  
| department;  
|  (2) pays the application fee; and  
|  (3) provides proof that the applicant holds a current  
| license to engage in the same or a similar activity issued by  
| another jurisdiction that has license requirements substantially  
| equivalent to those of this state.  
|  SECTION 2. Section 1601.402, Occupations Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (d) to read as follows:  
|  _(d)_ _____Notwithstanding Subsection (c), a Class A barber or_  
| _barber technician may not renew a certificate or license issued_  
| _under Section 1601.253 or 1601.256 unless the person has passed an_  
| _examination administered under Section 1603.2545. This subsection_  
| _expires June 1, 2022._  
|  SECTION 3. Subchapter F, Chapter 1603, Occupations Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 1603.2545 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___1603.2545._ _____EXAMINATION FOR CERTAIN CLASS A BARBER_  
| _CERTIFICATE OR BARBER TECHNICIAN LICENSE APPLICANTS._ ___An applicant_  
| _under Section 1601.265 for a Class A barber certificate or barber_  
| _technician license must pass an examination prescribed by the_  
| _department._ ___The content of the examination must focus on customer_  
| _safety, including sanitary and hygiene requirements._  
|  SECTION 4. (a) Not later than December 1, 2019, the Texas  
| Commission of Licensing and Regulation shall adopt rules necessary  
| to implement the changes in law made by this Act.  
|  (b) The changes in law made by this Act with respect to the  
| issuance of a certificate or license apply only to an application  
| for the issuance of a Class A barber certificate or barber  
| technician license submitted on or after January 1, 2020. An  
| application for the issuance of a Class A barber certificate or  
| barber technician license submitted before that date is governed by  
| the law in effect immediately before the effective date of this Act,  
| and the former law is continued in effect for that purpose.  
|  (c) Section 1601.402(d), Occupations Code, as added by this  
| Act, applies only to a Class A barber certificate or barber  
| technician license that expires on or after January 1, 2020.  A  
| certificate or license that expires before that date is governed by  
| the law in effect on the date the certificate or license expired,  
| and the former law is continued in effect for that purpose.  
|  SECTION 5. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
