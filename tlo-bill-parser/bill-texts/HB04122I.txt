|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the award of attorney's fees to a financial institution  
| that answers a writ of garnishment.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 276.002, Finance Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (d-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(d-1)_ _____Notwithstanding the Texas Rules of Civil Procedure or_  
| _any other law, if a financial institution timely files an answer to_  
| _a writ of garnishment, a court may not award attorney's fees to the_  
| _financial institution unless the garnishor unsuccessfully contests_  
| _the answer._  
|  SECTION 2. The change in law made by this Act applies only  
| to a writ of garnishment issued on or after the effective date of  
| this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
