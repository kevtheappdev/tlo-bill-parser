|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to filing fees in connection with assumed named  
| certificates for certain businesses owned by military veterans.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 71.155(a-1), Business & Commerce Code,  
| is amended to read as follows:  
|  (a-1) The county clerk may waive all fees under Subsection  
| (a) for a registrant _if the registrant is:_  
|  _(1)_ _____an individual who is a military veteran, as_  
| _defined by Section 55.001, Occupations Code; or_  
|  _(2)_ _____a business entity that does not have more than five_  
| _employees and is owned by at least one individual_ who is a military  
| veteran. [ ~~In this subsection, "military veteran" has the meaning~~  
|  ~~assigned by Section 55.001, Occupations Code.~~ ]  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
