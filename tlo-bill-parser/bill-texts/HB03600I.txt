|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to Texas Commission on Environmental Quality adoption of a  
| streamlined regulatory program for certain aggregate production  
| operations.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 28A, Water Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 28A.002 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___28A.002._ _____STREAMLINED PROGRAM FOR SMALL OPERATIONS._  
| _(a) The commission by rule shall adopt a program to offer_  
| _streamlined requirements for small aggregate production_  
| _operations._  
|  _(b)_ _____The commission shall establish eligibility criteria for_  
| _the program by rule._  
|  _(c)_ _____Notwithstanding Sections 28A.051 and 28A.053, the_  
| _program must:_  
|  _(1)_ _____allow the responsible party of a small aggregate_  
| _production operation required to register under this chapter to_  
| _register less frequently than annually; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____establish an inspection schedule for small_  
| _aggregate production operations that requires inspections at least_  
| _once every five years._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
