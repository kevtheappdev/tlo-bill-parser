|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to examination requirements for a journeyman lineman.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 1305.162, Occupations Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (f) to read as follows:  
|  _(f)_ _____Notwithstanding Subsection (b), an examination required_  
| _for a license as a journeyman lineman under Section 1305.1605 may_  
| _only test an applicant regarding activities performed by a_  
| _journeyman lineman as described in the National Electrical Safety_  
| _Code by the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, as_  
| _adopted by the commission._  
|  SECTION 2. As soon as practical, the Texas Commission of  
| Licensing and Regulation shall adopt rules as required by Section  
| 1305.102, Occupations Code, as amended by this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. Section 1305.162(f), Occupations Code, as added  
| by this Act, applies only to an examination for a journeyman lineman  
| license administered on or after June 1, 2020.  An examination for a  
| journeyman lineman license administered before that date is  
| governed by the law and rules in effect prior to the date this Act  
| was enacted, and the former law is continued in effect for that  
| purpose.  
|  
|  SECTION 4. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
