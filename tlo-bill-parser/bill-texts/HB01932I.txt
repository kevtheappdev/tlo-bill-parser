|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the inclusion of catastrophic debris management in  
| county emergency management programs.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 418.102, Government Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (a-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(a-1)_ _____An emergency management program required by_  
| _Subsection (a) and maintained by a county, or in which a county_  
| _participates, must provide for catastrophic debris management._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
