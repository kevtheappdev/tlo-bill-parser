|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the presiding officers of the boards of directors of  
| river authorities.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Title 5, Water Code, is amended by designating  
| Chapter 152 as Subtitle A and adding a subtitle heading to read as  
| follows:  
| _SUBTITLE A. RIVER AUTHORITIES_  
|  SECTION 2. Subtitle A, Title 5, Water Code, as added by this  
| Act, is amended by adding Chapter 150 to read as follows:  
| _CHAPTER 150. PROVISIONS GENERALLY APPLICABLE TO RIVER AUTHORITIES_  
|  _Sec. 150.0001. DEFINITIONS. In this chapter:_  
|  _(1)_ _____"Board" means the board of directors of a river_  
| _authority._  
|  _(2)_ _____"River authority" means a district created under_  
| _the authority of Section 59, Article XVI, Texas Constitution, as a_  
| _regional water management entity to provide water development and_  
| _planning services and other services to a river basin or portion of_  
| _a river basin._  
|  _Sec._ ___150.0002._ _____PRESIDING OFFICER. The governor shall_  
| _designate a member of the board of a river authority as the_  
| _presiding officer of the board to serve in that capacity at the_  
| _pleasure of the governor._  
|  SECTION 3. (a) In this section, "river authority" means a  
| district created under the authority of Section 59, Article XVI,  
| Texas Constitution, as a regional water management entity to  
| provide water development and planning services and other services  
| to a river basin or portion of a river basin.  
|  (b) For each river authority for which the governor was not  
| required to designate a presiding officer of the board of directors  
| for that river authority before the effective date of this Act, the  
| governor shall designate a presiding officer of the board of  
| directors of the river authority not later than January 31, 2020.  
|  SECTION 4. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
