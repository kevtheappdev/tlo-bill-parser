|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to an immunization rate tracking system for employees and  
| residents of certain long-term care facilities.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 161, Health and Safety  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 161.0053 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___161.0053._ _____IMMUNIZATION RATE TRACKING SYSTEM FOR_  
| _LONG-TERM CARE FACILITIES. (a) In this section, "long-term care_  
| _facility" means a facility licensed or regulated under Chapter 242,_  
| _247, or 252._  
|  _(b)_ _____A long-term care facility shall maintain a system to_  
| _track the immunization rates of the facility's employees and_  
| _residents. The system must include information on:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the aggregate immunization rates of the facility's_  
| _employees and residents; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____changes to the aggregate immunization rates_  
| _described by Subdivision (1)._  
|  _(c)_ _____A long-term care facility on request shall provide the_  
| _immunization rate data described by Subsection (b). The data_  
| _provided by the facility may not in any manner identify an employee_  
| _or resident._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
