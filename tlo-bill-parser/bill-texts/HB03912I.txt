|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to state agency reports submitted to the legislature.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 2052.0021, Government Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (f) to read as follows:  
|  _(f)_ _____Notwithstanding a law that requires a state agency to_  
| _submit a report to the legislature for an indefinite period, a state_  
| _agency:_  
|  _(1)_ _____is not required to submit the report to the_  
| _legislature after the 10th anniversary of the date the state agency_  
| _first submitted the report to the legislature; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____shall state in each report submitted under the law_  
| _requiring the report the date after which the agency is not required_  
| _to submit the report to the legislature as provided by Subdivision_  
| _(1)._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
