|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the authority of the Texas Water Development Board to  
| consider certain financial matters in a closed meeting.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 6, Water Code, is amended  
| by adding Section 6.0601 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___6.0601._ _____CONSULTATION REGARDING CERTAIN FINANCIAL_  
| _MATTERS; CLOSED MEETING._ ___(a)_ ___The board may hold a closed meeting_  
| _to consider and discuss:_  
|  _(1)_ _____financial issues related to bonds or securities_  
| _issued by the board;_  
|  _(2)_ _____financial issues related to bonds, securities,_  
| _negotiable instruments, or property interests to be issued or_  
| _purchased by the board; or_  
|  _(3)_ _____any matter related to the investment of the board's_  
| _funds that are pledged to or available for the payment of debt_  
| _service on bonds or securities issued by the board._  
|  _(b)_ _____A final action, decision, or vote on a matter considered_  
| _or discussed in a closed meeting held under this section must be_  
| _made in an open meeting conducted in compliance with the notice_  
| _provisions of Chapter 551, Government Code._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
