|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to damage to a motor vehicle caused by the vehicle being  
| flooded.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 501.091, Transportation Code, is amended  
| by adding Subdivision (4-a) to read as follows:  
|  _(4-a)_ _____"Flooded" means, with respect to damage to a_  
| _motor vehicle, submerged in water that:_  
|  _(A)_ _____rises higher than a doorsill of the vehicle,_  
| _enters the vehicle's passenger, trunk, or engine compartment, and_  
| _comes into contact with the vehicle's electrical system; or_  
|  _(B)_ _____causes an insurance company to pay a claim on_  
| _and take possession of the vehicle._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 501.09112(d), Transportation Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (d) A salvage vehicle title or a salvage record of title for  
| a vehicle that is a salvage motor vehicle because of damage caused  
| exclusively by _the vehicle being flooded_ [ ~~flood~~ ] must bear a  
| notation that the department considers appropriate. If the title  
| for a motor vehicle reflects the notation required by this  
| subsection, the owner may sell, transfer, or release the motor  
| vehicle only as provided by this subchapter.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
