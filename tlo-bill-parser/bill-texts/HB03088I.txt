|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to an exemption for certain purchasing personnel of the  
| Texas Department of Transportation from training on state  
| purchasing.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 656.051, Government Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (j) to read as follows:  
|  _(j)_ _____This section does not apply to purchasing personnel of_  
| _the Texas Department of Transportation whose duties relate_  
| _primarily to contracts described by Section 2262.002(b)._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
