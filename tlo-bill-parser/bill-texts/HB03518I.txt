|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the authority of a county to require a permit for  
| certain food service establishments.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 437.003, Health and Safety Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  Sec. 437.003. COUNTY AUTHORITY TO REQUIRE PERMIT. To  
| enforce state law and rules adopted under state law, the  
| commissioners court of a county by order may require food service  
| establishments, retail food stores, mobile food units, and roadside  
| food vendors _to obtain a permit from the county if the food service_  
| _establishment, retail food store, mobile food unit, or roadside_  
| _food vendor is located:_  
|  _(1)_ in _an_ unincorporated _area_ [ ~~areas~~ ] of the county,  
| including _an area_ [ ~~areas~~ ] in the extraterritorial jurisdiction of a  
| municipality _; or_  
|  _(2)_ _____on property or in a facility owned, operated, or_  
| _leased by_ [ ~~, to obtain a permit from~~ ] the county.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
