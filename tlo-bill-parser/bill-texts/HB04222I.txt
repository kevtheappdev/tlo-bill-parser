|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to health coverage for employees of a municipal school  
| district.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter D, Chapter 1579, Insurance Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 1579.156 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___1579.156._ _____PARTICIPATION BY MUNICIPAL SCHOOL DISTRICT._  
| _(a)_ ___A municipal school district, as described by Section 11.303,_  
| _Education Code, may:_  
|  _(1) elect to participate in the program; or_  
|  _(2)_ _____notwithstanding any previous election or_  
| _requirement to participate in the program, provide health coverage_  
| _to the district's employees through the health coverage plan_  
| _provided to the employees of the municipality in which the district_  
| _is located._  
|  _(b)_ _____If a municipal school district that participates in the_  
| _program elects to provide health coverage under Subsection (a)(2),_  
| _the municipal school district shall withdraw from the program by_  
| _notifying the trustee in writing that the district is withdrawing_  
| _from the program._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
