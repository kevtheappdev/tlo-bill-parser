|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the county hotel occupancy tax rate in certain  
| counties.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 352.003(c), Tax Code, is amended to read  
| as follows:  
|  (c) The rate in a county that does not have a municipality  
| may not exceed four percent of the price paid for a room in a hotel.  
| This subsection does not apply to _:_  
|  _(1)_ a county that[ ~~:~~  
|  [ ~~(1)~~ ] has a population of 10,000 or more _and_ [ ~~; and~~  
|  [ ~~(2)~~ ] borders the United Mexican States _; or_  
|  _(2)_ _____a county that borders three counties, each of_  
| _which borders the United Mexican States_.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
