|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to reinsurance for catastrophic losses provided to the  
| Texas Windstorm Insurance Association by member insurers.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter J, Chapter 2210, Insurance Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 2210.4522 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___2210.4522._ _____REINSURANCE REQUIRED. (a)_ ___Notwithstanding_  
| _any other provision of this chapter, a member insurer shall provide_  
| _to the association reinsurance in lieu of reinsurance purchased_  
| _under Section 2210.453 at no cost to the association. The_  
| _proportion of the losses that exceed the attachment point for the_  
| _provided reinsurance is calculated in the manner used to determine_  
| _each insurer's participation in the association for the year under_  
| _Section 2210.052._  
|  _(b)_ _____A member insurer may purchase reinsurance to cover the_  
| _proportion of losses for which the insurer is responsible under_  
| _Subsection (a)._  
|  SECTION 2. The Texas Windstorm Insurance Association shall  
| amend the association's plan of operation to conform to the changes  
| in law made by this Act not later than the 60th day after the  
| effective date of this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
