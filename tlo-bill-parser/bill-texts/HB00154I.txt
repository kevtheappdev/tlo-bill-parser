|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the recording of certain personal information of voters  
| by election officials.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 63, Election Code, is amended by adding  
| Section 63.0015 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___63.0015._ _____RECORDED DOCUMENTATION OF CERTAIN PERSONAL_  
| _INFORMATION. (a) An election officer may copy documentation_  
| _presented by a voter under Section 63.001(b) or record information_  
| _from the documentation._  
|  _(b)_ _____An election officer may photograph the entire face of a_  
| _voter who is accepted for voting under this chapter if:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the documentation presented by a voter under_  
| _Section 63.001(b) is not documentation issued by the Department of_  
| _Public Safety containing the person's photograph; or_  
|  _(2)_ _____the election official questions the authenticity_  
| _of the documentation presented by a voter under Section 63.001(b),_  
| _regardless of whether the documentation is issued by the Department_  
| _of Public Safety and contains the person's photograph._  
|  _(c)_ _____A voter may only be photographed under this section_  
| _while being accepted for voting and may not be photographed while_  
| _the voter is occupying a voting station._  
|  _(d)_ _____Information copied or recorded under this section,_  
| _including a photograph produced under Subsection (b), is_  
| _confidential except for use in a criminal investigation or_  
| _prosecution or a civil court proceeding._  
|  _(e)_ _____All information collected under this section shall be_  
| _provided to the secretary of state for election-related purposes._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
