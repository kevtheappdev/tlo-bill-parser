|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a study on the suitability of unused and underused  
| public facilities for joint use by state and local governmental  
| entities.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. DEFINITIONS. In this Act:  
|  (1) "Governmental entity" means this state, a county,  
| a municipality, or a school district.  
|  (2) "Public facility" means a facility owned or  
| operated by a governmental entity.  
|  SECTION 2. STUDY AND REPORT. (a)  The comptroller of public  
| accounts shall conduct a study to assess the availability and  
| suitability of existing unused and underused public facilities for  
| joint use by two governmental entities.  The study shall assess the  
| economic advantages of that use.  
|  (b) Not later than December 1, 2020, the comptroller shall  
| submit to the governor, lieutenant governor, speaker of the house  
| of representatives, and members of the legislature a written report  
| on the results of the study.  
|  SECTION 3. EXPIRATION DATE. This Act expires January 1,  
| 2021.  
|  SECTION 4. EFFECTIVE DATE. This Act takes effect September  
| 1, 2019.
