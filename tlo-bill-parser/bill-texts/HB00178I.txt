|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a credit toward payment of costs for a defendant  
| confined in jail or another facility before sentencing.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 2, Article 42.03, Code of Criminal  
| Procedure, is amended by adding Subsection (a-1) to read as  
| follows:  
|  _(a-1)_ _____In addition to the credit on the defendant's sentence_  
| _given under Subsection (a), for each day of confinement described_  
| _by Subdivision (1) of that subsection, the judge shall give the_  
| _defendant, at a rate of $100 per day, credit toward payment of costs_  
| _imposed on the defendant._  
|  SECTION 2. The change in law made by this Act applies to a  
| defendant who is sentenced for an offense on or after the effective  
| date of this Act, regardless of whether the offense is committed  
| before, on, or after the effective date of this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
