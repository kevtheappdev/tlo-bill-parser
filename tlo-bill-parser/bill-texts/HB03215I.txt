|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the authority of a county auditor to oversee the  
| expenditure of county funds.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 111.092, Local Government Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  Sec. 111.092. [ ~~DEPARTMENTAL~~ ] EXPENSES NOT TO EXCEED  
| APPROPRIATIONS.  The county auditor shall oversee the warrant  
| process to ensure that _:_  
|  _(1)_ the expenses of any department do not exceed the  
| budget appropriations for that department _; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____the expenses for each budgeted item in the budget_  
| _do not exceed the budget appropriations for that item_.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
