|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the number of emissions inspections performed by  
| certain vehicle inspection stations.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 548.3075(b), Transportation Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (b) A department rule that allows _an_ [ ~~a qualified~~ ]  
| inspection station to perform a limited emissions inspection of a  
| motor vehicle may not restrict the _number of limited emissions_  
| [ ~~station to fewer than 150~~ ] inspections _conducted by the station_  
| [ ~~per month~~ ].  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
