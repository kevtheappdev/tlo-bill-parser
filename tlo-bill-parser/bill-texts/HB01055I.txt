|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the elevation of high-speed rail tracks in certain  
| counties.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 112, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 112.104 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___112.104._ _____ELEVATED HIGH-SPEED RAIL TRACKS IN CERTAIN_  
| _COUNTIES._ ___(a)_ ___In this section, "high-speed rail" has the meaning_  
| _assigned by Section 112.201._  
|  _(b)_ _____Except as necessary for passenger boarding, high-speed_  
| _rail tracks located in a county with a population of more than one_  
| _million or a county adjacent to a county with a population of more_  
| _than one million must be elevated on pylons to 40 feet or more above_  
| _the ground._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
