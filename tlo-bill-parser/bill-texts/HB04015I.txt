|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to liability for the collection of late fees by a landowner  
| or landlord.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 75, Civil Practices and Remedies Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 75.008 to read as follows:  
|  
| _Sec._ ___75.008._ _____LIABILITY LIMITED FOR LATE FEES COLLECTED BY A_  
| _LANDLORD._  
|  (a) _A landlord is not liable for damages related to the_  
| _collection of a late fee from a tenant for the tenant's failure to_  
| _pay any portion of delinquent rent unless the late fee exceeds the_  
| _greater of $150 or ten percent of the amount of rent for the rental_  
| _pay period under the lease._  
|  (b) _The calculation of the fee under subsection (a) shall_  
| _include any direct or indirect costs or damages associated with the_  
| _landlord's collection of delinquent rent._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
