|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a limitation on life insurance proceeds for terroristic  
| acts.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 1101, Insurance Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 1101.014 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___1101.014._ _____LIMITATION ON PROCEEDS DUE TO TERRORISTIC_  
| _ACT. Notwithstanding any other law, a life insurance policy may_  
| _provide that proceeds are limited to the amount paid in premiums if:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the insured's death occurs as a result of the_  
| _insured's conduct constituting an offense involving violence to any_  
| _person or property and with the intent to:_  
|  _(A)_ _____place the public or a substantial group of_  
| _the public in fear of serious bodily injury;_  
|  _(B)_ _____influence the conduct or activities of a_  
| _branch or agency of the federal government, this state, or a_  
| _political subdivision of this state; or_  
|  _(C)_ _____significantly disrupt or interfere with_  
| _lawful commerce or the right of lawful assembly; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____the conduct described by Subdivision (1) occurred_  
| _not later than the second anniversary of the effective date of the_  
| _policy._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 1101.014, Insurance Code, as added by  
| this Act, applies only to an insurance policy delivered, issued for  
| delivery, or renewed on or after January 1, 2020.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
