|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to requiring public institutions of higher education to  
| document and maintain information regarding the granting of certain  
| undergraduate course credit to entering freshman students.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 51.968, Education Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (h) to read as follows:  
|  _(h)_ _____For purposes of evaluating the policy adopted under_  
| _Subsection (b), an institution of higher education shall document_  
| _and maintain information regarding each granting of undergraduate_  
| _course credit under that policy for achieving a required score on an_  
| _Advanced Placement examination, including:_  
|  _(1) the subject of the examination;_  
|  _(2) the score achieved on the examination; and_  
|  _(3) the course for which credit was granted._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 51.968(h), Education Code, as added by  
| this Act, applies beginning with undergraduate course credit  
| granted in the 2019-2020 academic year.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
