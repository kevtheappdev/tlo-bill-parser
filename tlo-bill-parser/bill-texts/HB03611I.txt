|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the number of persons authorized to reside in certain  
| homes and facilities for persons with disabilities.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 123.006, Human Resources Code, is  
| amended by adding Subsection (c) to read as follows:  
|  _(c)_ _____The limitation on the number of persons who may reside_  
| _in a community home at the same time imposed by this section does_  
| _not apply to an assisted living facility licensed under Chapter_  
| _247, Health and Safety Code, unless the assisted living facility_  
| _qualifies as a community home in accordance with Section 123.004._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
