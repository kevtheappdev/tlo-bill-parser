|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to requiring regional education service centers to provide  
| information to school district employees regarding health and  
| retirement benefits and financial literacy.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 8, Education Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 8.059 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___8.059._ _____INFORMATION REGARDING EMPLOYEE BENEFITS. Each_  
| _regional education service center, in cooperation with the Teacher_  
| _Retirement System of Texas, shall annually collaborate with school_  
| _districts in the center's region to:_  
|  _(1)_ _____organize an educational workshop to provide_  
| _information to district employees regarding:_  
|  _(A)_ _____health benefits for district employees and_  
| _retirees, including:_  
|  _(i) benefits provided by each district; and_  
|  _(ii)_ _____health benefit plans administered by_  
| _the Teacher Retirement System of Texas;_  
|  _(B)_ _____retirement benefits, including benefits_  
| _provided by the Teacher Retirement System of Texas; and_  
|  _(C)_ _____personal financial literacy regarding health_  
| _care and retirement planning; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____distribute the information described by_  
| _Subdivision (1) to the district employees._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
