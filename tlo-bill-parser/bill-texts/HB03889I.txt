|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the election of trustees of certain school districts.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 11.052, Education Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (b-3) and amending Subsection (d) to read as  
| follows:  
|  _(b-3)_ _____Subsection (c) does not apply in the case of an order_  
| _adopted under this subsection. The board of trustees of an_  
| _independent school district located wholly or partly in a county_  
| _with a population of more than two million but less than three_  
| _million shall order that trustees of the district are to be elected_  
| _from single-member trustee districts if the district, in October_  
| _2017, had:_  
|  _(1) a student enrollment of more than 50,000;_  
|  _(2)_ _____a student enrollment of more than 35,000 but less_  
| _than 40,000; or_  
|  _(3)_ _____a student enrollment of more than 24,000 but less_  
| _than 28,000._  
|  (d) An order of the board adopted under Subsection (a) _,_ [ ~~or~~ ]  
| (b) _, or (b-3)_ must be entered not later than the 120th day before  
| the date of the first election at which all or some of the trustees  
| are elected from single-member trustee districts authorized by the  
| order.  
|  SECTION 2. The board of trustees of a school district to  
| which Section 11.052(b-3), Education Code, as added by this Act,  
| applies shall adopt an order as required by that section not later  
| than December 31, 2019.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
