|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the designation of certain federal funds for border  
| infrastructure projects.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 222, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 222.036 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___222.036._ _____DESIGNATION OF CERTAIN FUNDS FOR BORDER_  
| _INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECTS. As authorized by the Fixing America's_  
| _Surface Transportation Act (Pub. L. No. 114-94) or other similar_  
| _federal law, the governor shall, each fiscal year, designate at_  
| _least five percent of funds available to this state under 23 U.S.C._  
| _Section 133(d)(1)(B) for border infrastructure projects._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
