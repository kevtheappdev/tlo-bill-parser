|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to certain notice and license display requirements  
| applicable to massage therapy.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 455.204, Occupations Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (b-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(b-1)_ _____The license of a massage therapist that is posted_  
| _under Subsection (b) must have a photograph of the massage_  
| _therapist attached to the front of the license._  
|  SECTION 2. Subchapter E, Chapter 455, Occupations Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 455.207 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___455.207._ _____POSTING OF CERTAIN NOTICES REQUIRED. (a)_ __  
| _Each massage establishment and massage school shall display in the_  
| _form and manner prescribed by the commission a sign concerning_  
| _services and assistance available to victims of human trafficking._  
|  _(b) The sign required by this section must:_  
|  _(1)_ _____be in English, Spanish, Korean, Mandarin, and any_  
| _other language required by commission rule;_  
|  _(2)_ _____include a toll-free telephone number of a_  
| _nationally recognized information and referral hotline for victims_  
| _of human trafficking; and_  
|  _(3)_ _____be displayed in a conspicuous place clearly_  
| _visible to the public._  
|  _(c)_ _____The commission by rule shall establish requirements_  
| _regarding the posting of signs under this section._  
|  SECTION 3. Not later than November 1, 2019, the Texas  
| Commission of Licensing and Regulation shall adopt rules necessary  
| to implement the changes in law made by this Act to Chapter 455,  
| Occupations Code.  The commission may adopt the rules in the manner  
| provided by law for emergency rules under Chapter 2001, Government  
| Code.  
|  SECTION 4. Each massage establishment and massage school,  
| as those terms are defined by Section 455.001, Occupations Code,  
| shall comply with Sections 455.204(b-1) and 455.207, Occupations  
| Code, as added by this Act, as applicable, not later than January 1,  
| 2020.  
|  SECTION 5. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
