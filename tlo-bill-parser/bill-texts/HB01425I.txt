|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the duration of laboratory support for testing of  
| vector-borne infectious diseases in border counties.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. The heading to Subchapter F, Chapter 12, Health  
| and Safety Code, is amended to read as follows:  
| SUBCHAPTER F. [ ~~OFFICE OF~~ ] BORDER HEALTH  
|  SECTION 2. Subchapter F, Chapter 12, Health and Safety  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 12.074 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___12.074._ _____YEAR-ROUND LABORATORY TESTING FOR_  
| _VECTOR-BORNE INFECTIOUS DISEASES. The department shall provide_  
| _year-round laboratory support for arboviral testing of_  
| _vector-borne infectious diseases in counties located along the_  
| _international border with Mexico._  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
