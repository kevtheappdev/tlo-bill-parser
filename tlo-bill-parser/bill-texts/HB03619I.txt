|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a requirement regarding the inclusion of certain  
| residential property in a school district's boundaries.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter F, Chapter 13, Education Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 13.232 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___13.232._ _____SUBDIVISION BOUNDARY ADJUSTMENT. The_  
| _commissioner by rule shall adopt procedures for school districts to_  
| _adjust boundaries to ensure that all homes in a particular_  
| _residential subdivision are included in the same district._  
|  SECTION 2. As soon as practicable after the effective date  
| of this Act, the commissioner of education shall adopt procedures  
| as necessary to implement Section 13.232, Education Code, as added  
| by this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
