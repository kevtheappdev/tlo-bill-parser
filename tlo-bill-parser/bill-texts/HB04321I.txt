|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to certain conflicts of interest of the commissioner of  
| insurance and employees of the Texas Department of Insurance;  
| requiring registration.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 33, Insurance Code, is amended by adding  
| Section 33.008 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___33.008._ _____PROHIBITION AGAINST ACCEPTANCE OF GIFTS,_  
| _GRATUITIES, OR OTHER THING OF VALUE; EXCEPTION. (a) Except as_  
| _provided by Subsections (b) and (d), the commissioner or an_  
| _employee of the department may not accept a gift, gratuity, or other_  
| _thing of value, including transportation and travel expenses, from_  
| _a person who:_  
|  _(1)_ _____is employed by or participates in the management_  
| _of an entity or other organization that is regulated by the_  
| _department;_  
|  _(2)_ _____owns or controls, directly or indirectly, more_  
| _than a 10 percent interest of a business entity or other_  
| _organization that is regulated by the department;_  
|  _(3)_ _____is related within the second degree of_  
| _consanguinity to a person described by Subdivision (1) or (2); or_  
|  _(4)_ _____is an agent, representative, attorney, employee,_  
| _officer, owner, director, or partner of an entity, organization, or_  
| _person described by Subdivision (1), (2), or (3)._  
|  _(b)_ _____Notwithstanding any other law, the commissioner or an_  
| _employee of the department may accept from a person described by_  
| _Subsection (a)(1), (2), or (4) a gift, gratuity, or other thing of_  
| _value if:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the person is registered under Chapter 305,_  
| _Government Code;_  
|  _(2)_ _____the gift, gratuity, or other thing of value has a_  
| _value of $50 or less;_  
|  _(3)_ _____the registrant states, on the registrant's report_  
| _under Section 305.006, Government Code:_  
|  _(A)_ _____that the gift, gratuity, or other thing of_  
| _value was given by or on behalf of the registrant to the_  
| _commissioner or the employee of the department;_  
|  _(B)_ _____a general description of the gift, gratuity,_  
| _or other thing of value; and_  
|  _(C)_ _____the amount of the expenditure by appropriate_  
| _category of amount, as determined by the Texas Ethics Commission;_  
| _and_  
|  _(4)_ _____the gift, gratuity, or other thing of value does_  
| _not violate Section 36.08 or 36.09, Penal Code, as described by_  
| _Section 36.10, Penal Code._  
|  _(c)_ _____Notwithstanding Section 305.003, Government Code, a_  
| _person described by Subsection (a)(1), (2), or (4) shall register_  
| _with the Texas Ethics Commission under Chapter 305, Government_  
| _Code, if the person gives to the commissioner or an employee of the_  
| _department a gift, gratuity, or other thing of value the value of_  
| _which is $50 or less._  
|  _(d)_ _____The commissioner or an employee of the department may_  
| _not accept from a person described by Subsection (a)(1), (2), or (4)_  
| _gifts, gratuities, or other things of value the aggregate value of_  
| _which exceeds $2,000 during a calendar year._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 33.008, Insurance Code, as added by this  
| Act, applies only to a gift, gratuity, or thing of value accepted or  
| given on or after the effective date of this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
