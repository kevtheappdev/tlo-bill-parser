|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to an agreement between the Texas State Library and  
| Archives Commission and the City of Austin regarding an easement  
| across commission property.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 441, Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 441.0165 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___441.0165._ _____EASEMENT OVER COMMISSION PROPERTY. (a) The_  
| _commission and the City of Austin shall enter into an agreement_  
| _under which the city may use any amount credited in favor of the_  
| _city under Section 2166.052(c) to acquire, for a reasonable value,_  
| _an easement across commission property._  
|  _(b)_ _____The use of any amount credited in favor of the City of_  
| _Austin under Subsection (a) is subject to approval by the Texas_  
| _Facilities Commission._  
|  _(c)_ _____Section 272.001, Local Government Code, does not apply_  
| _to a transaction governed by this section._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
