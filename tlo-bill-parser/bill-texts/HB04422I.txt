|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to permit conditions relating to road paving for certain  
| mining-related facilities.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 382, Health and Safety  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 382.071 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___382.071._ _____ROAD PAVING AT MINING-RELATED FACILITIES._ __  
| _(a) This section applies only to a permit issued under this chapter_  
| _relating to the:_  
|  _(1)_ _____production of aggregates, as defined by Section_  
| _28A.001, Water Code;_  
|  _(2)_ _____operation of a concrete plant that performs wet_  
| _batching, dry batching, or central mixing; or_  
|  _(3) operation of a hot mix asphalt plant._  
|  _(b)_ _____The commission by rule shall require as a condition of a_  
| _permit that the permit holder:_  
|  _(1)_ _____pave each road inside the permitted facility used_  
| _for the movement of commercial vehicles:_  
|  _(A)_ _____that are subject to the federal electronic_  
| _logging device requirements of 49 C.F.R. Part 395; or_  
|  _(B)_ _____that are operating only in intrastate_  
| _commerce that would be subject to the federal electronic logging_  
| _device requirements of 49 C.F.R. Part 395 if operating in_  
| _interstate or international commerce._  
|  _(2)_ _____construct acceleration and deceleration lanes for_  
| _egress from and ingress to the aggregate production operation to_  
| _protect_  
| _public safety._  
|  _(c)_ _____Rules adopted under this section may specify vehicles_  
| _and equipment that may be moved on an unpaved road because the_  
| _movement produces minimal dust or other emissions of air_  
| _contaminants._  
|  SECTION 2. As soon as practicable after the effective date  
| of this Act, the Texas Commission on Environmental Quality shall  
| adopt rules as necessary to implement Section 382.071, Health and  
| Safety Code, as added by this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
