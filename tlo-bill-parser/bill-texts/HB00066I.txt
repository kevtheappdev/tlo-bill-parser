|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to student success-based funding recommendations for  
| certain continuing workforce education courses offered by public  
| junior colleges.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 61.0593, Education Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (f-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(f-1)_ _____In making its recommendations under Subsection (f)_  
| _relating to institutional appropriations of incentive funds for_  
| _public junior colleges based on achievement on student success_  
| _measures, the board shall incorporate the consideration of student_  
| _success measures, and include appropriate funding recommendations_  
| _based on those success measures, achieved in qualified continuing_  
| _workforce education courses offered by those colleges for which_  
| _credit toward a certificate or associate's degree is not awarded._ __  
| _For purposes of this subsection, a continuing workforce education_  
| _course is considered qualified if the course is consistent with the_  
| _role and mission of a public junior college and is designed to_  
| _prepare students with skills necessary to attain external workforce_  
| _credentials, including a certification or license, or employment in_  
| _a specific high-demand occupation, regardless of whether the course_  
| _is part of a recognized sequence of courses that requires a specific_  
| _number of contact hours._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
