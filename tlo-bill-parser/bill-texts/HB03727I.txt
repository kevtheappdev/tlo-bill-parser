|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the online publication of information by certain  
| special purpose districts.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 775, Health and Safety  
| Code, is amended by adding Section 775.046 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___775.046._ _____ONLINE PUBLICATION. (a)_ ___A district with a_  
| _population of more than 100 shall maintain an Internet website._  
|  _(b)_ _____The board shall ensure that the following information is_  
| _posted on the district's Internet website:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the name and contact information for each_  
| _commissioner;_  
|  _(2) meeting agendas;_  
|  _(3) meeting minutes; and_  
|  _(4)_ _____dates of elections of commissioners, if the_  
| _district's commissioners are elected._  
|  _(c)_ _____The board shall ensure that the district's election_  
| _information, tax rate, and budget are posted on the district's_  
| _Internet website at least 60 days before the date of an election_  
| _held by the district._  
|  SECTION 2. Subchapter D, Chapter 54, Water Code, is amended  
| by adding Section 54.244 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___54.244._ _____ONLINE PUBLICATION. (a)_ ___A district with a_  
| _population of more than 100 shall maintain an Internet website._  
|  _(b)_ _____The board shall ensure that the following information is_  
| _posted on the district's Internet website:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the name and contact information for each_  
| _director;_  
|  _(2) meeting agendas;_  
|  _(3) meeting minutes; and_  
|  _(4)_ _____dates of elections of directors, if the district's_  
| _directors are elected._  
|  _(c)_ _____The board shall ensure that the district's election_  
| _information, tax rate, and budget are posted on the district's_  
| _Internet website at least 60 days before the date of an election_  
| _held by the district._  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
