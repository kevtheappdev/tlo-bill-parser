|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the creation of an additional judicial district  
| composed of Denton County.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 24, Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 24.60011 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___24.60011._ _____467TH JUDICIAL DISTRICT (DENTON COUNTY)._  
| _The 467th Judicial District is composed of Denton County._  
|  SECTION 2. The 467th Judicial District is created on  
| January 1, 2021.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
