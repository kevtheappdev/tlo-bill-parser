|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the disposition of certain amounts collected for rail  
| safety.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 111.101(e), Transportation Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (e) A fee collected under this section shall be deposited to  
| the credit of the _state highway fund_ [ ~~general revenue fund~~ ] to be  
| used for the rail safety program.  
|  SECTION 2. Section 111.103, Transportation Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (f) to read as follows:  
|  _(f)_ _____A fee collected under this section shall be deposited to_  
| _the credit of the state highway fund._  
|  SECTION 3. Section 471.005(c), Transportation Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (c) Money received under Subsection (a)(2) shall be  
| deposited in the state treasury _to the credit of the state highway_  
| _fund_.  
|  SECTION 4. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
