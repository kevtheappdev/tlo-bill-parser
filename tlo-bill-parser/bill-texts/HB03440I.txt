|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the electronic submission of required jail reports.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 511, Government Code, is amended by  
| adding Section 511.0104 to read as follows:  
|  _Sect. 511.0104. ELECTRONIC SUBMISSION OF FORMS, DATA, AND_  
| _DOCUMENTS._ ___(a)_ ___The commission by rule shall:_  
|  _(1)_ _____develop and establish a system for the submission_  
| _of forms, data, and documents required to be submitted to the_  
| _commission under this chapter in electronic format; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____once that system is established, require_  
| _facilities under the commission's purview to submit to the_  
| _commission electronically and in electronic format any form, data,_  
| _or document required to be submitted to the commission under this_  
| _chapter._  
|  _(3)_ _____The commission may allow submission in a_  
| _non-electronic format but shall set and collect a reasonable fee_  
| _for processing._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2020.
