|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a report on cost savings attributable to the Medicaid  
| managed care program.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 533, Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 533.037 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___533.037._ _____REPORT ON PROGRAM SAVINGS. Not later than_  
| _December 1 of each even-numbered year, the commission shall submit_  
| _to the legislature a written report that specifies, for each of the_  
| _two preceding state fiscal years:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the amount of money appropriated to the commission_  
| _for the Medicaid managed care program that was not spent;_  
|  _(2)_ _____the amount of profit sharing received under_  
| _Section 533.014;_  
|  _(3)_ _____the estimated savings resulting from access to_  
| _preventive care and improved quality of health care services for_  
| _recipients; and_  
|  _(4)_ _____a comparison of the total cost of providing_  
| _services to recipients for that state fiscal year with the total_  
| _cost of providing those services in the preceding state fiscal_  
| _year, adjusted to eliminate the effects of program enrollment_  
| _growth._  
|  SECTION 2. Not later than December 1, 2020, the Health and  
| Human Services Commission shall submit the first report required by  
| Section 533.037, Government Code, as added by this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
