|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a prohibition on the use of certain audio recordings or  
| video footage to influence the negotiation of a real estate  
| transaction.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 1101.651, Occupations Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (e) to read as follows:  
|  _(e)_ _____A license holder representing a seller in a real estate_  
| _transaction may not use or obtain an audio recording or video_  
| _footage from surveillance equipment installed or operated inside_  
| _the seller's property to influence negotiations with a prospective_  
| _buyer of that property._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
