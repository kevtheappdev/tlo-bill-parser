|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to grants for community supervision and corrections  
| departments for supervision officer caseload management.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 509, Government Code, is amended by  
| adding Section 509.0135 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___509.0135._ _____GRANT FOR CASELOAD MANAGEMENT. The division_  
| _may award grants to departments to enable the department to obtain_  
| _the services of a social worker licensed under Chapter 505,_  
| _Occupations Code, to assist in:_  
|  _(1)_ _____reviewing the department's caseload management_  
| _policies; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____revising the policies so that an offender's risk_  
| _level is considered in determining supervision officer caseloads._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
