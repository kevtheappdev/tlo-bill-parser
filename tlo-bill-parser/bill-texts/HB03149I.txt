|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to examinations required for licensure by the Texas Real  
| Estate Commission.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 1101.403, Occupations Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (d-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(d-1)_ _____The commission shall ensure that any examination_  
| _required by this chapter or Chapter 1102, including an examination_  
| _administered by a testing service, is offered in Spanish on request_  
| _of an applicant._  
|  SECTION 2. The change in law made by this Act applies only  
| to an examination under Chapter 1101 or 1102, Occupations Code,  
| that is administered on or after January 1, 2020.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
