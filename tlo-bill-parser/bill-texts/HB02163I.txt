|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the types of vehicles regulated as neighborhood  
| electric vehicles.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 551.301, Transportation Code, is amended  
| to read as follows:  
|  Sec. 551.301. DEFINITION. In this subchapter,  
| "neighborhood electric vehicle" means _an electric_ [ ~~a~~ ] vehicle that  
| can attain a maximum speed of 35 miles per hour on a paved level  
| surface and otherwise complies with _:_  
|  _(1)_ _____Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards applicable_  
| _to motorcycles in 49 C.F.R. Part 571 for a vehicle that has at least_  
| _three wheels in contact with the ground; or_  
|  _(2)_ Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard 500 (49  
| C.F.R. Section 571.500) _for low-speed vehicles_.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
