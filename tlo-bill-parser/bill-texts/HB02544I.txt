|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the authority of certain tier 1 general-law  
| municipalities to annex certain areas on petition by the area's  
| property owners.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 43, Local Government Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 43.0325 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___43.0325._ _____AUTHORITY OF CERTAIN MUNICIPALITIES TO ANNEX_  
| _NONCONTIGUOUS AREA ON OWNER PETITION. (a) This section applies_  
| _only to a municipality with a population of 1,780 to 1,800._  
|  _(b)_ _____A municipality may annex an area that is noncontiguous_  
| _to the boundaries of the municipality if:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the area's land development standards are more_  
| _stringent than the land development standards in the municipality;_  
| _and_  
|  _(2)_ _____the municipality receives a petition requesting_  
| _the annexation that:_  
|  _(A) describes the area by metes and bounds;_  
|  _(B)_ _____is signed by each owner of real property in_  
| _the area; and_  
|  _(C)_ _____is filed with the secretary of the_  
| _municipality._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
