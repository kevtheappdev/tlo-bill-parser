|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to limitations periods in arbitration proceedings.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 171, Civil Practice and  
| Remedies Code, is amended by adding Section 171.004 to read as  
| follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___171.004._ _____APPLICABILITY OF LIMITATIONS PERIODS. (a)_ ___A_  
| _party may not assert a claim in an arbitration proceeding if the_  
| _party could not bring suit for the claim in court due to the_  
| _expiration of the applicable limitations period._  
|  _(b)_ _____A party may assert a claim in an arbitration proceeding_  
| _after expiration of the applicable limitations period if:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the party brought suit for the claim in court_  
| _before the expiration of the applicable limitations period; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____a court ordered the parties to arbitrate the_  
| _claim._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
