|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the status of personal automobile or residential  
| property insurance policy summary documents.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter B, Chapter 2301, Insurance Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 2301.057 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___2301.057._ _____CERTAIN DOCUMENTS NOT PART OF FORM. (a)_ ___A_  
| _document providing a summary of a policy of personal automobile_  
| _insurance or residential property insurance or a summary of an_  
| _endorsement to such a policy or other ancillary material, including_  
| _an advertisement for the policy or endorsement, is not part of the_  
| _policy or endorsement form._  
|  _(b)_ _____A summary described by Subsection (a) does not modify_  
| _the provisions of the insurance policy for which the summary was_  
| _provided._  
|  _(c)_ _____A summary described by Subsection (a) is not admissible_  
| _as evidence in any court or in any other legal or administrative_  
| _proceeding._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
