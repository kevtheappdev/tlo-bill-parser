|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the issuance of sickle cell disease awareness specialty  
| license plates.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter G, Chapter 504, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 504.671 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___504.671._ _____SICKLE CELL DISEASE AWARENESS LICENSE PLATES._  
| _(a)_ ___The department shall issue specialty license plates that_  
| _include the words "Sickle Cell Matters"_ ___and an image of the burgundy_  
| _sickle cell ribbon._ ___The department shall design the plates in_  
| _consultation with the Sickle Cell Association of Houston._  
|  _(b)_ _____After deduction of the department's administrative_  
| _costs, the remainder of the fee for issuance of the license plates_  
| _shall be deposited to the credit of an account created by the_  
| _comptroller in the manner provided by Section 504.6012(b). Money_  
| _deposited to that account may be used only by Texas Southern_  
| _University to make grants to a nonprofit organization that has a_  
| _history of providing services to persons with sickle cell disease_  
| _for the purpose of providing those services._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
