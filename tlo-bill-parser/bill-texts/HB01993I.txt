|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to an exemption for teachers and retired teachers from the  
| payment of a fee for registration of a motor vehicle.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter J, Chapter 502, Transportation Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 502.458 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___502.458._ _____TEACHER AND RETIRED TEACHER EXEMPTION. (a)_  
| _In this section, "teacher" means a principal, supervisor, classroom_  
| _teacher, school counselor, or other full-time professional_  
| _employee who is required to hold a certificate issued under_  
| _Subchapter B, Chapter 21, Education Code._  
|  _(b)_ _____A vehicle may be registered without payment if the_  
| _vehicle is owned or leased by a person who is a teacher or a retired_  
| _teacher._  
|  _(c)_ _____An application for registration under Subsection (b)_  
| _must be accompanied by proof of the applicant's entitlement to the_  
| _exemption._ ___The department shall adopt rules relating to the proof_  
| _of entitlement to the exemption, which may include, for retired_  
| _teachers, statements or other communications issued by the Teacher_  
| _Retirement System of Texas that evidence payment of an annuity from_  
| _the system._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 502.458, Transportation Code, as added  
| by this Act, applies only to an application submitted on or after  
| the effective date of this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
