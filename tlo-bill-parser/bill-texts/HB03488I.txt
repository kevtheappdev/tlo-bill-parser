|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to selection of election officers for early voting.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter A, Chapter 85, Election Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 85.0092 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___85.0092._ _____OPTIONAL EARLY VOTING ELECTION OFFICER_  
| _SHIFTS. (a)_ ___In selecting election officers for the main early_  
| _voting polling place and any branch polling place, the county clerk_  
| _may select a sufficient number of presiding judges, alternate_  
| _presiding judges, and other election officers to enable the_  
| _election officers to serve at a polling place in shifts._  
|  _(b)_ _____In selecting judges and officers under Subsection (a),_  
| _the county clerk may select a person to serve as a judge or officer_  
| _at more than one polling place._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
