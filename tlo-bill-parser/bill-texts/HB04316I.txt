|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a dog's restraint and shelter.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. The heading to Subchapter D, Chapter 821, Health  
| and Safety Code, is amended to read as follows:  
| SUBCHAPTER D. UNLAWFUL RESTRAINT OF _AND SHELTER FOR_ DOG  
|  SECTION 2. Section 821.076, Health and Safety Code, is  
| amended by amending Subdivision (1) and adding Subdivision (1-a) to  
| read as follows:  
|  (1) _"Adequate shelter" means a sturdy structure that_  
| _allows a dog protection from the rain, hail, sleet, snow, and_  
| _subfreezing temperatures._  
|  _(1-a)_ "Collar" means any collar constructed of nylon,  
| leather, or similar material, specifically designed to be used for  
| a dog.
