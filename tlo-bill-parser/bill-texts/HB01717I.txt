|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to repealing a provision authorizing a person to hold  
| office as a municipal judge in more than one municipality only if  
| each office is filled by appointment.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 574.001(b), Government Code, is  
| repealed.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect January 1, 2020, but only  
| if the constitutional amendment proposed by the 86th Legislature,  
| Regular Session, 2019, permitting a person to hold more than one  
| office as an unpaid municipal judge is approved by the voters. If  
| that proposed constitutional amendment is not approved by the  
| voters, this Act has no effect.
