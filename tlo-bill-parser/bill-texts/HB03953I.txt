|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to motor vehicle operation and accidents.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 550.022(b), Transportation Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (b) If an accident occurs on a main lane, ramp, shoulder,  
| median, or adjacent area of a _highway_ [ ~~freeway in a metropolitan~~  
|  ~~area~~ ] and each vehicle involved can be normally and safely driven,  
| each operator shall move the operator's vehicle as soon as possible  
| to a designated accident investigation site, if available, a  
| location on the frontage road, the nearest suitable cross street,  
| or other suitable location to complete the requirements of Section  
| 550.023 and minimize interference with [ ~~freeway~~ ] traffic.  
|  SECTION 2. Section 550.062(a), Transportation Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  (a) A law enforcement officer who in the regular course of  
| duty investigates a motor vehicle accident shall make a written  
| report of the accident if the accident resulted in injury to or the  
| death of a person or damage to the property of any one person to the  
| apparent extent of _$5,000_ [ ~~$1,000~~ ] or more.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
