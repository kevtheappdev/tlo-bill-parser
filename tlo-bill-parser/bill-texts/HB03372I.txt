|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to causes of action for withholding payments of the  
| proceeds from the sale of oil and gas production.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 91.402, Natural Resources Code, is  
| amended by adding Subsection (b-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(b-1)_ _____A payee may not bring an action for breach of contract_  
| _against a payor for withholding payments under Subsection (b)._  
|  SECTION 2. The change in law made by this Act applies only  
| to an action filed on or after the effective date of this Act. An  
| action filed before the effective date of this Act is governed by  
| the law applicable to the action immediately before the effective  
| date of this Act, and that law is continued in effect for that  
| purpose.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
