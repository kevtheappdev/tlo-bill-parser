|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the authority of political subdivisions to establish  
| election precincts.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 42.061, Election Code, is amended to  
| read as follows:  
|  (a) The governing body of a political subdivision other than  
| a county _may_ ~~shall~~ establish the election precincts for elections  
| ordered by an authority of the political subdivision.  
|  (c) An election precinct established for an election  
| ordered by a city authority may not divide a county election  
| precinct except as necessary to follow the city's boundary.  
|  (d) _If a county precinct is divided according to Section_  
| _42.061(c)the City shall maintain the informational integrity of the_  
| _county precincts by submitting the voter ID numbers of those who_  
| _cast votes to the county elections office by county precinct as well_  
| _as the election precinct that was created under this section._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 42.0615, Election Code, is amended to  
| read as follows:  
|  (2) Provide the voter registrar with a map of an  
| adopted boundary change in a format that is compatible with the  
| mapping format used by the registrar's office.  
|  (3) _Provide the voter registrar with a corresponding_  
| _list of voters in each of the county precincts that have been_  
| _divided_.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution.  If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
