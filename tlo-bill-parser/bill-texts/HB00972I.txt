|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to repealing the authority of a county to regulate hunting  
| with bows and arrows in certain subdivisions.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 235, Local Government  
| Code, is repealed.  
|  SECTION 2. The repeal by this Act of Subchapter C, Chapter  
| 235, Local Government Code, does not apply to an offense committed  
| under that subchapter before the effective date of the repeal. An  
| offense committed before the effective date of the repeal is  
| governed by that subchapter as it existed on the date the offense  
| was committed, and the former law is continued in effect for that  
| purpose. For purposes of this section, an offense was committed  
| before the effective date of the repeal if any element of the  
| offense occurred before that date.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
