|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to state recognition of the Tap Pilam Coahuiltecan Nation.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. The heading to Title 11, Government Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
| TITLE 11. STATE SYMBOLS _, RECOGNITIONS,_ AND HONORS; PRESERVATION  
|  SECTION 2. The heading to Subtitle A, Title 11, Government  
| Code, is amended to read as follows:  
| SUBTITLE A. STATE SYMBOLS _, RECOGNITIONS,_ AND HONORS  
|  SECTION 3. Subtitle A, Title 11, Government Code, is  
| amended by adding Chapter 3107 to read as follows:  
| _CHAPTER 3107. STATE RECOGNITION_  
|  _Sec._ ___3107.001._ _____STATE RECOGNITION OF NATIVE AMERICAN INDIAN_  
| _TRIBE. The Tap Pilam Coahuiltecan Nation is designated and_  
| _recognized by this state as a Native American Indian Tribe_  
| _exercising substantial governmental powers and duties. The tribe_  
| _is recognized as eligible for all programs, services, and other_  
| _benefits provided to state-recognized Native American Indian_  
| _Tribes by the United States, this state, or any other state because_  
| _of the tribe members' status as Native American Indians._  
|  SECTION 4. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
