|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to registration as an organ donor at the time of motor  
| vehicle registration.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 502.189, Transportation Code, is amended  
| by adding Subsection (c) to read as follows:  
|  _(c)_ _____An office authorized to issue motor vehicle_  
| _registrations shall:_  
|  _(1)_ _____specifically ask each person who registers a motor_  
| _vehicle or renews a motor vehicle registration at that office only_  
| _the question, "Would you like to register as an organ donor?"; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____if the person responds affirmatively to the_  
| _question asked under Subdivision (1), provide the person's name,_  
| _date of birth, driver's license number, most recent address, and_  
| _other information needed for identification purposes at the time of_  
| _donation to the nonprofit organization designated to maintain and_  
| _administer the statewide donor registry under Section 692A.020,_  
| _Health and Safety Code, for inclusion in the registry._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
