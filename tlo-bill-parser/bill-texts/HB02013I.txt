|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the implementation of certain rules regarding public  
| school accountability adopted by the commissioner of education or  
| the Texas Education Agency.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 39.001, Education Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (c) to read as follows:  
|  _(c)_ _____Notwithstanding any other law, the implementation of a_  
| _rule adopted by the commissioner or the agency that affects methods_  
| _or procedures for administering provisions of this chapter may be_  
| _delayed until the second school year after the school year the rule_  
| _is adopted unless the commissioner or the agency is required by law_  
| _to adopt and implement such a rule in a shorter period._  
|  SECTION 2. Section 39.001(c), Education Code, as added by  
| this Act, applies only to a rule adopted on or after the effective  
| date of this Act.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
