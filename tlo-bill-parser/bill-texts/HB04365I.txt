|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to a study on the impact of psychotropic medications.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY  
| THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. (a)  The Health and Human Services Commission  
| shall conduct a study to determine:  
|  (1) the number of suicides of persons having been  
| prescribed psychotropic medication; and  
|  (2) the length of psychotropic treatment completed by  
| persons described by Subdivision (1) of this subsection.  
|  (b) Not later than October 1, 2020, the Health and Human  
| Services Commission shall file a report containing the results of  
| the study and recommendations for legislation with the governor,  
| the lieutenant governor, and the speaker of the house of  
| representatives.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
