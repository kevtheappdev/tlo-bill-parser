|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the content of photographic traffic monitoring system  
| signs.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 544.012, Transportation Code, is amended  
| by amending Subsection (c) and adding Subsection (c-1) to read as  
| follows:  
|  (c) The municipality shall install signs along each roadway  
| that leads to an intersection at which a photographic traffic  
| monitoring system is in active use. The signs must be at least 100  
| feet from the intersection or located according to standards  
| established in the manual adopted by the Texas Transportation  
| Commission under Section 544.001, be easily readable to any  
| operator approaching the intersection, and clearly indicate _:_  
|  _(1)_ the presence of a photographic _traffic_ monitoring  
| system that records violations that may result in the issuance of a  
| notice of violation and the imposition of a monetary penalty _; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____the range of the dollar amounts of the monetary_  
| _penalties that may be imposed for a violation recorded by a_  
| _photographic traffic monitoring system_.  
|  _(c-1)_ _____A municipality is exempt from the requirements of_  
| _Subsection (c)(2) if before September 1, 2019, the municipality_  
| _installed a sign:_  
|  _(1)_ _____along each roadway leading to an intersection at_  
| _which a photographic traffic monitoring system is in active use;_  
| _and_  
|  _(2)_ _____indicating the amount of a monetary penalty that_  
| _may be imposed for a violation recorded by the system._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
