|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the licensing of certain child-care facilities that  
| detain certain children.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 42, Human Resources Code,  
| is amended by adding Section 42.0539 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___42.0539._ _____RESIDENTIAL DETENTION FACILITIES._  
| _Notwithstanding any other law, the commission shall:_  
|  _(1)_ _____license each child-care facility, that is operated_  
| _by or under a contract with United States Immigration and Customs_  
| _Enforcement or the United States Department of Health and Human_  
| _Services to enforce federal immigration laws and that detains_  
| _unaccompanied children, in the same manner as the commission_  
| _licenses a residential child-care facility under this chapter; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____require the facility to comply with the same_  
| _requirements, including minimum standards, applicable to a_  
| _residential child-care facility._  
|  SECTION 2. A child-care facility subject to Section  
| 42.0539, Human Resources Code, as added by this Act, must obtain the  
| required license not later than December 1, 2019.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
