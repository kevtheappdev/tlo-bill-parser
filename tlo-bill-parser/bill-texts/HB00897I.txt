|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to safety requirements for a person directly operating an  
| amusement ride.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter C, Chapter 2151, Occupations Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 2151.1013 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___2151.1013._ _____AMUSEMENT RIDE ATTENDANT SAFETY_  
| _REQUIREMENTS. (a) In this section, "attendant" means a person_  
| _directly operating an amusement ride._  
|  _(b)_ _____To directly operate an amusement ride, an attendant must_  
| _be:_  
|  _(1) at least 16 years of age; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____trained in the proper use and operation of the_  
| _amusement ride the attendant is operating._  
|  _(c) An attendant may not directly operate an amusement ride:_  
|  _(1)_ _____while the attendant is directly and simultaneously_  
| _operating another amusement ride; or_  
|  _(2)_ _____if operation by the attendant constitutes an_  
| _offense under Section 49.065, Penal Code._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
