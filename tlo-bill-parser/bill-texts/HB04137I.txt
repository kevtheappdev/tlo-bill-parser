|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the required notice of a patient's right to receive an  
| estimate of certain health care services provided by a physician.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 101.352, Occupations Code, is amended by  
| adding Subsection (c-1) to read as follows:  
|  _(c-1)_ _____A physician or physician group shall post a clear and_  
| _conspicuous notice of a patient's right to receive an estimate of_  
| _charges under Subsection (c):_  
|  _(1)_ _____in at least one area of the physician's or group's_  
| _office where patients are commonly present; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____on the physician's or group's Internet website if_  
| _the physician or group maintains an Internet website._  
|  SECTION 2. (a)  The Health Professions Council may  
| prescribe the form and content required for a notice under Section  
| 101.352(c-1), Occupations Code, as added by this Act.  
|  (b) Notwithstanding Section 101.352(c-1), Occupations  
| Code, as added by this Act, a physician or physician group is not  
| required to comply with that section until December 1, 2019.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
