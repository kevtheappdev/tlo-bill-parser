|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the use of certain drugs in breeding stock by or on the  
| prescription of a veterinarian.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter H, Chapter 801, Occupations Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 801.365 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___801.365._ _____BREEDING STOCK. (a)_ __  
| _In this section,_  
| _"breeding stock" means an animal intended by its owner or_  
| _caretaker, with the input of the veterinarian, to be used for_  
| _selective mating to maintain or enhance desirable genetic traits in_  
| _future generations and not intended for human or animal_  
| _consumption. Desirable genetic traits may include, but not limited_  
| _to, growth rate and yield of products including eggs, milk, or meat._  
|  _(b)_ _____A veterinarian may administer, prescribe, dispense, or_  
| _deliver any drug for off-label use in breeding stock._  
|  _(c)_ _____An owner or caretaker may use a drug described by_  
| _Subsection (b) in breeding stock in the manner prescribed by a_  
| _veterinarian._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
