|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the authority of a municipality to require that an  
| intrastate pipeline be buried to a certain depth.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 211, Local Government Code, is amended  
| by adding Subchapter Z to read as follows:  
| _SUBCHAPTER Z. MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS_  
|  _Sec._ ___211.901._ _____REGULATION OF PIPELINES. (a) This section_  
| _applies only to an intrastate pipeline that transports hazardous_  
| _liquid, as defined by Section 117.001, Natural Resources Code,_  
| _carbon dioxide, or natural gas._  
|  _(b)_ _____The governing body of a municipality may require a_  
| _pipeline to be buried to a depth of not more than 12 feet._  
|  _(c)_ _____In the case of a conflict between this section and_  
| _Chapter 81, 111, or 117, Natural Resources Code, or Chapter 121,_  
| _Utilities Code, this section prevails._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
