|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the duties of the board of trustees of certain school  
| districts.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Subchapter D, Chapter 11, Education Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 11.1517 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___11.1517._ _____REQUIRED REPORT FOR CERTAIN SCHOOL DISTRICTS._  
| _(a) This section applies only to a school district with student_  
| _enrollment of 10,000 or more._  
|  _(b) The board of trustees of a school district shall:_  
|  _(1)_ _____not later than the 10th day of each month, publish_  
| _a report that includes:_  
|  _(A)_ _____minutes of all board meetings conducted_  
| _during the preceding month; and_  
|  _(B)_ _____all plans and objectives under consideration_  
| _by the board regarding the district;_  
|  _(2)_ _____at least once in each quarter of the calendar year,_  
| _publish a report that includes:_  
|  _(A) student academic achievement; and_  
|  _(B) current details of district finances;_  
|  _(3)_ _____publish the reports required by this section on_  
| _the home page of the district's Internet website; and_  
|  _(4)_ _____announce at district board meetings the dates on_  
| _which the reports required by this section will be posted on the_  
| _district's Internet website._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
