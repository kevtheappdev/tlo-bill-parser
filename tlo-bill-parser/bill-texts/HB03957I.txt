|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to expedited death certificates for religious purposes.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 193, Health and Safety Code, is amended  
| by adding Section 193.0025 to read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___193.0025._ _____PROCESS FOR EXPEDITED DEATH CERTIFICATES FOR_  
| _RELIGIOUS PURPOSES. (a)_ ___The executive commissioner by rule shall_  
| _establish a process by which an individual may submit a written_  
| _request to the person required to file a death certificate under_  
| _Section 193.002 for the person to expedite the completion of a_  
| _decedent's death certificate if the requestor demonstrates that:_  
|  _(1)_ _____the expedited completion is necessary for_  
| _religious purposes; and_  
|  _(2)_ _____the requestor is authorized to receive the_  
| _certificate._  
|  _(b)_ _____The process must seek to enable a person required to_  
| _file a death certificate who receives a request under Subsection_  
| _(a) to issue a copy of a decedent's death certificate to the_  
| _requestor not later than 24 hours after the time the person receives_  
| _the request, provided an inquest will not be conducted for the_  
| _decedent._  
|  SECTION 2. As soon as practicable after the effective date  
| of this Act, the executive commissioner of the Health and Human  
| Services Commission shall adopt rules necessary to implement  
| Section 193.0025, Health and Safety Code, as added by this Act.  
|  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
