|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the award of attorney's fees in certain civil actions.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 38.006, Civil Practice and Remedies  
| Code, is repealed.  
|  SECTION 2. The repeal of Section 38.006, Civil Practice and  
| Remedies Code, by this Act applies only to a cause of action that  
| accrues on or after the effective date of this Act. A cause of  
| action that accrues before the effective date of this Act is  
| governed by the law as it existed immediately before the effective  
| date of this Act, and that law is continued in effect for that  
| purpose.  
|  SECTION 3. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
