|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the transport of alcoholic beverages for personal  
| consumption.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 107.08, Alcoholic Beverage Code, is  
| amended to read as follows:  
|  Sec. 107.08. TRANSPORTATION OF BEVERAGES FOR PERSONAL  
| CONSUMPTION. A person who purchases an alcoholic beverage for _the_  
| _person's_ [ ~~his~~ ] own consumption may _personally_ transport it from a  
| place where its sale is legal to a place where its possession is  
| legal without holding a license or permit.  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
