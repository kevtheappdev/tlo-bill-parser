|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to the creation of an incentive program to fund innovative  
| solutions for clinical education of graduate nurses.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Chapter 61, Subchapter Z, Education Code, is  
| amended by adding Section 61.9630 read as follows:  
|  _Sec._ ___61.9630._ _____GRADUATE NURSING EDUCATION GRANTS. (a)_  
| _Using funds appropriated for this purpose, and funds returned to_  
| _the board under Subsection 61.9629(e), the board shall administer a_  
| _grant program to fund innovative approaches for increasing the_  
| _availability of clinical education practice sites for graduate_  
| _nurses._  
|  _(b)_ _____The board shall require a grant recipient to submit_  
| _periodic reports describing the outcome of the activities funded_  
| _through the grant._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
