|  A BILL TO BE ENTITLED  
|  AN ACT  
| relating to allowing the Bastrop County Commissioners Court to  
| charge interest on assessments for certain county improvements of  
| subdivision roads.  
|  BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:  
|  SECTION 1. Section 253.008, Transportation Code, is amended  
| by amending Subsection (b) and adding Subsection (e) to read as  
| follows:  
|  (b) Except as provided by Subsection (d) _or (e)_ , the  
| commissioners court may not require the payment of interest on an  
| assessment.  
|  _(e)_ _____The Commissioners Court of Bastrop County may require_  
| _the payment of interest on an assessment._  
|  SECTION 2. This Act takes effect immediately if it receives  
| a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as  
| provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this  
| Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this  
| Act takes effect September 1, 2019.
